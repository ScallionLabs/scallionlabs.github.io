Fresh and Simple Swai Ceviche

  • http://www.aspicyperspective.com/2010/04/eco-ceviche.html
  • Servings: 8
  • Ready in: 45min
  • Tags:
    Ketogenic Side Dish Gluten Free Dairy Free Pescatarian South American Latin American

Vitamins RDA %

Minerals RDA %

Macros

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado, diced
  • ¼- ½ tsp chile-garlic paste
  • 1 Tb. chopped cilantro
  • 3 Swai fillets, diced (about 1 lb.)
  • 2 limes, juiced
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 shallot, diced (or ¼ cup red onion)
  • 1 tomato, diced

Preparation

  • Place the diced fish into ¾ of the lime juice and refrigerate for at least 3 hours. Strain when ready to proceed.
  • Mix the veggies with the remaining lime juice.Stir in the fish and cilantro.
  • Drizzle with olive oil and add the chile-garlic sauce. Gently toss.Salt and pepper to taste.
  • Serve with tortilla chips or a salad.
