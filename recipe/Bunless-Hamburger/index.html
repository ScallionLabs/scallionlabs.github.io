Bunless Hamburger

  • Servings: 3
  • Ready in: 45min

    Ketogenic Lunch Main Course Main Dish Dinner Gluten Free Dairy Free American

Macros

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp capers
  • ¼ cup coconut flour
  • 1 tbsp fresh horseradish, grated
  • ¼ cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tbsp sliced green olives, chopped
  • 450g ground grass fed beef
  • ½ tsp ground smoked chipotle pepper
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • ½ tsp ground white pepper
  • ½ tsp freshly grated nutmeg
  • ¾ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 whole egg
  • 1 tbsp whole grain mustard

Preparation

  • Add all the ingredients to a mixing bowl and knead delicately with clean hands until all the ingredients are uniformly distributed. Divide the meat into 3 or 4 equal portions and shape into patties. You can cook the patties immediately or cover and refrigerate them for later use)When ready to cook the patties, preheat your outdoor grill to high.Once grill is nice and hot, lower the heat to medium and place the patties on the grill; cook for about 3-4 minutes per side or until the patties are done to your liking.
  • Serve with your favourite fixin’s and sides.
