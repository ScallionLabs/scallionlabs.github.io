The Ultimate Oven Fried Chicken

Ingredients

  • 3 Tablespoons butter
  • 3 to 4 pounds chicken pieces (Skin on)
  • 1 teaspoon chipotle chili powder (Or cayenne, if you want a lot of heat)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 cup flour or rice flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Preparation

  • Heat oven to 425 degrees.
  • Place butter in a shallow baking dish and place in the oven. Wash chicken and pat dry.
  • Mix flour paprika chili powder salt and pepper in a shallow dish. Coat chicken pieces in seasoned flour. Shake off excess. flour.When butter is melted, place chicken skin side down in baking dish. (Careful, it'll be hot!)
  • Bake chicken for 30 minutes. Turn chicken and bake an additional 15 minutes, or until cooked through. (Juices should run clear.)
  • Remove from oven and let stand 5 minutes before serving.Share and enjoy!
