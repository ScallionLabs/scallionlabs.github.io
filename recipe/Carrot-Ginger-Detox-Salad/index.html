Carrot Ginger Detox Salad

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1 T soy sauce or Bragg's Aminos (for GF) (I used organic SanJ shoyu)
  • 1-2 heads of broccoli
  • 3 carrots shredded
  • 2 cups cooked quinoa
  • 2 tsp fresh grated ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 1/4 cup mirin
  • salt to taste
  • 2 T toasted sesame oil

Preparation

  • Steam the broccoli. I used a rice cooker/steamer to do this.  In a sauce pan, cook the quinoa according to the package instructions.  I already had some quinoa in my fridge.  We always have some kind of grain in the fridge to make our meals stretch longer.  While the broccoli is steaming and the quinoa is cooking, throw all the ingredients together for the dressing. 
  • Whisk them in a small bowl.  Once the quinoa and broccoli are done throw everything together in one big bowl.
