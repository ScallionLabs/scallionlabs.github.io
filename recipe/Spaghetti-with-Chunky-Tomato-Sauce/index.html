Spaghetti with Chunky Tomato Sauce

Ingredients

  • 8 cups baby spinach leaves
  • 2 15-oz. cans diced tomatoes

  • 2 tsp. dried thyme
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced (1 Tbs.)

  • 2 Tbs. olive oil
  • 2 Tbs. dried oregano
  • 1 medium red bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • ¼ tsp. red pepper flakes
  • ¼ cup red wine
  • 8 oz. spaghetti
  • 8 oz. (2 cups) sliced white mushrooms

  • 1 large leek, white part cut into chunks

  • 8 oz. spaghetti
  • 8 oz. (2 cups) sliced white mushrooms

Preparation

  • Place mushrooms, bell pepper, and leek in bowl of food processor. Pulse until ingredients are coarsely chopped.
  • Heat oil in large saucepan or stockpot over medium-high heat.
  • Add mushroom mixture and garlic, and season with salt and pepper, if desired. Cook 3 to 5 minutes, or until vegetables are softened and most of liquid has evaporated. Stir in oregano, thyme, and red pepper flakes, and cook 30 seconds, or until fragrant.
  • Add tomatoes, chickpeas, and wine, and season with salt and pepper, if desired.
  • Cover, and simmer 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Add spinach, and cook 3 to 5 minutes more, or until spinach has wilted.
  • Meanwhile, cook spaghetti according to package directions.
  • Serve spaghetti topped with sauce.
