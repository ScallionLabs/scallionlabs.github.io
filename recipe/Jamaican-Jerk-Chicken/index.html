Jamaican Jerk Chicken

  • http://www.nutmegnanny.com/2014/06/11/jamaican-jerk-chicken/
  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 50min
  • Preparation time: 15min
  • Cooking time: 35min
  • Tags:

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons allspice
  • 6 chicken drumsticks (legs)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoons dried thyme
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 3 green onions, cut in half
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1/4 cup low sodium soy sauce
  • Olive oil
  • 1-2 Habenero peppers, seeded
  • 1 small yellow onion, quartered

Preparation

  • In a blender or food processor add yellow onion, green onions, Habenero pepper, garlic, soy sauce, lime juice, thyme, allspice, black pepper, nutmeg, cinnamon, coriander and salt. Blend just until combined and the onion has broken down.
  • Drizzle in olive oil to make a thick marinade.
  • Pour marinade into a large 1 gallon ziptop bag and add chicken. Secure the top of the bag and move the chicken around in the bag to make sure it's completely coated.
  • Add bag to a bowl or dish and marinade over night or at least 4 hours. {Keeping the bag in a bowl/dish helps keep your fridge clean in case there is a leak in the bag}Once the chicken is done marinading heat your grill till medium heat. Since you are using bone-in chicken you need to cook it long enough to fully cook in the inside meat without burning the outside. Grill for about 30-35 minutes until the outside of the chicken is crisp and the inside is fully cooked.
  • Serve with a sprinkle of cilantro and wedge of lime if desired.
