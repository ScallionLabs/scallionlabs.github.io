Holiday Appetizer: Roasted Broccoli & Carrots and Herb Marinated Olives

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup basil leaves packed, julienned
  • 2 big bunches of broccoli- - 1 to 1 1/2 lb
  • 1 lb carrots, peeled and sliced on the diagonal 1/4 inch thick
  • 1/2 teaspoons fresh thyme leaves, chopped
  • 2 garlic clove, sliced
  • 3 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • a couple springs of herbs- fresh thyme, rosemary
  • juice of 1 lemon wedge
  • 1 cup brine-packed olives (green, black or a mix). I used Kalamata olives (can buy with or without pits)
  • zest of 1 lemon
  • - 1 Tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  • Cut your carrots and broccoli (should be about 8 cups raw veggies).
  • Place on cookie sheet and toss with olive oil, salt, pepper.Roast for 18 to 20 minutes. Half way through the cooking time add the garlic.
  • Remove from oven, add herbs, lemon zest, juice of the wedge of lemon.
  • Transfer to a serving bowl.
  • Drain the olives.
  • Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  • Let olives marinate for a couple hours or overnight before serving.
