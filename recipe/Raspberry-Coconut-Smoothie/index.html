Ingredients

  • 1 cup Almond Breeze Unsweetened Almondmilk Coconutmilk
  • 1/2 medium banana
  • 2 tablespoons coconut, for garnish
  • 1 cup raspberries (fresh or frozen)
  • 1/2 cup spinach

Preparation

  • Place the milk, raspberries, banana, and spinach in the blender and blend until smooth.
  • Pour into a glass and garnish with coconut.
  • Serve immediately. Note-if I use fresh raspberries I like to throw in a few ice cubes to get a thicker/colder smoothie.
