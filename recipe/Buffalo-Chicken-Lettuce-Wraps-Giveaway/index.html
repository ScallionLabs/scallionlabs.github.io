Buffalo Chicken Lettuce Wraps {Giveaway}

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup buffalo sauce
  • 1 carrot, thinly sliced into 2-inch lengths
  • 1 celery stalk, thinly sliced into 2-inch lengths
  • 2 cloves garlic, diced
  • 12 lettuce leaves, such as iceberg or bibb
  • ½ cup mushrooms, diced
  • blue cheese dipping sauce
  • olive oil
  • ½ medium onion, diced
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breasts, chopped into 1-inch pieces

Preparation

  • Drizzle olive oil into a 12-inch skillet over medium-low heat.
  • Add chicken and cook, stirring occasionally, until cooked through, about 7-10 minutes.
  • Add onion, garlic, mushrooms and buffalo sauce. Stir to combine and allow to simmer for about 3 minutes.While buffalo chicken mixture is simmering, assemble platter for making lettuce wraps of lettuce leaves, celery, carrots, blue cheese crumbles, and blue cheese dipping sauce.
  • Serve warm.
