Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley
  • 4 cloves garlic, sliced
  • kosher salt and black pepper
  • 1 tsp finely grated lemon zest
  • 12 ozs linguine (3/4 box)
  • 2 6-oz cans oil-packed tuna
  • 3 Tbsps olive oil
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes

Preparation

  • Cook the pasta according to the package directions. Reserve cup of the cooking water; drain the pasta.Wipe out the pasta pot.
  • Add the oil, garlic, and red pepper and cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until fragrant, 2 to 3 minutes.
  • Add the pasta, tuna, lemon zest, teaspoon salt, teaspoon black pepper, and the reserved pasta water and cook over low heat, tossing, until the sauce is slightly thickened and coats the pasta, 1 to 2 minutes.
  • Add the parsley and toss to combine.
