Meatless Monday: Thai Peanut Slaw

Ingredients

  • 4 cups shredded carrots
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 1 small head napa cabbage, thinly sliced
  • ½ cup peanuts
  • 1 bunch radishes (about 8), halved and thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons rice wine vinegar
  • 1 bunch scallions, thinly sliced

Preparation

  • Combine the cabbage, carrots, radishes, and scallions in a large mixing bowl.In a small mixing bowl, whisk together the fish sauce, vinegar, and lime juice.
  • Pour the dressing over the cabbage and toss to combine. Taste for seasoning and add salt as necessary.
  • Transfer the salad to a platter and garnish with the peanuts.
