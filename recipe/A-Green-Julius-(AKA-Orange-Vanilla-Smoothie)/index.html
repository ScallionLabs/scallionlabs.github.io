A Green Julius (AKA Orange Vanilla Smoothie)

  • http://greenlitebites.com/2012/01/26/orange-vanilla-smoothie/
  • Servings: 1
  • Ready in: 45min
  • Tags:
    Vegetarian Side Dish Gluten Free Lacto Ovo Vegetarian Primal
  • High in: K C A

Vitamins RDA %

Minerals RDA %

Macros

Ingredients

  • 3 small ripe clementines
  • About 6 ice cubes
  • 1/2 cup of skim milk (4 oz)
  • 2 cups of raw spinach (60g)
  • 1/2 tsp of vanilla extract

Preparation

  • Put all the ingredients except the ice in the blender.Blend until smooth.
  • Add the ice cubes and blend again until smooth.Enjoy!(note: points are based on Nutritional Information)
