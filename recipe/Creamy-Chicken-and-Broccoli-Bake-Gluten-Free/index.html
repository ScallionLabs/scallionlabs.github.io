Creamy Chicken & Broccoli Bake {Gluten Free}

  • Ready in: 75min

  • Cooking time: 45min

Ingredients

  • ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 10-oz packages broccoli pieces
  • ¼ cup brown rice flour (or all purpose if you aren't gluten free)
  • ½ stick butter (1/4 cup)
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • ¼ cup dry sherry
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • ½ cup milk
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 8 ounces shredded cheddar, divided
  • 3 cups cooked chicken, chopped or shredded
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 400°F.In a saucepan over medium heat, melt ½ a stick of butter.
  • Add the flour and whisk till smooth.
  • Add the chicken stock and milk and salt and pepper, and stir until it thickens nicely (about 5-10 minutes).
  • Remove it from the heat and set aside.Boil broccoli for 5 minutes or until crisp-tender; drain.Meanwhile, stir together cream sauce, mayonnaise, sour cream, sherry and Worcestershire sauce until well blended.
  • Add the chicken and broccoli.
  • Add about half the cheese, and fold it all together carefully.
  • Spread the chicken mixture into a 11x8 casserole dish.
  • Sprinkle remaining cheese on top.
  • Bake at 400 for 40-45 minutes, until lightly brown on top.Allow to sit for about 5 minutes before serving.
