Italian Sub Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Black Olives
  • 1/4 teaspoon Black Pepper
  • 3 ounces of Thinly Sliced Capocollo
  • 2 to 4 Garlic Cloves, pressed
  • 1 cup Shredded Iceberg Lettuce
  • 1/2 teaspoon Kosher Salt
  • 4 Slices of Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1/3 cup Olive Oil
  • 1 teaspoon Dried Oregano
  • 4 Slices of Provolone Cheese
  • Sliced Red and Green Bell Pepper
  • Thinly Sliced Red Onion
  • 1/4 cup Red Wine Vinegar
  • 1/4 pound of Thinly Sliced Genoa Salami
  • 1/2 pound of Thinly Sliced Turkey
  • 1 16 oz Loaf of French or Italian Bread

Preparation

  • FOR THE DRESSING: In a medium sized Mason jar add pressed garlic cloves, oregano, salt, pepper, vinegar and olive oil. Shake and let it sit on the counter to get the flavors mingling.FOR THE SUB: Divide the loaf into 4 portions.
  • Cut each portion in half lengthwise (but not all the way through) and tear out the centers of each loaf, leaving the shell of the bread.Stir the Italian dressing again before drizzling a little bit of the dressing all over the inside of each loaf.
  • Layer the a few slices of Capocollo, turkey, salami and then Provolone. Top with two slices of peppers and then repeat the meat layer again. Top that with a slice of Mozzarella and a 1/4 cup of lettuce.
  • Serve and enjoy!
