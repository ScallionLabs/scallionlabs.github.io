Ingredients

  • Japanese chili pepper (Ichimi Togarashi), for garnish
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • toasted sesame seeds, for garnish
  • 1 teaspoon gluten-free soy sauce
  • 1 large bunch spinach
  • Japanese chili pepper (Ichimi Togarashi), for garnish
  • toasted sesame seeds, for garnish
  • 1 teaspoon gluten-free soy sauce
  • 1 large bunch spinach

Preparation

  • Bring a pot of water to boil.
  • Add spinach and cook until just wilted, about 30 seconds.
  • Drain and rinse with cold water. Squeeze out excess water.
  • Cut into bite size pieces. Toss with soy sauce and sesame oil. Chill.
  • Sprinkle with sesame seeds and Japanese chili pepper just before serving.
