Quinoa Vegetable Salad with Tahini Dressing

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp agave nectar
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 ½ cup chopped cucumber
  • 4 cups chopped kale
  • 3 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1 ½ cup snap peas, trimmed
  • 2 tbsp tahini
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp warm water

Preparation

  • Place the kale in a colander and run warm water over it while massaging the leaves until the kale softens.In a large bowl, combine the kale, cucumber, snap peas, tomatoes and quinoa. Toss with the dressing.
  • Serve.In a small glass bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, tahini, water and agave nectar. If the tahini is difficult to whisk, microwave the mixture on HIGH for about 10 seconds.
