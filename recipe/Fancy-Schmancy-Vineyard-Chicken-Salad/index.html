Fancy Schmancy Vineyard Chicken Salad

  • Ready in: 30min

Ingredients

  • 4 teaspoons Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 2 Celery Stalks , chopped small
  • 6 teaspoons Honey
  • 1 cup Mayo
  • 2 oz Pecans, lightly toasted then coarsely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon Poppy Seeds
  • 1 cup small Red Grapes
  • 1 small Rotisserie Chicken, shredded in to bite size pieces
  • Salt and Black Pepper, to taste

Preparation

  • Measure and mix dressing ingredients, taste and adjust seasoning if needed. Set aside.
  • Combine salad ingredients, then pour the dressing over top. Toss and refrigerate for at least an hour before serving.
  • Serve this salad on a buttery croissant or on a bed of romaine lettuce leaves! *To toast pecans; place in a dry pan over medium heat, tossing occasionally until fragrant.
