Crock Pot Cheesy Stuffing Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1 (10-ounce) can Rotel tomatoes
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 (10-ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon peppers
  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 box Stove Top stuffing mix (dry) I used chicken flavor
  • 10 slices swiss cheese

Preparation

  • Lightly spray a 6 to 7-quart crock pot with cooking spray.
  • Place onions in the bottom of the crock pot.
  • Place chicken breasts on top of onions and sprinkle with garlic salt and pepper.
  • Pour butter over chicken and add dry stuffing mix to crock pot.Spoon the Rotel tomatoes and soup over stuffing mix.
  • Add chicken broth. Cover and cook on LOW for 6 hours.
