Ingredients

  • 1 wedge of The Laughing Cow Smooth Sensations Cream Cheese Spread Garden Vegetable 1/3 Less Fat
  • 5 grape tomatoes diced
  • Pinch of Kosher Salt and Pepper
  • 1 5oz can of Light Tuna drained well
  • 1 Mini Babybel Light grated
  • 1 sweet gherkin (pickle) diced

Preparation

  • Preheat the broiler.
  • Put the tuna, tomatoes, pickle and the Garden Vegetable Wedge a bowl.
  • Combine.Now, I almost stopped right there and just put the on a slice of toast but I wanted to try this Tuna Melt idea. So I put the tuna salad on a wrap and topped with the grated minibabybel.Again, I used 2 small wraps but you could use 1 big one as well.
  • Put the wrap(s) under the broiler just long enough to melt the cheese but not crisp the wrap. I did exactly 2 minutes and the edges of the wrap were just starting to curl.
