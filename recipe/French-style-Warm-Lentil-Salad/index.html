Ingredients

  • 1 medium carrot, finely chopped
  • 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
  • ½ Tbsp (7 mL) Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp (5 mL) finely chopped fresh thyme
  • 2 medium garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup (250 mL) French green (also known as “Puy”) lentils, picked over and rinsed
  • 5 Tbsps (65 mL) olive oil, divided
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 Tbsps (25 mL) finely chopped Italian parsley, for serving
  • 2 Tbsps plus ½ tsp (27 mL) red wine vinegar
  • ½ tsp (2 mL) salt, divided
  • Crunchy sea salt, for serving
  • 1 Turkish bay leaf
  • 3 cups (750 mL) water
