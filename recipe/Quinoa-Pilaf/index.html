Quinoa Pilaf

Ingredients

  • 2 cups baby spinach, chopped
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 1/4 cups reduced-sodium chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, plus extra for drizzling
  • 1 cup tricolor quinoa
  • 1/8 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 shallot, chopped
  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes in oil, cut into 1/2-inch dice
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, cut into 1/2-inch dice
  • 2 cups baby spinach, chopped
  • 1 1/4 cups reduced-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 cup tricolor quinoa
  • 1/8 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 shallot, chopped
  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes in oil, cut into 1/2-inch dice
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, cut into 1/2-inch dice

Preparation

  • Watch how to make this recipe.
  • In a medium saucepan, heat the oil over medium heat.
  • Add the shallot, tomatoes, peppers and red pepper flakes and cook, stirring often, until slightly softened and aromatic, about 4 minutes. Season with the salt and add the quinoa. Using a wooden spoon, stir to coat the quinoa in the oil.
  • Add the chicken broth, stir and bring to a simmer. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pan and cook until the quinoa is tender and the liquid is absorbed, about 20 minutes. Stir in the spinach to wilt and serve drizzled with olive oil.
