Ingredients

  • 3 cups packed baby spinach (don't use regular, baby spinach has a milder and sweeter flavor)
  • 1 cup ice
  • 2 mandarin oranges, peeled and halved
  • 6 oz fresh strawberries, hulled (about 8 - 9 medium)
  • 1 cup cold water

Preparation

  • Add bananas, strawberries, oranges, spinach, water and half of the ice to a blender. Blend until combined then add remaining ice and blend until smoothie is well pureed.
  • Serve immediately.
