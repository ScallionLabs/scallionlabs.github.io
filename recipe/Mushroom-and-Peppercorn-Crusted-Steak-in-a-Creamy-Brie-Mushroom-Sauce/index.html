Mushroom and Peppercorn Crusted Steak in a Creamy Brie Mushroom Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup beef broth
  • 4 ounces brie
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup cream
  • 1/4 cup dry white wine (or broth)
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon white miso paste (optional, gluten-free for gluten-free)
  • 1/4 ounce dried mushrooms, coarsely chopped in food processor
  • 8 ounces various mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1/2 cup parmigiano reggiano (parmesan), grated
  • 1 teaspoon fresh crack peppercorns
  • 1 pound ribeye steak (or tenderloin, or your favourite cut)
  • salt to taste
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon thyme, chopped

Preparation

  • FOR THE PEPPERCORN AND MUSHROOM CRUSTED STEAK:Melt the butter and heat the oil in a pan over medium high heat, press the mixture of the dried mushrooms, peppercorns and salt into the steak(s), and cook in the pan to the desired level of doneness, about 2-4 minutes per side.
Let sit for 5 minutes before optionally slicing and serve topped with the mushroom sauce!FOR THE CREAMY MUSHROOM SAUCE
  • Add the onions and mushrooms and cook until the mushrooms release their liquids and it's absorbed, about 10 minutes.
  • Add the garlic and thyme and cook for a minute.
  • Add the wine and deglaze the pan.
  • Add the broth and cream, bring to a boil and simmer to thicken, about 2-4 minutes, before mixing in the cheeses and miso and removing from the heat.Season with salt and pepper to taste.
