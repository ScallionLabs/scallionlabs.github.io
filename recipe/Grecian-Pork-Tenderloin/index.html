Grecian Pork Tenderloin

Ingredients

  • 6 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh lime juice
  • 3/4 cup olive oil
  • 6 tablespoons dried oregano
  • 2 (1 pound) pork tenderloins
  • 2 teaspoons salt

Preparation

  • Place lime juice, olive oil, garlic, salt, and oregano in a large resealable plastic bag. Shake sealed bag until ingredients are well mixed. Taste the marinade for tartness. If too tart, add a little more oil. Not enough zing, add more lime. The garlic and salt flavors should also be up front, yet not overpowering.
  • Place tenderloins in the bag, seal, and turn to coat. Marinate in the refrigerator for 2 to 5 hours.
  • Preheat grill for medium heat.
  • Lightly oil the grill grate, and discard marinade. Grill tenderloins for 20 to 30 minutes, turning once, or to desired doneness.
