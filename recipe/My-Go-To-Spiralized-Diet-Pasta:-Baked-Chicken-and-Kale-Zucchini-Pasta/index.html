My Go-To Spiralized Diet Pasta: Baked Chicken and Kale Zucchini Pasta

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 cup of chopped kale
  • 1 lemon wedge
  • 1 tsp oregano flakes
  • 1 pinch red pepper flakes
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 large zucchini, Blade C

Preparation

  • Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.Lightly coat a baking tray with the cooking spray and add in the chicken strips. Season with salt, pepper and oregano flakes.
  • Bake for 15-20 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink on the inside. Once done, remove from oven and set aside.After 10 minutes of cooking the chicken, add a large skillet over medium heat.
  • Let the pan heat up and coat lightly with cooking spray.
  • Add in the kale and cook for about 3 minutes or until mostly wilted.
  • Add in the zucchini noodles, red pepper flakes, garlic powder, juice from the lemon wedge and toss to combine. Cook, stirring frequently, for about 3 minutes or until zucchini noodles soften.
  • Add the chicken to the zucchini pasta, toss to combine and plate. Enjoy!
