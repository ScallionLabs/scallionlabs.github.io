Simple Wintry Salad

  • Ready in: 45min
  • Tags:
    Vegetarian Salad Gluten Free Dairy Free Paleolithic Lacto Ovo Vegetarian Primal Vegan
  • High in: K A

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup apple cider vinegar
  • ½ cup shredded carrots
  • ¼ cup lightly toasted shredded coconut
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1-2 tablespoons hemp seeds
  • 3 cups Massaged Kale Salad
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 persimmon, sliced into rounds
  • ¼ cup lightly toasted pine nuts
  • ¼ cup pomegranate seeds
  • 1 head of radicchio
  • Pinch of sea salt
  • ½ cup water
  • 1 satsuma, peeled (or use an orange)

Preparation

  • In a medium salad bowl, simply combine kale salad, carrots, pomegranates, persimmons, thyme, pine nuts, toasted coconut, and hemp seeds together. (if you're adding "boosters" to the salad, add them here).In a blender or using a whisk, simply whisk all ingredients for the salad dressing into a small bowl then drizzle upon serving the salad.Stir with a fork and adjust seasonings to taste, serve.
