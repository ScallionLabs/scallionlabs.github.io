Baked Salmon with Garlic and Dijon

  • http://natashaskitchen.com/2012/06/08/baked-salmon-with-garlic-and-dijon/
  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 25min
  • Preparation time: 10min
  • Cooking time: 15min
  • Tags:
    Pescetarian Lunch Main Course Main Dish Dinner Gluten Free Dairy Free Fodmap Friendly Pescatarian

Ingredients

  • ½ Tbsp Dijon mustard (we used grey poupon)
  • Lemon slices (for effect :)
  • 2 Tbsp of fresh lemon juice
  • 2 Tbsp parsley, freshly chopped
  • 1½ lbs salmon fillets (this was a wild sockeye)
  • ½ tsp salt

Preparation

  • In a small bowl, mix together: 2 Tbsp chopped parsley, 2-3 pressed cloves garlic, Tbsp Dijon mustard, tsp salt, tsp of pepper, cup of olive oil and 2 Tbsp of lemon juice.Slice salmon into even portions.
  • Lay them onto a lined baking dish with the skin-side-down.Generously brush all sides of salmon with sauce and top with fresh slices of lemon.
  • Bake at 450F for 12-15 min or until just cooked through and flaky. Don't over-cook or it will be dry. There you have it! An easy, excellent dish.
