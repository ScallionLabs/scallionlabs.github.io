Southwestern Chili Con Carne

Ingredients

  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 4 tablespoons chili powder
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro leaves
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 green bell pepper, large diced
  • 2 1/2 pounds ground chuck
  • 3 tablespoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 red bell pepper, large diced
  • 1/2 cup red onion, large diced
  • 3 Roma tomatoes, diced
  • 1 (15-ounce) can soy black beans, drained and rinsed, optional
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Preparation

  • Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  • Add peppers and onions and cook until slightly tender.
  • Add the ground beef and spices and cook until browned. Skim off excess grease.
  • Add the diced tomatoes and soy black beans and simmer for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • To serve, garnish chili with cilantro. If desired, top with shredded Cheddar or Jack cheese, sour cream, and sliced scallion tops.
