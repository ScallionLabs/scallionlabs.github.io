Lemon Lime Green Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 small frozen banana, peeled
  • ½ cup green grapes
  • 1 cup fresh curly kale, stems removed
  • ¼ lemon, peeled
  • ¼ lime, peeled
  • 4 ounces filtered water

Preparation

  • Place all ingredients in your blender, and puree until smooth.
  • Pour into glass and garnish with a kale leaf or lime wedge if you're feeling fancy.
