chard and egg bake

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch chard, leaves and stems chopped and separate
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • ¼ teaspoon freshly ground pepper, or to taste
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 3 ounces cheddar cheese, shredded

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Coat a 2 quart baking dish with cooking spray.
  • Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  • Add chard stems and onion and cook, stirring often until the onion and chard stems start to soften, 2 t 4 minutes.
  • Add chard leaves and 2 to 3 tablespoons water and cook, stirring to wilt them down until they are soft and the water is evaporated.
  • Transfer the chard mixture to the prepared baking dish.
  • Beat eggs, dill, Dijon, salt and pepper in a medium bowl.
  • Pour over the chard.
  • Bake until the eggs are just slightly wobbly in the center, 20 to 24 minutes.
  • Add cheese on top and continue baking until the eggs are cooked through and the cheese is melted, about 4 minutes.
  • Let cool 5 to 10 minutes before slicing into 4 squares to serve.
