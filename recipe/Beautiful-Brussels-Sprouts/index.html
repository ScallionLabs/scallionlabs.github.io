Beautiful Brussels Sprouts

Ingredients

  • Pinch freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 1/2 pounds Brussels sprouts
  • 1 large butternut squash
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • Pinch kosher salt
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup pomegranate molasses
  • 1 cup pomegranate seeds
  • 4 red onions, cut into chunks

Preparation

  • Watch how to make this recipe.
  • Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F.
  • Use a knife to lop off the top and bottom of the squash. Slice the skin off the sides, then cut the squash in half lengthwise and remove the seeds.
  • Cut the squash into large pieces, cut the pieces into slices, cut the slices into matchsticks and then cut the matchsticks into dice. Divide the squash between 2 baking sheets.
  • Trim the Brussels sprouts, then cut them in half if desired (or you can leave them whole). Arrange them on the baking sheets with the squash and add the red onions.
  • Drizzle the vegetables with the olive oil, sprinkle with the chili powder, salt and pepper and toss. Roast until browned, 25 to 30 minutes.
  • Tip the vegetables into a serving dish, drizzle with the pomegranate molasses and sprinkle on the pomegranate seeds. Toss and serve immediately.
