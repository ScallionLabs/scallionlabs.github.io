Curry Roasted Carrots with Peanut Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 medium bundle ( - 8) carrots, rinsed, scrubbed, tops removed and cut in half if large
  • 2 tsp curry powder
  • 1-2 Tbsp melted coconut oil (for roasting), or sub olive or grapeseed oil
  • 1 1/2 cups cooked millet (preferably in vegetable stock for best flavor)
  • peanut sauce for topping
  • - 1/4 tsp each sea salt and black pepper

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  • Place carrots on baking sheet and toss with oil, salt, pepper and curry powder.Roast for 20-25 minutes or until tender and brown, tossing once halfway through.In the meantime prepare peanut sauce (see link above).Once carrots are roasted, place over 3/4 cup millet and top with peanut sauce. Other toppings might include sriracha, fresh herbs, crushed peanuts or lime juice.
