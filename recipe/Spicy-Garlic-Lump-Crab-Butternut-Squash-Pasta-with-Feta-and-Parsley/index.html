Spicy Garlic Lump Crab Butternut Squash Pasta with Feta & Parsley

  • Servings: 2
  • Ready in: 25min
  • Preparation time: 15min
  • Cooking time: 10min
  • Tags:

Ingredients

  • 1 butternut squash, peeled, Blade C
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 3/4 cup lump crab meat
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tbsp freshly chopped parsley
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Preparation

  • Preheat the oven to 400 degrees.In a baking tray, spread out the butternut squash noodles.
  • Drizzle lightly with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.Once the oven preheats, add in the butternut squash noodles. Cook for 7-10 minutes or until softened.Next, in a large skillet, add in the olive oil. Once the oil heats, add in the garlic and cook for 30 seconds. Then, add in the red pepper flakes and crab meat.Cook, stirring frequently, for about 3 minutes or until the crab meat is heated.
  • Place the butternut squash noodles in a bowl and pour in crab meat mixture.Top with parsley and feta. Enjoy!
