Stewed Beef Neck Tacos

Ingredients

  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 pounds beef neck
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 dried chile guajillos
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 small to medium onion, roughly chopped
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano

Preparation

  • Rinse the beef neck under cold running water and place into a pot along with all the ingredients for braising.
  • Add enough water to cover all the beef neck. Bring the water to boil, skimming off scum from the necks as necessary. Reduce the heat to a simmer and cook for 3 hours, until the meat is falling off the bone and the liquid is reduced by at least one-half.
  • Allow the meat to cool slightly in its cooking liquid.
  • Remove the bones and break up the meat slightly with a fork, mixing it with the reduced braising liquid.
  • Remove the bay leaves and the chilies. Use the meat as a filling for tacos with salsa, cilantro, and cheese or crema.
