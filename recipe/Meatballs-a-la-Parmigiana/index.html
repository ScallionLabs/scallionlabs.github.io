Meatballs a la Parmigiana

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsps. of chopped basil
  • 4 to 6 tbsps. of bread crumbs (I used panko)
  • 8 to 10 slices of cheese (I used mild cheddar)
  • 1/2 tsp. of minced garlic
  • 150 g. of ground beef
  • 150 g. of ground pork
  • 1 tbsp. of milk
  • 4 tbsps. of finely chopped onion
  • 2 tbsps. of chopped parsley
  • 1/4 tsp. of pepper
  • 1/2 tsp. of salt
  • 8 to 10 heaping tbsps. of tomato sauce (get recipe)

Preparation

  • Preheat the oven to 375F.Line a baking tray with non-stick paper.In a bowl, mix together the ground pork and beef, onion, garlic, salt, pepper, basil, parsley, bread crumbs and milk.Shape into 8 to 10 balls about two inches in diameter. Arrange on the prepared baking tray and cook in the oven for 20 minutes.Take the meatballs out of the oven and arrange on a heatproof plate that you will serve them in.Spoon a heaping tablespoonful of tomato sauce over each meatball. Top with a slice of cheese.Put back in the oven and cook for another 10 minutes or until the cheese is melted and the sauce is bubbly.
  • Sprinkle with more chopped parsley or basil, or both, before serving.
