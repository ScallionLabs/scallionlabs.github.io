Caprese Salad in a Jar

  • http://skinnyms.com/caprese-salad-in-a-jar/
  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 45min
  • Tags:
    Ketogenic Salad Gluten Free Lacto Ovo Vegetarian
  • High in: Saturated Fat Calcium Protein

Vitamins RDA %

Minerals RDA %

Macros

Ingredients

  • 5 cups cherry tomatoes
  • 1/8 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 20 fresh basil leaves
  • 5 cups pearl mozzarella or 4 (9-ounce) mozzarella balls
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Preparation

  • In four individual jars, distribute the tomatoes, mozzarella and basil.
  • Sprinkle with the salt and drizzle with the extra virgin olive oil.Seal the jars and refrigerate. You can prepare them the night before.Take out from the refrigerator when ready to go.
