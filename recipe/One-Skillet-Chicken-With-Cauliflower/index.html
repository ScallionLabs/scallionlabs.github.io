One Skillet Chicken With Cauliflower

  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 45min
  • Tags:
    Ketogenic Lunch Main Course Main Dish Dinner Gluten Free Primal

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon Butter
  • 1 head Cauliflower, Broken Into Bite Size Pieces Not Too Small
  • 8 whole Chicken Thighs or Drumsticks
  • 2 tablespoons Flat Leaf Parsley, Chopped
  • 1 teaspoon Fresh Or Dried Rosemary
  • 3 cloves Garlic, Minced
  • 1 whole Lemon Juiced and Zested
  • 2 tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 1 teaspoon Pepper
  • 1 teaspoon Sea Salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon Thyme

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
  • Season chicken on both sides with salt, pepper, rosemary, thyme, and lemon zest.
  • Heat a oven proof skillet over medium-high heat with butter and olive oil until butter melts.
  • Add chicken skin side down and cook until golden brown about 7 to 8 minutes per side. Flip chicken and brown the other side.
  • Stack chicken on side of pan or remove to plate.
  • Place cauliflower and garlic in pan and coat with pan juices. Cook for approximately 3 minutes or until bottom of cauliflower has a bit of color. Stir.
  • Place chicken on top of cauliflower and place in to oven for about 30 - 35 minutes or until chicken is cooked through and no longer pink near the bone.
  • Top with parsley and fresh squeezed lemon.
