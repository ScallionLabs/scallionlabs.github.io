Indian Chicken (Paleo, Whole30 + Keto)

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs chicken breast, cut into cubes
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 poblano pepper, diced (or sub bell pepper)
  • 1 head cauliflower, riced (about 6 cups)
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice (about 1/2 lemon)
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro (for serving)

Preparation

  • Make the seasoning by combining all of the seasoning ingredients in a small bowl or jar and mixing well.
  • Cut chicken into cubes and toss with the seasoning to cover the chicken cubes.
  • Rice cauliflower and chop the onion, pepper and cilantro. Set aside.
  • Heat a large skillet over medium heat and add 1 tbsp ghee. Once the skillet is hot, add the chicken pieces, onion and poblano pepper to cook. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the chicken is fully cooked, about 8-10 minutes.
  • Add the cauliflower rice, lemon juice, salt and pepper to the pan with the chicken.
  • Mix everything together and cook, stirring occasionally, until the rice just starts to soften, about 3-4 minutes.
  • Remove from heat and serve chicken and cauliflower rice topped with chopped cilantro.
