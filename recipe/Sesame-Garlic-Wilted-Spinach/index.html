Sesame Garlic Wilted Spinach

  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 5min

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of baby spinach
  • 1 clove of garlic, thinly sliced
  • Seasoned rice vinegar, to taste
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce

Preparation

  • Heat the sesame oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Once the oil is hot, add the garlic and cook, stirring constantly, for 1 minute.
  • Add the soy sauce and spinach then cook, stirring occasionally, until the spinach is nearly wilted.
  • Remove from the heat then drizzle with the seasoned vinegar, to taste then sprinkle with sesame seeds, if desired.
  • Serve immediately.
