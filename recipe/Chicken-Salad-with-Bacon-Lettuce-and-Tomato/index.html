Chicken Salad with Bacon, Lettuce and Tomato

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado - peeled, seeded and sliced
  • 5 slices bacon
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 3 cups chopped cooked chicken breast
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons chopped green onion
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 3/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 12 leaves romaine lettuce
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup chopped fresh tomato
  • 1 dash Worcestershire sauce

Preparation

  • Place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown.
  • Drain, crumble and set aside to cool.
  • Prepare the dressing by mixing together the mayonnaise, parsley, green onions, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, salt and pepper.
  • In a medium bowl, stir together the chicken breast, tomatoes and bacon.
  • Pour dressing over chicken mixture and toss well to coat. Refrigerate until chilled; serve over lettuce leaves and garnish with avocado slices.
