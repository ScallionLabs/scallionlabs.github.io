Cream Cheese Chicken Stuffed Peppers

  • https://www.bunsinmyoven.com/2017/03/27/cream-cheese-chicken-stuffed-peppers/
  • Servings: 8
  • Ready in: 50min
  • Preparation time: 10min
  • Cooking time: 40min
  • Tags:
    Ketogenic Lunch Main Course Main Dish Dinner Gluten Free

Ingredients

  • 4 bell peppers, any color
  • 4 ounces cheddar, grated
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, room temperature
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 jalapeno, minced
  • 4 ounces monterey jack, grated
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups cooked, shredded chicken

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 350 degrees.Slice peppers in half and remove the seeds and membrane.
  • Place in a 9x13 baking dish.
  • Add chicken, cream cheese, monterey jack, cheddar, jalapeno, cumin, salt, and salsa to a medium mixing bowl and stir well to combine.Spoon chicken mixture evenly into each halved pepper.Cover tightly with foil and bake for 40 minutes.
  • Serve immediately.
