Lentil Tabouli

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp allspice
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 C finely chopped fresh mint
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 1/2 cups Puy Lentils ( Palouse Black Caviar Lentils)
  • 3 Tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large bunch Italian Parsley- finely chopped ( about 1 1/2 cups)
  • Cracked Pepper to taste
  • 1/3 C finely diced Red onion or shallot
  • 4 medium tomatoes- (2 cups finely diced)
  • 3 cups water

Preparation

  • In a medium pot bring lentils and water to a boil. Turn down heat, cover and let simmer on med-low to low heat for 25-35 minutes, until just tender. Most of the water will be gone.Strain and rinse with cold water until lentils are nice and cold. While lentils are simmering. Finely dice tomatoes, onion, parsley and mint, and place in a medium sized bowl. Toss in lentils and mix in olive oil, lemon juice, kosther salt, pepper, cinnamon and allspice. Don't be tempted to leave the spices out, trust me, they work well. Toss. Adjust salt and lemon if necessary. Chill until ready to serve.
  • Garnish with lemon zest.
