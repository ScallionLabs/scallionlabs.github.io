Carrot, Beet and Pineapple Juice

Ingredients

  • 1 beet, peeled
  • 5 carrots
  • ½” piece of ginger root
  • ¼ pineapple peeled

Preparation

  • One by one, place your ingredients onto your juicer and juice.
  • Pour into a glass and refrigerate while you clean your juicer.
