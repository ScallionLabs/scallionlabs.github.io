Preparation

  • Place the olive oil and butter for the lamb chops in a large skillet over medium-high heat.Liberally season the lamb chops with salt and pepper.Once hot, add the onions to the skillet and let brown on one side, about 3-5 minutes. Flip onions over, cover the skillet and let brown on the other side for another 3-5 minutes.
  • Remove lid from the skillet, place the lamb chops among the onions and cook for about 3-4 minutes per side for medium rare.Make the sauce by combining all the ingredients except the olive oil in a food processor. With the processor running, drizzle the olive oil in and process until well blended.
  • Serve the lamb chops and onions with the sauce.
