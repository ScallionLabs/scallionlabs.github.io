Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup canola oil
  • 18 oz. Coca-Cola
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 3 lbs of meat: I used ribeye steaks that I trimmed the extra fat from and cut up into cubes; tri tips would also be great!
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire Sauce

Preparation

  • In a large tupperware container, whisk together all ingredients of meat marinade.
  • Add in the meat and stir to coat all of it in marinade. Cover tightly with a lid and marinate 24 hours.To grill, soak wooden skewers in water for 1 hour, then skewer meat onto wet sticks. Grill anywhere from 1 minute to 6 minutes per side, depending how hot your grill is and how you like your meat cooked.
  • Remove from the grill to a clean plate, cover with foil and rest 5-10 minutes before serving.
