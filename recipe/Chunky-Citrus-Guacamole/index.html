Chunky Citrus Guacamole

  • http://www.foodiecrush.com/2012/03/craving-chunky-citrus-guacamole/
  • Servings: 3
  • Ready in: 45min
  • Tags:
    Vegetarian Side Dish Gluten Free Dairy Free Lacto Ovo Vegetarian Vegan Mexican

Macros

Ingredients

  • 5 avocados, chopped
  • 1/4 teaspoon chili powder
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jalapeno, seeds and veins removed and finely diced
  • 1 lime, juiced (1/8 cup)
  • 1 small orange, juiced (1/4 cup)
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt or to taste
  • 1/8 teaspoon smoked paprika (optional)
  • 1/3 cup tomato, diced
  • 1/3 cup white onion, diced

Preparation

  • Place avocado, onion, tomato, garlic and jalapeno in a medium size bowl.In a small bowl, combine orange and lime juices with chili powder, smoked paprika and salt.
  • Mix well.
  • Pour 3/4 of the juice mixture over the avocado mixture and gently combine, keeping the avocado chunky.
  • Add more of the juice mixture to taste.
  • Serve with tortilla chips, sliced fresh vegetables or as a topping for a tasty sandwich.
