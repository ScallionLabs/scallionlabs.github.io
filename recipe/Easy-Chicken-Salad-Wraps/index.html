Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sliced almonds
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 2 1/2 cup cooked and diced chicken breasts
  • 1/4 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 6 cranks black pepper
  • romaine lettuce, optional
  • 1/4 cup sour cream

Preparation

  • Mix all the ingredients in a large bowl until fully combined. To serve, lay a piece of romaine lettuce (optional) onto a wheat tortilla (or bread). ENJOY!
