Pasta Salad with Tuna and Beans

  • http://thelemonbowl.com/2012/07/pasta-salad-with-tuna-and-beans.html
  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 25min
  • Preparation time: 10min
  • Cooking time: 15min
  • Tags:
    Pescetarian Salad Dairy Free Pescatarian

Ingredients

  • 15 oz can cannelini beans - drained and rinsed
  • 2 celery stalks - diced
  • Juice and zest of 1 lemon
  • 2 Tbs olive oil
  • ¼ c parsley - minced
  • ¼ c pine nuts - toasted
  • 1 red pepper - diced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • ¼ c scallions - diced
  • 1 tomato - diced
  • 6 oz tuna canned in water - drained
  • 6 oz whole wheat penne

Preparation

  • Cook whole wheat penne in salty water according to package directions (about 8 minutes for whole wheat.)
  • Place pasta in a large bowl and mix in beans, tuna, red pepper, scallions, celery, parsley and tomato.Toast pine nuts in a dry pan over medium heat for about 5-6 minutes until brown; set aside to cool.Meanwhile, drizzle olive oil, lemon zest and lemon juice over the pasta salad and mix well.Take a bite to check for seasoning and add salt and pepper if needed.
  • Sprinkle on toasted pine nuts before serving.
