Teriyaki Salmon and Broccoli Bowls

  • http://damndelicious.net/2015/06/01/teriyaki-salmon-and-broccoli-bowls/
  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 40min
  • Preparation time: 15min
  • Cooking time: 25min
  • Tags:

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces broccoli florets, about 2-3 cups
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon freshly grated ginger
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1/4 cup reduced sodium soy sauce
  • 1 cup uncooked rice
  • 4 (5-ounce) salmon fillets

Preparation

  • In a large saucepan of 1 1 /2 cups water, cook rice according to package instructions; set aside. In a small bowl, whisk together cornstarch and 1/4 cup water; set aside. In a small saucepan over medium heat, add soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic, ginger, honey and 1 cup water; bring to a simmer. Stir in cornstarch mixture until thickened enough to coat the back of a spoon, about 2 minutes; let cool to room temperature. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Lightly coat an 88 baking dish with nonstick spray.
  • Place salmon filets along with the soy sauce mixture into the prepared baking dish and bake until the fish flakes easily with a fork, about 14-15 minutes.
  • Place broccoli florets into a steamer or colander set over a pan of boiling water. Cover and steam for 5 mins, or until cooked through and vibrant green.
  • Serve salmon immediately with rice and broccoli.
