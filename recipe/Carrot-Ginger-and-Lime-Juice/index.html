Carrot, Ginger, and Lime Juice

  • Ready in: 45min

Minerals RDA %

Macros

Ingredients

  • 2½ pounds carrots, scrubbed, tops trimmed
  • 1 4” piece ginger
  • ¼ cup fresh lime juice

Preparation

  • Pass carrots and ginger through a juicer; stir in lime juice.
  • Serve over ice.
