Thai Ginger Salmon Basil Arugula Salad

  • http://lexiscleankitchen.com/2014/04/06/thai-ginger-basil-arugula-salmon-salad/
  • Servings: 2
  • Ready in: 18min
  • Preparation time: 10min
  • Cooking time: 8min
  • Tags:
    Pescetarian Salad Gluten Free Dairy Free Paleolithic Primal Pescatarian Thai Asian
  • High in: Selenium B12 B6

Ingredients

  • 3 cups fresh arugula
  • 1 tbsp organic coconut aminos (or soy sauce/tamari sauce)
  • 1 cucumber, sliced thin
  • 1 tbsp dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp evoo
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil
  • 1 1/2 tbsp fresh grated ginger (more to taste)
  • 1 clove crushed garlic
  • 1/4 of a lemon juiced
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • Dash of pepper
  • 1 tsp organic raw honey
  • Dash red pepper flakes
  • 1 lb. wild salmon

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to broil
  • Mix marinade ingredients in a bowl, place salmon in glass dish, cover with marinade and set aside
  • In a bowl, combine dressing ingredients; mix well and place in refrigerator until ready to use
  • Place salmon into oven for about 8 minutes
  • While salmon is cooking, slice cucumbers
  • In a large bowl add cucumbers, arugula, and basil
  • Remove salmon, let cool for 5 minutes, chop up and place on top of salad
  • Serve with dressing
