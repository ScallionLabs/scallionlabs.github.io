Bacon Ranch Potato Salad

  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 45min
  • Preparation time: 30min
  • Cooking time: 15min

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon champagne vinegar
  • 1 pound bacon - cooked and crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons dijon mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 3/4 - 1 cup mayonnaise (more or less depending on how creamy you want to the salad to be)
  • 2 tablespoons parsley - chopped
  • Pepper - to taste
  • 1/4 cup ranch salad dressing
  • 4 cups cubed red potatoes
  • Salt - to taste
  • 1/2 cup chopped yellow onion

Preparation

  • In a large pot add potatoes and cover with cold water. Bring to a boil and let potatoes cook until tender. About 15 minutes.
  • Drain. In a large bowl add potatoes, chopped onion and bacon. Set aside. In a small bowl whisk together mayonnaise, ranch dressing, dijon mustard, champagne vinegar, garlic, salt and pepper.
  • Pour mayonnaise mixture overtop potato mixture, toss gently and let sit overnight (covered in a refrigerator) for the best flavor. Season with more salt and pepper if needed after sitting. When you are ready to serve sprinkle with parsley, chives or green onion. *Note: The bacon will bring a nice level of salt to the dish so keep this in mind when salting the potato salad.
