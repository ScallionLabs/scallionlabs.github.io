Lentil and Collard Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 bunch collard greens, rinsed, stemmed, and roughly chopped
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/3 cup lemon juice
  • 1 cup dry red lentils, rinsed and picked over
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 6 cups water

Preparation

  • Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat, stir in onion, garlic and salt; cook, stirring often, until softened and translucent, about 4 minutes. Stir in lentils, and cook for 1 minute.
  • Pour in water, then bring to a boil over high heat; lower heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until the lentils are tender, about 15 minutes.Meanwhile, heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  • Add collard greens, and cook until wilted, about 10 minutes. When the lentils are tender, stir in the collard greens and season with cumin, cinnamon, and garlic; simmer 10 more minutes. Stir in lemon juice before serving.
