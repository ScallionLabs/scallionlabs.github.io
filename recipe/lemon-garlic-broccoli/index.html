lemon garlic broccoli

Ingredients

  • 5 cups raw broccoli, cut into florets
  • 1 tablespoon chopped garlic
  • 1 ½ teaspoon lemon juice
  • ½ teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
  • rounded ¼ teaspoon salt

Preparation

  • Bring 1 to 2 inches of water to a boil in a large saucepan fitted with a steamer attachment.
  • Add broccoli, cover and steam until crisp tender, about 3 ½ minutes. Meanwhile, heat oil and garlic in a medium skillet over medium heat, stirring occasionally. Cook until the garlic is fragrant and just starting to brown. Scrape garlic mixture into a large bow. Immediately add lemon juice.
  • Add lemon zest, salt and pepper. Stir broccoli into the lemon mixture and stir to coat.
