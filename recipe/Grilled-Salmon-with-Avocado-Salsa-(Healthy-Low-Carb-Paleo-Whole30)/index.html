Grilled Salmon with Avocado Salsa (Healthy, Low-Carb, Paleo, Whole30)

  • Servings: 2
  • Ready in: 22min
  • Preparation time: 10min
  • Cooking time: 12min

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe avocado, pitted and diced
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon chili powder
  • 2 tablespoons cilantro, minced
  • ½ teaspoon cumin
  • 1 clove garlic, minced or crushed
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons onion, diced
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • 2 (4-6 oz) salmon fillets
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • ½ cup tomato, diced (any type of tomato)

Preparation

  • Stir the olive oil, garlic, and spices in a small bowl.
  • Brush or rub salmon with the spice mixture.
  • Heat a large heavy-duty (preferably non-stick) pan or grill medium-high heat.
  • Add salmon to the pan and cook for 5-6 minutes per side.
Remove from pan, top with avocado salsa and serve immediately.To make the avocado salsa
  • Add the avocado, tomato, onion, and cilantro to a large mixing bowl.
  • Drizzle with olive oil, fresh lime juice and a pinch of salt and pepper. Gently mix with a spoon until fully combined. Cover with plastic wrap until ready to serve.
