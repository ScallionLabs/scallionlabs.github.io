Crispy Indian Spiced Potato Wedges

  • http://www.jocooks.com/side-dishes/crispy-indian-spiced-potato-wedges/
  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 40min
  • Preparation time: 10min
  • Cooking time: 30min
  • Tags:
    Vegetarian Side Dish Gluten Free Dairy Free Lacto Ovo Vegetarian Vegan Indian Asian

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp dry basil
  • ¼ tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • ½ tbsp ginger and garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil, plus extra for brushing parchment paper
  • 1 tsp dry oregano
  • 6 large potatoes or about 8 to 10 smaller potatoes, peeled and cut into wedges
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp turmeric

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 425 F degrees.Line baking tray with aluminum foil and parchment paper, or just parchment paper, but make sure it covers the entire tray.
  • Brush olive oil over the parchment paper.In a small bowl combine the rest of the ingredients and slightly whisk.
  • Pour this mixture over the potatoes and toss them around to make sure each wedge is fully coated in the spice.
  • Place the potatoes on the baking sheet and bake on the middle rack for 15 minutes. Take the tray out and turn wedges over and bake for another 10 or 15 minutes or until potatoes are done.
