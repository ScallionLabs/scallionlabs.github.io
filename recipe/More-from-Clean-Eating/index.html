Ingredients

  • 6 packed cups fresh baby spinach
  • 10 egg whites
  • 1/2 tsp fresh ground black pepper, divided
  • 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 3 cups portobello mushrooms, chopped
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
  • 3/4 cup low-fat sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup skim milk
  • 4 whole-grain English muffins, split and toasted
  • 2 tbsp whole-wheat flour

Preparation

  • Place flour in a heavy saucepan over medium heat; gradually add milk, stirring with a whisk until blended. Cook for about 6 to 8 minutes or until thickened.
  • Remove from heat, add cheese, salt and 1/4 tsp pepper, stirring until melted.
  • Heat oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat.
  • Add and saut spinach, mushrooms and remaining pepper until spinach is wilted and mushrooms are tender, about 5 minutes.In a separate skillet coated with cooking spray, scramble egg whites over medium heat for 3 to 5 minutes. Top English muffin halves evenly with scrambled egg whites and spinach-mushroom mixture, then drizzle with cheese sauce.
  • Serve immediately.
