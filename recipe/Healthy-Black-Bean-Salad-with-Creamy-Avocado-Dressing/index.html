Preparation

  • Puree the avocado and lime juice until smooth (can also be mashed with a fork or hand blender).
  • Place the beans, lettuce, tomatoes, corn, pepper, pumpkin seeds, and cilantro in a large bowl, top with the avocado mixture, and toss everything together until evenly coated.Season to taste with salt and pepper.
