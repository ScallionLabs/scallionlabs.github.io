Salmon Florentine

  • Servings: 2
  • Ready in: 25min
  • Preparation time: 5min
  • Cooking time: 20min
  • Tags:
    Ketogenic Lunch Main Course Main Dish Dinner Gluten Free Pescatarian
  • High in: K A Selenium Saturated Fat B12

Ingredients

  • 6 oz baby bella mushrooms, sliced
  • 3 large garlic cloves, pressed
  • ¾ cup heavy whipping cream
  • ½ cup diced onion
  • ½ tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 lb salmon
  • Salt
  • 5 oz fresh spinach, chopped
  • ¼ cup white wine

Preparation

  • Preheat the oven to 425 and line a small rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper.Rub the bottom of the salmon with oil and place it skin down on the parchment paper.Season salmon with salt and pepper.
  • Bake for 13-18 minutes, depending on the size and thickness of your salmon piece.While salmon is cooking, heat up a large pan over medium heat.
  • Add some cooking oil and diced onion.
  • Saute onion until transparent.
  • Add mushrooms, garlic, and spinach. Cover and let the spinach cook down, stirring occasionally.Once spinach is cooked down, drain off the liquid that accumulated from mushrooms and spinach.Return to the stove and add white wine. Simmer for a few minutes to cook down the alcohol.
  • Add heavy whipping cream and mix well. Season with salt and red pepper flakes. Stir and cook for a few minutes.
