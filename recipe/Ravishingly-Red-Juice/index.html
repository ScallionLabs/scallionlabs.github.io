Ingredients

  • 2 small beets or 1 large beet plus beet greens
  • 1/2 inch piece of fresh ginger root
  • Pomegranate seeds from a small to medium pomegranate
  • 1/4 of a medium red cabbage

Preparation

  • Chop everything into chunks that fit into the juicer chute.Pass all the ingredients through the juicer, alternating hard and soft ingredients.
  • Pour juice into ice-filled glass and serve immediately.
