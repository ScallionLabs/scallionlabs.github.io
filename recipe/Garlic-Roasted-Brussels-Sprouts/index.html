Garlic Roasted Brussels Sprouts

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds fresh Brussels sprouts, cut in half (or quarters if sprouts are large)
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 6 garlic cloves, rough chopped
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 medium red onion, sliced

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 450F. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
  • Add Brussels sprouts, onions and garlic to pan.
  • Drizzle with olive oil.
  • Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Toss with clean hands, until coated. Wiggle the pan until Brussels spouts are in a single layer.
  • Bake 20-25 minutes until fork tender.
  • Serve and enjoy!PRINT RECIPERecipe developed by Donna Elick - The Slow Roasted Italian
