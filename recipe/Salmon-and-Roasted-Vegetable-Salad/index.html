Salmon & Roasted Vegetable Salad

  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 45min
  • Preparation time: 25min
  • Cooking time: 20min
  • Tags:
    Pescetarian Salad Gluten Free Dairy Free Pescatarian

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon minced anchovy fillet or paste
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 8 cups mixed salad greens
  • 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, divided
  • 3/4 teaspoon freshly ground pepper, divided
  • 2 tablespoons sherry vinegar or red-wine vinegar
  • 6 cups cubed (1/2-inch) peeled root vegetables, such as potatoes, turnips, carrots and beets
  • 2 6- to 7-ounce cans boneless, skinless wild Alaskan salmon, drained and flaked
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, divided
  • 2 scallions, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon whole-grain mustard

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 450F.Toss root vegetables in a large bowl with 1 tablespoon oil, 1/2 teaspoon pepper and 1/4 teaspoon salt.
  • Spread in a single layer on a large rimmed baking sheet. Roast for 15 minutes. Stir and continue roasting until soft and golden brown in spots, 13 to 15 minutes more.Meanwhile, whisk the remaining 2 tablespoons oil, vinegar, garlic, mustard, anchovy and the remaining 1/4 teaspoon each pepper and salt in a large bowl. Reserve 2 tablespoons of the dressing in small bowl.
  • Add the salad greens to the large bowl and toss to combine; divide among 4 dinner plates.When the vegetables are done, transfer them to the large bowl and gently combine with the reserved dressing, salmon and scallions. Top the greens with the salmon and vegetables.
