Spinach Bell Pepper Dip

  • Servings: 10
  • Ready in: 40min
  • Preparation time: 10min
  • Cooking time: 30min
  • Tags:
    Ketogenic Condiment Dip Spread Gluten Free Super Bowl
  • High in: K

Minerals RDA %

Ingredients

  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 10 oz. frozen spinach, defrosted, drained, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 1 cup mozarella, shredded
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 1/2 cup parmesan, grated
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 8 oz. sour cream
  • 1 yellow onion, chopped

Preparation

  • preheat oven to 350 degrees Fheat olive oil in a skillet over medium heatadd onion to skillet and cook for 3-4 minutes until softenedplace bell pepper into pan and cook until just softenedadd garlic and cook for 30 seconds to 1 minute, then remove the skillet from the heat and set asidebeat cream cheese, parmesan, sour cream together until smoothadd in the thawed spinach, onion bell pepper mixture, 1/2 cup mozarella and salt and pepper and stirgrease a 3 to 4 quart casserole dish with cooking spray and add the cheese mixturespread into an even layer and top with remaining mozarellaplace in oven and bake for 25 to 30 minutesremove and serve with bread or crackers for dipping
  • Enjoy!!
