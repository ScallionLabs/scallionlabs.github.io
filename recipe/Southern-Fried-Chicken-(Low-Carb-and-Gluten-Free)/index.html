Southern Fried Chicken (Low Carb and Gluten Free)

Ingredients

  • 5lbs chicken leg quarters
  • 1 cup coconut flour
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • oil for frying
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp salt

Preparation

In a large bowl, combine the chicken, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika. Using your hands, massage the spices into the chicken and be sure its well coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.To prepare
  • Add the coconut flour to the seasoned chicken and toss well to coat.
  • Heat oil about 2 inches deep in a large, heavy bottomed pan (cast iron is ideal or use a deep fryer if you have one) to 375 degrees (F).
  • Add chicken in batches. Dont overcrowd the pan or it wont get crisp. Cook for about 8 minutes per side, or until a rich, golden brown.Test with a meat thermometer to ensure that the internal temperature of the meat reaches 165 degrees. If you dont have one, cut into a piece and make sure the meat isnt pink before serving.
