Lobster Cobb Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces avocado, diced
  • 2 cups baby greens
  • 1/2 cup *cooked corn kernels (from 1 fresh cobb)
  • 1 tsp dijon mustard
  • 2 hard boiled eggs, sliced
  • 1/2 cup quartered grape tomatoes
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 10 oz cooked, chilled lobster meat (yield from 2 – 1-1/2 lb lobsters)
  • 4 tsp olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons chopped red onion
  • 2 tbsp plus 1 tsp red wine vinegar
  • 4 slices center cut bacon, cooked and crumbled

Preparation

  • Combine the first 4 ingredients for the vinaigrette in a small bowl and whisk well, add onions and set aside.
  • Cut the lobster into large chunks.
  • Place 1 cup greens in each bowl and top with lobster, avocado, eggs, bacon, tomatoes and corn.
  • Drizzle 2 1/2 tbsp vinaigrette over each salad and enjoy.
