Braised Kale and Tomatoes

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup chicken stock
  • 3 cloves garlic, roughly chopped
  • 2 bunches curly kale, cleaned well, center stems removed, and torn
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 3 to 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 2 cups whole peeled tomatoes
  • 1 yellow onion, chopped

Preparation

  • Watch how to make this recipe.
  • Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Once hot, stir in the onions and saute until softened, about 4 minutes. Toss in the red pepper, garlic, kale and some salt and pepper and continue to cook for 2 more minutes.
  • Pour in the tomatoes and stir to combine, then add the chicken stock. Turn the heat to medium, cover with a lid and cook until the kale is silky and tender, about 15 minutes.
