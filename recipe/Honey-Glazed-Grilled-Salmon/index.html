Honey Glazed Grilled Salmon

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 2 tsp honey
  • 1 lb salmon fillet
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tsp soy sauce

Preparation

  • Preheat an indoor grill (can also use outdoor grill).
  • Cut the salmon fillet into individual pieces (I got four pieces out of mine).
  • Sprinkle salt and pepper over the top side to season.In a small bowl, mix honey, soy sauce and ginger together with a fork.
  • Place the salmon SKIN SIDE UP on the grill, and close. Cook for 3 minutes, then flip and close grill again.Cook for 2 minutes, then brush the glaze over the salmon and close again.After 30 seconds, re-open and brush glaze over the salmon. Repeat this a few times until the salmon is fully cooked (you can check by flaking a pieces off with a fork - should come off easily).
