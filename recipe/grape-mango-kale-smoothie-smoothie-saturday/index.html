grape mango kale smoothie {smoothie saturday}

Ingredients

  • 1 carrot, cut into chunks
  • 8 ounces coconut water
  • 1 date
  • 1 1/2 cups of ice
  • juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 1 cup kale
  • 1 cup mango, fresh or frozen
  • 1/2 cup red grapes
  • 1/2 cup watermelon

Preparation

  • Place all ingredients in a blender (I used my Blendtec) and blend until smooth.
