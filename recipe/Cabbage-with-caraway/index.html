Cabbage with caraway

Ingredients

  • 1 Savoy cabbage, cored and shredded
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2-3 tsp caraway seeds

Preparation

  • Cook the cabbage in boiling water for 3 mins until tender, then drain.
  • Heat the oil in a frying pan.
  • Add the onion and cook for 2-3 mins until starting to soften and turn golden.
  • Sprinkle over the caraway seeds and cook for a further 2 mins until fragrant. Stir in the cabbage and heat through.
