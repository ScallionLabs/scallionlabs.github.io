Crispy Buffalo Wings

  • Ready in: 105min

  • Cooking time: 40min

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 tablespoons baking powder
  • 2 lbs chicken wings
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon curry
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Preparation

  • In a large bowl, combine baking powder, salt, garlic powder, curry, chili powder until blended and whisk to combine well.
  • Add the chicken wings into this mixture and coat each evenly with the mixture.
  • Pour olive oil over them and stir.Cover with plastic wrap and let it rest in the refrigerator at least for an hour (overnight is the best).Preheat oven to 400F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  • Place chicken wings on the baking sheet and bake for 30 minutes. After this time, remove them from oven, turn them over and bake for another 10 minutes.
  • Serve hot.
