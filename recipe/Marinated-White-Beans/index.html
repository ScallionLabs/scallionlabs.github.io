Marinated White Beans

Ingredients

  • 2 15oz. cans butter beans $2.18
  • 1 clove garlic, minced $0.08
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice $0.05
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil $0.22
  • ½ tsp dried oregano $0.05
  • ¼ bunch fresh parsley $0.20
  • Salt and pepper to taste $0.05

Preparation

  • Drain the beans and rinse them with cool water in a colander.
  • Let the excess water drain away.Rinse and drain the parsley. Pull the parsley leaves from the stems, then chop them roughly.
  • Combine the rinsed beans, chopped parsley, olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, oregano, a pinch of salt, and some freshly cracked pepper in a bowl. Stir until everything is well mixed. Taste the beans and adjust the salt if needed. Refrigerate until ready to eat.
