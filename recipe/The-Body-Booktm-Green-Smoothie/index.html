Ingredients

  • ¼ large avocado (or ½ of a small)
  • ½ frozen banana
  • dash of cinnamon
  • ½ green apple
  • juice of 1 meyer lemon/regular lemon
  • 2 cups organic spinach
  • stevia (optional only to sweeten)
  • 1½ cups of filtered cold water

Preparation

  • Simply combine all ingredients in a high speed blender until smooth and creamy.See notes for further details on variations.Enjoy!
