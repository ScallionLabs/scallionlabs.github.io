Lamb Chops with Roasted Salsa

Ingredients

  • 2 bell peppers, seeded and quartered
  • 2 teaspoons freshly ground black pepper, divided
  • 1/4 cup chopped flat-leaf parsley
  • 2 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt, divided
  • 8 lamb rib chops (about 1 3/4 lbs. total)
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • Finely shredded zest of 1 1/2 lemons
  • 3 tablespoons chopped mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup olive oil, divided
  • 1 small red onion, peeled and halved
  • 1 ripe tomato

Preparation

  • Prepare a charcoal or gas grill for high heat (450 to 550; you can hold your hand 5 in. above cooking grate only 2 to 4 seconds). In a large resealable plastic bag, combine garlic, mint, lemon zest, 1/4 cup olive oil, and 1 tsp. each salt and pepper.
  • Add lamb chops and mix to coat.
  • Grill tomato, onion, and bell peppers, turning twice, until soft and blackened, 6 minutes.
  • Remove from heat.
  • Remove chops from bag, keeping marinade on meat. Grill chops, turning once, until done the way you like, 5 minutes total for medium-rare (cut to test).
  • Chop vegetables and mix with remaining 1/4 cup olive oil, the parsley, lemon juice, and remaining 1 tsp. each salt and pepper.
  • Serve with lamb.
