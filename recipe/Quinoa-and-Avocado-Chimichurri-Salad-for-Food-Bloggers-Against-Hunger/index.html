Quinoa and Avocado Chimichurri Salad for Food Bloggers Against Hunger

Ingredients

  • 2 avocados, peeled, pitted, and sliced
  • ¼ cup chopped cilantro
  • 2 cups quinoa, cooked to package directions
  • ¼ cup fruity flavored extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • kosher salt and freshly ground pepper
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • ½ teaspoons dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoons red pepper flakes
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 cup roasted red bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 cups fresh spinach

Preparation

  • Combine lemon juice, vinegar, garlic, salt, red pepper flakes, oregano, and black pepper in small bowl.
  • Whisk in oil, then stir in cilantro and parsley.
  • Add spinach, roasted red bell pepper, quinoa and avocado to a bowl and dress with chimichurri sauce.
  • Garnish with feta cheese and serve with flaxseed crackers if desired.
