Ingredients

  • 30 ounces black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 30 ounces black-eyed peas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 bunch fresh parsley, minced
  • 1 whole green bell pepper, seeded and diced
  • 2 tbsp raw sugar
  • 1 whole red bell pepper, seeded and diced
  • 30 ounces rotel tomatoes, drained and chopped
  • ¼ cup basalmic vinegar
  • 1 whole yellow bell pepper, seeded and diced

Preparation

  • Mix all ingredients together and allow to marinate for at least twelve hours. Chill before serving.Nutritional Information
