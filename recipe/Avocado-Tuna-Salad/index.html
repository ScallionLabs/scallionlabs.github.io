Avocado Tuna Salad

  • Cooking time: 10min

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 1/4 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp dried dill
  • 2 cans of flaked light tuna
  • 1 T pickle juice (or water-if using increase salt slightly)
  • 1/2 red apple, chopped
  • 1/2 cup chopped red onion
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup chopped, toasted walnuts

Preparation

  • In a large bowl, mash up avocado with the back of a fork or potato masher.
  • Add in tuna, celery, red onion, apple and walnuts.
  • Mix well.
  • Add in pickle juice, dried dill, dijon, cumin and salt and pepper.
  • Mix well.
  • Serve on bread, top on salad or eat plain.Will keep up to 1 week in refrigerator.
