Preparation

  • Top cooked quinoa with carrot, red bell pepper, edamame, cabbage, and salmon.
  • Sprinkle with peanuts.
  • In a small bowl, combine peanut butter, soy sauce, vinegar, sesame oil, and lime juice, stirring well with a whisk.
  • Drizzle over bowl.
