Sesame Crusted Ahi Tuna Steak

Ingredients

  • 5 oz ahi tuna steak
  • 2 Tbs black sesame seeds ,. toasted
  • 1 Tbs oil
  • 2 Tbs white sesame seeds , toasted

Preparation

  • Press the tuna steak into the sesame seeds to coat.
  • Heat the oil in a pan.Fry the tuna steak for 2 minutes per side.
