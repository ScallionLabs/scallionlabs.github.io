Coconut Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 1.5 to 2 pounds chicken drumsticks
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon dried cilantro or 1 tablespoon fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 clove garlic
  • juice and zest of 1 lime (to get the zest of the lime, use a zester or the smallest holes on a cheese grater to shave the outer skin of the lime)
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 (13.5 ounce) can unsweetened coconut milk

Preparation

  • In a large bowl combine yogurt, olive oil, garlic, turmeric, cayenne pepper (if using), garam masala, salt, cumin, lime and lime zest.
  • Place chicken in marinade and toss to coat. Cover and refrigerate 8 hours or overnight.
  • Remove the chicken from the marinade. Reserve marinade.In a large skillet, cook chicken over medium heat until it browns slightly. About 4 minutes each side.
  • Add onions and cook 2 minutes.
  • Pour coconut milk and marinade over the top, stir. Bring to a boil and then reduce heat to medium-low. Simmer for 30-35 minutes or until chicken is cooked through.Stir in cilantro just before serving.
  • Serve.
