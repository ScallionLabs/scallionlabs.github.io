Chili Lime Jicama Sticks

Ingredients

  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Chipotle powder, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 pounds jicama, peeled and cut into 1/4-inch sticks
  • 1/4 fresh lime juice from 1 lime
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Fine sea salt, to taste

Preparation

  • Combine the jicama, lime juice, cilantro, and red pepper flakes in a large bowl and toss until well-incorporated.
  • Place the dressed jicama on a large platter and season with salt, pepper, and chipotle powder.
  • Serve immediately.
