Ingredients

  • 3 cups drained Cuban black beans
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro leaves (optional), plus more for garnish
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup olive oil, plus more for garnish
  • 1/4 cup well-stirred tahini
  • Water as needed
  • 3 cups drained Cuban black beans
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro leaves (optional), plus more for garnish
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup well-stirred tahini

Preparation

  • In a food processor fitted with a knife blade, combine all ingredients except water. Process until smooth.If a thinner consistency is desired, add 1 tablespoon of water at a time and process after each addition.
  • Transfer to a serving bowl.
  • Drizzle with olive oil and garnish with chopped fresh cilantro if desired.
  • Serve with crackers, pita bread, pita chips, or raw vegetables.
