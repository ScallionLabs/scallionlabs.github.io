Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • Handful of ice cubes ( or use frozen bananas)
  • 1 C kale, chopped broccoli, or spinach or any healthy green veggie
  • tablespoon of lemon
  • ½ teaspoon matcha powder, plus more to taste
  • 1 C fresh pineapple
  • ½ C nut or soy milk

Preparation

  • Blend all in a blender until very smooth. Enjoy!!
