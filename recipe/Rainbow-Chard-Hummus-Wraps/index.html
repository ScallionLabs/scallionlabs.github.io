Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup hummus
  • Veggies for topping, such as tomato, cucumber + onion
  • 1 large rainbow swiss chard leaf

Preparation

  • Rinse and dry your swiss chard, chop off the stem, and shave the bottom thicker part of the stem so it will roll up easier.Then, top the center with hummus and veggies, wrap up and enjoy. You can also secure it with toothpicks or wrap it in parchment to take on the go.
