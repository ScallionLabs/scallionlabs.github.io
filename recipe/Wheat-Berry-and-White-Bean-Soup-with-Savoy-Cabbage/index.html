Wheat Berry and White Bean Soup with Savoy Cabbage

Ingredients

  • freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 28 oz canned crushed tomatoes
  • 1 carrot, peeled and cut into 1/4-inch dice
  • 1 cup dried cannellini beans
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 tsp minced rosemary
  • salt, to taste
  • 1 Savoy cabbage, shredded
  • 1 cup wheat berries

Preparation

  • Soak the wheat berries and white beans in water for 6-8 hours.
  • Drain and combine the wheat berries and white beans in a large pot. Cover with water by about 2-inches and add a tbsp of salt. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer, covered, for an hour or until beans are tender.
  • Drain, reserving the cooking liquid.
  • Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium-low heat.
  • Add in the onion, rosemary, and carrot. Cook for about 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Add in the canned tomatoes, cabbage, and 1 tsp salt. Cook until the cabbage has wilted, about 5 minutes.
  • Add in the beans, farro, and bean cooking liquid plus enough water to equal 3 cups. Simmer for about 20 minutes.Season to taste with salt and black pepper.
