Ingredients

  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • ½ cup cilantro leaves left whole
  • 4 cups finely shredded Napa cabbage
  • ½ cup scallions sliced on the bias (tops and bottoms)

Preparation

  • In a medium bowl, mix cabbage, scallions, carrots and cilantro.
  • Add dipping sauce and toss.
  • Serve immediately.
