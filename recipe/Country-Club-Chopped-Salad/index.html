Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup pickled banana peppers
  • 4 thin slices deli ham
  • 4 thin slices deli turkey
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 2 thin slices provolone cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups chopped romaine hearts
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 small tomato
  • 2 teaspoons yellow mustard

Preparation

  • Add the chopped romaine to a medium bowl.
  • Place the turkey, ham, and cheese on a cutting board and chop into 1 inch pieces.
  • Add to the salad.Dice the tomato and banana peppers into bite sized pieces.
  • Add to the salad.
  • Whisk together the mayonnaise and mustard.
  • Add to the salad and toss.
  • Add salt and pepper, as desired.
