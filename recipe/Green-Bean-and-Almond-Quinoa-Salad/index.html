Green Bean & Almond Quinoa Salad

  • http://www.queenofquinoa.me/2013/05/green-bean-almond-quinoa-salad/
  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 15min
  • Preparation time: 5min
  • Cooking time: 10min
  • Tags:
    Vegetarian Salad Gluten Free Dairy Free Lacto Ovo Vegetarian Fodmap Friendly Vegan
  • High in: Manganese

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1/2 cup sliced almonds
  • 1 lb green beans, clean and chopped into 2" pieces
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 cups cooked quinoa (I have used both red & rainbow, and I actually prefer the red!)
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Preparation

  • Steam the green pieces until just tender, about 5 - 7 minutes. Allow them to cool slightly.
  • Add all the ingredients into a mixing bowl and toss to combine.
  • Serve immediately.
