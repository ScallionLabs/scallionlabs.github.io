Ingredients

  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons brown rice syrup
  • 2 tablespoons roasted peanut oil
  • 3/4 cup raw peanuts
  • 2 cups shredded red cabbage
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups shredded savoy cabbage
  • ¼ cup diced scallions, whites and greens

Preparation

  • Place the shredded cabbage and scallions in a large bowl.
  • Heat a medium dry skillet over medium-low heat.
  • Add nuts and let toast, stirring every 20 seconds or so, until the nuts are lightly browning.
  • Add to cabbage mixture.In a small bowl, whisk together the ingredients for the dressing.
  • Pour over the cabbage and toss until everything is well combined and cabbage is coated. Taste and add more salt if desired.
  • Let sit for 5 minutes before serving.
