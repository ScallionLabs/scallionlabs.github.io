Ingredients

  • 12 ounces baby spinach
  • 1 tablespoon bacon grease
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and ground black pepper
  • Pinch of crushed red pepper flakes (for a little spice because Trisha likes it)
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • 1/2 yellow onion, finely diced
  • 12 ounces baby spinach
  • 1 tablespoon bacon grease
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Pinch of crushed red pepper flakes (for a little spice because Trisha likes it)
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • 1/2 yellow onion, finely diced

Preparation

  • Watch how to make this recipe.
  • In a large saute pan, melt the bacon grease over medium heat.
  • Add the onion and garlic and stir to cook until the onions are very soft, about 5 minutes.
  • Add the vinegar, crushed red pepper flakes, salt and pepper and stir; add the spinach and toss it around, cooking until it's just wilted, 1 to 2 minutes.
  • Serve immediately so the spinach is wilted but not soggy.
