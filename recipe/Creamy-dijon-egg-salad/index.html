Creamy dijon egg salad

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon dijon mustard
  • 2 hard boiled eggs, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons green olives, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon parsley, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons plain greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons red cabbage, chopped
  • salt & pepper

Preparation

  • Combine all ingredients in a small bowl and mix together.Season with salt & pepper to taste.
  • Serve on toasted bread and garnish with additional chopped parsley.
