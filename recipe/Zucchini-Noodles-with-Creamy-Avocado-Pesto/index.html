Zucchini Noodles with Creamy Avocado Pesto

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 2 Tbsp lemon juice
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • Cracked black pepper, to taste
  • ¼ cup pine nuts
  • ½ tsp. sea salt
  • 2 large zucchini, spiralized

Preparation

  • Spiralize your zucchini and set aside on paper towels so that any excess water is soaked up.In a food processor, add avocados, basil leaves, garlic, pine nuts, lemon juice and sea salt and pulse until finely chopped. Then with the motor still running, add olive oil in a slow stream until emulsified and creamy.
  • Drizzle olive oil in a large skillet over medium high heat then add zucchini noodles, cooking for about 1 to 2 minutes until tender.
  • Add zucchini noodles to a large bowl and toss with avocado pesto. Season with cracked pepper and a little Parmesan, serve and enjoy!
