Grown Up Stir Fry

  • Ready in: 30min
  • Preparation time: 10min

  • High in: Saturated Fat

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 5 Button Mushrooms, de-stemed + chopped
  • ½ pint Cherry Tomatoes, halved
  • 1 Tbsp Dried Basil
  • Fat of Choice
  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 1 Onion, diced
  • Salt + Peppers

Preparation

  • Get a big skillet heating with some butter and add in your chopped onions & mushroom.Once those have started to soften add in your ground beef, spice, salt + pepper and basil.When you’re meats almost cooked add in your cherry tomatoes and let it all finish up.
