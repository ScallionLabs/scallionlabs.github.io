Cheese-Stuffed Meatballs

  • Ready in: 45min

Ingredients

  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 pound mozzarella, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons Italian parsley, minced
  • 1/2 white onion finely chopped

Preparation

  • In a large bowl, combine ground beef, onions, garlic, parsley, egg, salt, pepper and breadcrumbs (don't overmix or they'll be rock-solid).Divide mixture into 8 balls.Semi-flatten each ball, add a cube of mozzarella to the center and sculpt the meat mixture around it to form a seal.
  • Heat olive oil in a large, heavy pan and fry meatballs on each side (we know they're balls, find sides) for 3-5 minutes, or until browned.
  • Serve while piping hot for best cheese effect.
