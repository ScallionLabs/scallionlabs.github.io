  • Dipping Sauce
  • 1 large carrot, trimmed, peeled, and cut into 3–inch matchsticks
  • 1 cucumber, peeled, and cut into 3–inch matchsticks

  • 1/2 tbsp. soy sauce
  • 8 rice paper spring roll wrappers
  • 1/3 cup Thai basil

Preparation

  • Whisk together the dipping sauce ingredients and set aside.Squeeze the tofu between paper towels to release extra water.
  • Cut into 12 rectangles, about 2 inches by a quarter inch.Fill a shallow bowl with hot, but not boiling, water. Submerge 1 spring roll wrapper until softened.Assemble spring rolls.
  • Lay one wrapper flat and place two mint leaves lengthwise in center.
  • Layer on three pieces of tofu, several carrot and cucumber slices, a sprinkling of cilantro and basil.Top with a lettuce leaf.Fold the long edge of the wrapper over ingredients. Then fold in ends and continue rolling wrapper lengthwise.
  • Place seam side down on a plate. Repeat with remaining wrappers.
