Ingredients

  • baby carrots
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • sliced celery
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 pounds ground beef (I used ground chuck.)
  • sliced mushrooms
  • sliced onion
  • potatoes (cut into 1 inch cubes)
  • 2 teaspoons seasoned salt

Preparation

  • Combine ground beef, salt, garlic powder, and black pepper (and any other spices and seasoning you might like – I sometimes use a bit of Mrs. Dash also.)
  • Mix well and form meat into 4 to 5 hamburger patties.
  • Place each patty in the middle of a large piece of aluminum foil and top with your favorite veggies and a little more salt and pepper. Seal aluminum foil into a packet.
  • Bake at 350 degrees F for 50 to 60 minutes.
