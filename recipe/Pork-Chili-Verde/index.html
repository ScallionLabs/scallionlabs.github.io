Pork Chili Verde

Ingredients

  • 1/3 bunch fresh cilantro leaves, chopped
  • Garlic powder
  • 1 1/2 ounces canned diced green chiles
  • 1 21-ounce can green enchilada sauce
  • 1/3 bunch green onions, chopped
  • 1 fresh jalapeno, seeded and chopped
  • 1/3 large onion, chopped
  • 3 3/4 pounds lean pork, diced
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Preparation

  • Simmer the pork in large pot of water until tender.
  • Drain the pork and return it to the pot.
  • Add the enchilada sauce, cilantro, onion, green onion and green chiles and simmer until heated thoroughly.
