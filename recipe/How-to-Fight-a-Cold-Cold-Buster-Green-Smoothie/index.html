Ingredients

  • 1/2 small apple
  • 3 baby carrots
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup ice
  • 1/2 orange with peel
  • 1 cup orange juice
  • 1/2 cup frozen pineapple
  • 2 cups spinach

Preparation

  • Place the juice, spinach and remaining ingredients in a blender, ending with the ice. Blend until smooth and drink up!
