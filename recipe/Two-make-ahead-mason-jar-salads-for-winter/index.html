Two make-ahead mason jar salads {for winter}

Ingredients

  • 1 cup canned black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 2 whole carrots, peeled
  • 1 cup roasted cauliflower, cooled
  • 1/4 cup cubed feta
  • 4 cups chopped kale
  • 4 cups shredded napa cabbage
  • 1/2 cup pepitas
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • 1/4 cup Buttermilk Salad Dressing (or any creamy dressing)
  • 1 cup cubed roasted sweet potatoes

Preparation

  • Shred carrots into ribbons using a vegetable peeler.In the bottom of a clean, dry quart jar, spread 2 Tablespoons of salad dressing. Repeat with second jar.Divide carrot ribbons between each jar on top of the dressing.
  • Add the raisins next, then the roasted cauliflower and pepitas, dividing them between two jars, and layering them.Cover ingredients with 2 cups shredded napa cabbage.Top with a clean, dry lid and refrigerate for up to 5 days.In the bottom of a clean, dry quart jar, spread 2 Tablespoons of salad dressing. Repeat with second jar.Crumble 2 Tablespoons of feta into each jar on top of the dressing.
  • Add the black beans and the cubed roasted sweet potato, dividing them between two jars, and layering them.Top with chopped kale, again dividing the portion between the two jars.Top with a clean dry lid and refrigerate for up to 5 days.
