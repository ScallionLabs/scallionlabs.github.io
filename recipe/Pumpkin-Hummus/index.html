Pumpkin Hummus

  • http://littleleopardbook.com/2012/09/03/whats-for-a-side-pumpkin-hummus/
  • Servings: 2
  • Ready in: 10min
  • Tags:
    Vegetarian Gluten Free Dairy Free Lacto Ovo Vegetarian Vegan Middle Eastern
  • High in: Manganese Fat A B6

Vitamins RDA %

Minerals RDA %

Macros

Ingredients

  • 1 (29 oz) can garbanzo beans (chickpeas)
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1 cup pumpkin
  • 2 tbsp tahini

Preparation

  • Place all of the ingredients in a food processor.Blend until a creamy texture forms.
  • Place in the refrigerator for 30 minutes.
  • Serve with cinnamon sugar pita chips or apple slices.
