Mexican Style Omelette with Chorizo, Mushrooms and Tomatoes

  • http://www.jocooks.com/breakfast-2/mexican-style-omelette-with-chorizo-mushrooms-and-tomatoes/
  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 15min
  • Preparation time: 5min
  • Cooking time: 10min
  • Tags:

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp butter
  • ½ cup cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup chopped chorizo sausage
  • 6 eggs
  • green onions for garnish
  • 1 cup chopped mushrooms
  • 1 onion chopped
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped

Preparation

  • Turn on your oven to broil. In a large skillet, melt the butter and add the onion and saute for a couple minutes until the onion is tender and translucent.
  • Add the mushrooms and chorizo sausage and cook for a couple more minutes.
  • Add tomatoes and stir. Season with salt and pepper.Beat the eggs in a bowl.
  • Pour the eggs over the mushrooms and sausage.Cook the eggs, for just a minute and sprinkle the cheddar cheese over the top.To finish this frittata, place the skillet in the oven under the broiler for a couple minutes, just until the top is nice and golden.
  • Garnish with chives or green onions.
