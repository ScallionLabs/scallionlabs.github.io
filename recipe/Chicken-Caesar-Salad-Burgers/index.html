Chicken Caesar Salad Burgers

Ingredients

  • 1/2 teaspoon anchovy paste
  • 1 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Pinch of freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons caesar dressing (see below)
  • 3 Tablespoons caesar dressing
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced (about 1 tablespoons minced garlic)
  • 1 pound ground chicken
  • 1/4 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup homemade mayo (page 312) or store-bought
  • 2 teaspoons avocado oil or extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • Parmesan Crisps
  • Optional: 3 tablespoons grated parmesan cheese
  • Sliced red onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Shredded Romaine Lettuce
  • 1 Tablespoon chopped scallions
  • 1/2 teaspoon fine sea salt
  • Pinch of fine sea salt
  • 1 teaspoon sriracha
  • Sliced tomato

Preparation

  • Pre-heat the oven to 350F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.
  • Place all of the ingredients for the dressing in a high-speed blender and pulse once (about 5 seconds) just until the dressing is thick and smooth. If you over blend, the dressing will become watery.In a large bowl, toss all of the chicken burger ingredients and mix until well combined.Divide the mixture into 4-parts, and place 4 patties on the baking sheet.
  • Bake for 20-25 minutes.Broil on high for 2-3 minutes, or until the tops become a light golden brown.While the burgers are baking, make caesar slaw and assemble other desired toppings
  • Pre-heat oven to 400F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.Spoon cheese by tablespoonfuls two inches apart on the prepared baking sheet (1 tablespoon per crisp).
  • Bake for 6 minutes. Take out of the oven and let cool completely before lifting each crisp up with a spatula.
