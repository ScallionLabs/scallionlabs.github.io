Quick, Healthy and Super Simple Tuna Fish Salad

Ingredients

  • ½ avocado, diced
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • ¼ cup fresh mint, chopped
  • ¼ cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 green onion, sliced
  • 10 large kalamata olives, pitted
  • 2 cups mixed greens
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • ¾ tsp freshly cracked black pepper
  • ¼ tsp Himalayan or fine sea salt
  • 1 large tomato, diced
  • 1 can chunk light tuna in water, drained
  • 1 small zucchini, sliced lengthwise

Preparation

  • Grill the zucchini slices on both sides in a sizzling hot cast iron skillet grill pan (or on a very hot grill).
  • Remove from pan and let cool for a few minutes.
  • Cut into bite size pieces. Throw all the ingredients in a large mixing bowl and stir delicately until well combined.
  • Serve immediately.
