Easy Vegetarian Quinoa Chili

Ingredients

  • 1 small avocado
  • 2 can red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 large carrot, peeled and chopped
  • 1-2 tablespoons chili powder, depending on your taste
  • 1 cup corn kernels, thawed
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/4 medium onion, minced
  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation

  • Wash quinoa to remove saponins. Chop the carrot and pepper.In a large pot, heat the olive oil.
  • Add the onion and garlic and cook until translucent, about 2-3 minutes. Stir in the carrot, corn and peppers and cook about 3-4 minutes.
  • Add the quinoa, kidney beans and tomato sauce. Season with chili powder, cumin and salt. Simmer the chilli for about 20 minutes and add water if it gets dry.
  • Serve warm.
  • Garnish with avocado slices.
