Cinnamon toast crunch roasted chickpeas

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon cinnamon
  • 1 15oz. can chickpeas, drained, rinsed and thoroughly dried
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon tahini

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 425 degrees and grease a baking sheet.
  • Combine sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl until fully combined.Toss dried beans with tahini first to coat and then sprinkle cinnamon sugar and mix until all are coated.
  • Spread out onto baking sheet and roast for 15-20 minutes, stirring every 5 minutes so they don't burn on any one side.
