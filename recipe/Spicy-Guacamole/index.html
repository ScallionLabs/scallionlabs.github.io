Spicy Guacamole

Ingredients

  • 5 large ripe avocados
  • 2 cans diced tomatoes with green chilies (drain liquid from 1 can)
  • 1/2 c. chopped cilantro
  • 2 Tablespoons lime juice
  • 1/2 c. finely chopped red onion
  • salt and pepper

Preparation

  • Peel and scoop the avocados into a large bowl. Smash with a potato masher or a fork.Stir in the tomatoes and chilies, onion, cilantro, and lime juice. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Cover and refrigerate.
