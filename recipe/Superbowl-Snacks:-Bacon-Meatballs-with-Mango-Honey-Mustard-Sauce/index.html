Superbowl Snacks: Bacon Meatballs with Mango Honey Mustard Sauce

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup almond flour
  • 6 slices of bacon, cut into 1 inch pieces
  • ½ teaspoon chili powder
  • dash of chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 egg, whisked
  • 1.5lbs ground beef (or any other ground meat you see fit)
  • 2-3 tablespoons ground old fashioned mustard (in the jar)
  • 1 mango, peeled and cut away from seed inside
  • 1 tablespoon raw honey
  • salt, to taste
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1-2 tablespoons yellow mustard
  • ½ yellow onion, diced

Preparation

  • Preheat your oven to 350 degrees.Put a large skillet over medium heat and add your diced bacon to it.Once bacon has rendered some fat in the pan, add your onions.
  • Mix together and poke at it randomly to make sure the bacon and onions don’t burn. Once bacon is cook through, add the bacon and onions to a plate with a paper towel on it to cool and soak up some excess fat.Now add your ground beef to a large bowl, then add your cooled bacon and onions, along with egg, almond flour, and seasonings.Use your hands to mix all that goodness up thoroughly.Now roll into little bite size balls and place on a parchment paper lined cookie sheet.
  • Bake for around 10-15 minutes, depending on the size of the meatballs.While your meatballs are baking, time to make your sauce.
  • Add your peeled and sliced mango to a food processor and puree until your have a sauce.Now add your ground mustard, yellow mustard, and honey to it and puree it all together.Taste to see if you want it sweeter or more tangy and add extra honey or mustard as needed.
  • Add spices and salt and puree one more time.
  • Place some toothpicks in your meatballs and let guests dip away!
