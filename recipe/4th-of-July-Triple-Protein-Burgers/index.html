4th of July Triple Protein Burgers

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 8 strips of bacon
  • ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 4 hot dogs (nitrate and filler free- I used Coleman Natural hot dogs)
  • 5-6 tablespoons leftover homemade mayo
  • ¼ teaspoon onion powder
  • head of romaine lettuce
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • ½ teaspoon smoked paprika
  • ½ white onion, thinly sliced

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  • Place bacon on a baking sheet and cook bacon for about 15 minutes until just slightly crispy.In a large bowl, mix together ground beef, cayenne pepper, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, and a little salt and pepper. Then form in 4 patties.
  • Cut hot dog down the middle to butterfly the hot dog.
  • Place beef patties on the grill and cook on both sides until you get however you like your meat cooked. I cooked mine for about 4-5 minutes per side for medium rare.When flip the beef patties, place butterflied hot dogs on the grill and flip after about 2 minutes.When all meat is done cooking, place a beef patty in a lettuce leaf, top it with mayo, then a hot dog, then bacon, then any other fixings you like. I used onion and avocado on my burger. Then top it with one more piece of lettuce.Enjoy summer!
