Ahi Tuna Poke

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces ahi tuna (boneless)
  • 1/2 medium avocado (diced fine)
  • 1 tablespoon black sesame seeds
  • 1/2 head Boston or butter lettuce (leaves separated)
  • 3 tablespoons fresh chopped cilantro
  • lime wedges (to serve)
  • Pinch salt
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce

Preparation

  • Dice the ahi tuna into small cubes and place them in a bowl.
  • Toss in the sesame oil, soy sauce, and a pinch of salt.
  • Add the avocado, cilantro, and sesame seeds, tossing everything until well combined.
  • Spoon into lettuce cups and serve with lime wedges.
