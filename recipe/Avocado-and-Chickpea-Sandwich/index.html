Avocado and Chickpea Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 cup boiled chickpeas
  • ½ teaspoon chili
  • ½ teaspoon cumin
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • Sandwich bread
  • 1 tablespoon choppd green oion for garnish

Preparation

  • Mash the avocado, sprinkle salt, add in lime juice and mix.In a seperate bowl, mash the boiled chickpeas roughly.
  • Remove their skin beforehand if you like. Spice with cumin, chili and salt.
  • Drizzle olive oil and mix.
  • Spread the avocado mixture on a bread slice. Top with the chickpea mixture, garnish with green onion and serve.
