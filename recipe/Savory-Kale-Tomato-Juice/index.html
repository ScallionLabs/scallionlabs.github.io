Ingredients

  • 2 medium stalks celery
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds, optional
  • 1 cup loosely packed flat-leaf parsley leaves
  • 3 medium kale leaves with stems
  • 1/2 large juicy lemon, peel and pith removed
  • 3 medium plum tomatoes

Preparation

  • Juice, in this order, the tomatoes and parsley (together), celery, kale and lemon, following your juicer's specific settings for each. Stir in the chia seeds if using and let soak for 5 minutes.
  • Serve the juice immediately over ice, if desired.
