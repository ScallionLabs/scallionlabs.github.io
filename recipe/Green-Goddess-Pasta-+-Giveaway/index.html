Green Goddess Pasta + Giveaway

Ingredients

  • 2 cups arugula
  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • ½ avocado
  • 6 fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 1 sprig each fresh parsley, cilantro, and tarragon, chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • ½ lemon, juiced
  • ½ lime, juiced
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • 1 cup frozen peas, thawed
  • ¼ cup reduced fat sour cream
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 shallot, minced
  • ½ package Dreamfields® high fiber spaghetti

Preparation

  • Bring a large pot of water to a rolling boil.
  • Add the spaghetti and cook 5 minutes.
  • Add the asparagus and cook an additional 4 minutes, or until the pasta is al dente.
Drain.While the pasta cooks, prepare the sauce
  • Add the avocado, 2 tablespoons of olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, lime, juice, and fresh herbs to a food processor or blender. Process on high for 20-30 seconds, or until smooth.
  • Scrape into a small bowl and stir in the sour cream. Set aside.
  • Heat the remaining tablespoon of olive oil in a large pan set over medium heat.
  • Add the shallot and cook 2-3 minutes, or until soft.
  • Add the peas and arugula cook another 2-3 minutes, or until the arugula is wilted. Turn off the heat and stir in the cooked spaghetti, asparagus, and sauce. Stir to combine; season with salt and pepper.
