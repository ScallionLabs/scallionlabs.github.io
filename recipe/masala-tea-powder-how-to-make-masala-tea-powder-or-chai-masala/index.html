Ingredients

  • ½ cup dry holy basil/tulsi leaves
  • 1 tsp black pepper (optional)
  • 10 g small cardamom/choti elaichi
  • 7-8 long cinnamon pieces/dalchini
  • 3 tbsp fennel seeds/saunf
  • 4-5 pieces of dry ginger/saunth or ½ cup dry ginger powder
  • 7-8 pieces of dry ginger/saunth or ¾ cup dry ginger powder
  • 1 half nutmeg/jaiphal
  • 2-3 whole nutmeg/jaiphal
  • 5 g cloves/laung

Preparation

  • first grind the dry ginger.now grind the nutmeg and cardamom.store the simple and basic chai masala in an air tight container.firstly grind the dry ginger.then grind the nutmeg.finally grind all the rest of the herbs and spices with the already ground ginger and nutmeg.store in an air-tight container.use the chai masala whenever you make an indian chai.around ¼ tsp of chai masala is perfect for 2 cups of tea, but you can make it to ½ tsp for a more spicy indian chai.
