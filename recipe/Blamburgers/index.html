Blamburgers

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 1/2 cups chopped fresh parsley leaves
  • 1 large garlic clove, minced
  • l/2 pound ground lamb
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup sesame seeds
  • 2 tablespoons high-grade steak sauce
  • 1 tablespoon plus 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup wheat germ

Preparation

  • In a skillet, lightly saute the minced garlic clove in 1 tablespoon of oil.
  • Add parsley, wheat germ, and sesame seeds and saute lightly again.
  • Remove from heat and let cool.
  • Put ground beef and ground lamb in a mixing bowl.
  • Add the cooled sauteed ingredients, salt, steak sauce, 1/2 cup vegetable oil, and eggs. Form into 3-inch patties.
  • Place patties on grill and cook about 5 minutes on each side, or until cooked to desired doneness. You will need to lengthen cooking time if at a higher altitude.
