Blueberry-Spinach Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp. agave nectar (optional)
  • 4 cups baby spinach leaves
  • 1 ¼ cups frozen blueberries
  • 2 Tbs. Manitoba Harvest Hemp Hearts
  • ½ cup plain soymilk
  • ½ cup firm tofu cubes

Preparation

  • Purée spinach leaves with soymilk in blender.
  • Add blueberries, tofu, Manitoba Harvest Hemp Hearts, agave nectar (if using), and allspice (if using); blend until smooth.
