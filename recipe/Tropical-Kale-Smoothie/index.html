Tropical Kale Smoothie

Macros

Ingredients

  • 1 cup crushed ice
  • 2 cups baby kale
  • 1 kiwi fruit peeled and sliced
  • about 1 cup unsweetened milk alternative
  • 1 pear peeled and diced
  • 1 cup diced pineapple

Preparation

  • Add all ingredients to a blender and puree until smooth.
  • Add more milk alternative to blender for a smoother smoothie if desired.
