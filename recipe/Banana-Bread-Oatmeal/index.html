Banana Bread Oatmeal

Ingredients

  • Unsweetened almond breeze (use up to 1 cup)
  • 1/2 banana sliced
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Stevia, agave, honey - or your natural sweetener. I use about 1-2 tsp max.
  • 1/2 cup Whole rolled oats (although I like to use the multi grain oats from Trader Joe's because it has oats, barley, rye and wheat - and has a great nutty flavor!)
  • Organic soy milk (use up to 1/3 cup)
  • 4-5 walnuts chopped

Preparation

  • Cook the oats according to package directions (I cooked mine in the microwave with 1 & 1/4 cup water). The thing I love about the multi-grain oats is that they cook just like regular oatmeal but have a nice variety of grains.Top with bananas, walnuts, cinnamon and stevia and stir to combine
  • Pour the almond milk & soy milk over the oats until they have reached the consistency you want (for thicker oats, use less milk)Stir to combine.
