Preparation

  • Whisk together sauce ingredients until smooth (add more water to get a thinner sauce consistency)
  • Heat a non stick pan or wok at medium heat.
  • Add kale and broccoli slaw. Cook and stir for 2 to 3 minutes, or untilvegetables are crisp-tender, the kale has wilted, and they are coated in the sauce.
  • Serve over hot cooked brown rice (or quinoa) and sprinkle with the sunflower seeds.Enjoy!
