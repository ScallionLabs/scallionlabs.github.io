Parm & Red Pepper Mini Frittatas

Ingredients

  • 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup low-fat milk
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/4 cup diced onions
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped red bell pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher or sea salt

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.Spray ramekins with cooking spray.
  • Place olive oil in a saut pan over medium heat.
  • Place onions, bell peppers, and zucchini in the pan and cook for 5 to 8 minutes, until softened and onions are slightly translucent.
  • Whisk eggs with milk, salt, pepper, and parmesan.
  • Place egg mixture into 4 individual ramekins placed directly on the rack on the stove or spaced out on a sheet tray. Cook for 16-18 minutes, until set in the centers. Frittatas can be served warm or cold and will keep well in the fridge for up to one day. Enjoy!
