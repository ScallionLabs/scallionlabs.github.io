Spicy-Tart Kale Limeade

  • Servings: 2
  • Ready in: 15min
  • Tags:
    Vegetarian Side Dish Gluten Free Dairy Free Paleolithic Lacto Ovo Vegetarian Primal Vegan
  • High in: K A

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup (4 ounces) fresh apple juice (from 2 large green apples or 4 small)
  • 1/2 cup chilled coconut water
  • 1/2 cup (4 ounces) fresh ginger juice (from about 5 ounces peeled ginger)
  • 1/2 cup (4 ounces) fresh kale juice (from about 3/4 pounds kale)
  • Garnish: lime slices

Preparation

  • Mix kale juice, ginger juice, and apple juice with coconut water.
  • Serve in tall glasses.
  • Garnish with lime slice if desired.
