Ingredients

  • 1 medium apple
  • 1/2 head cabbage
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • 1 medium jicama
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 large orange (zested and juiced)
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons unrefined sea salt
  • 2 handfuls fresh spinach
  • 1 package vegetable starter culture (or 1/4 cup fresh whey)
