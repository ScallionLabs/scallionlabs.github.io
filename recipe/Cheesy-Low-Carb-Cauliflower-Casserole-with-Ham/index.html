Cheesy Low Carb Cauliflower Casserole with Ham

  • https://lowcarbyum.com/cheesy-cauliflower-ham-casserole/
  • Servings: 6
  • Ready in: 45min
  • Preparation time: 10min
  • Cooking time: 45min
  • Tags:
    Ketogenic Lunch Main Course Main Dish Dinner Gluten Free Fall Winter

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 16 oz bag frozen cauliflower or mix with broccoli
  • 1 Tablespoon dried minced onion
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 pound ham
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup crushed pork rinds optional
  • 2 cups taco or cheddar cheese shredded

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 325F.Microwave cauliflower 5 minutes.
  • Mix ham and cauliflower in 9x13 casserole dish.Beat egg with milk, cream, dried onion and garlic powder.Stir egg mixture into ham and cauliflower.Reserve 1/2 cheese.
  • Mix remaining 1 1/2 cups cheese with other ingredients.Top mixture with reserved cheese and crushed pork rinds, if desired.
  • Bake for 40 minutes.
