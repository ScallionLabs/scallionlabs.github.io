Lebanese Hushwee – Ground Beef with Cinnamon and Toasted Pine Nuts

  • http://thelemonbowl.com/2014/03/lebanese-hushwee-meat-with-cinnamon-and-toasted-pine-nuts.html
  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 20min
  • Preparation time: 5min
  • Cooking time: 15min
  • Tags:
    Ketogenic Lunch Main Course Main Dish Dinner Gluten Free Middle Eastern

Macros

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon clarified butter (ghee)
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cloves
  • 20 ounces ground sirloin (lamb or bison work well too)
  • ½ teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 medium onion - minced (my Aunt Paula uses frozen diced onions to save time)
  • fresh minced parsley - garnish
  • ½ teaspoon pepper
  • ¼ cup toasted pine nuts (See Notes)
  • 1½ teaspoons salt

Preparation

  • In a deep pan, melt butter over medium high heat then add the minced onion.
  • Sautee until translucent, 4-5 minutes.
  • Add ground sirloin and all spices (salt through cloves). Cook 8-10 minutes or until meat is browned, stirring occasionally with a wooden spoon to break up meat.Stir in toasted pine nuts before serving and garnish with fresh minced parsley.
