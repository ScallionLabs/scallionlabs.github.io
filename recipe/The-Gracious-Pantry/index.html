Ingredients

  • 2 medium avocados
  • 10 hard boiled eggs, chilled
  • 1/2 tsp. dried parsley or 1 tbsp. finely chopped, fresh parsley
  • 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
  • Large lettuce leaves
  • 1/4 cup clean eating mayo (store bought or homemade)
  • 1 tbsp. minced red onion
  • Salt to taste
  • Shredded, cooked chicken
  • Cooked shrimp

Preparation

  • Peel and slice or chop all the eggs.
  • Remove the avocado from its skin and cut into small pieces.In a large mixing bowl, combine all the ingredients except the avocado, mixing well to distribute the mayo.When everything is mixed, gently fold in the avocado pieces until well combined.
  • Add salt to taste.Spoon onto washed and dried, large lettuce leaves, fold like a taco and enjoy!
