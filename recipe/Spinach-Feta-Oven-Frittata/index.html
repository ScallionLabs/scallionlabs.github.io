Spinach Feta Oven Frittata

  • Ready in: 45min

Ingredients

  • 1 pound baby spinach
  • 8 eggs
  • 4 ounces feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Dried oregano
  • Sea salt and pepper

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 35
  • Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium high heat.
  • Saute spinach until wilted, and then transfer to a 10 inch oven-safe skillet (I use my cast iron skillet).Beat eggs until frothy and pour over spinach.Crumble feta and sprinkle over eggs. Season with a pinch of salt and a grinding of pepper.
  • Bake for 30 minutes or until eggs are set.
  • Sprinkle liberally with ground oregano. Cool slightly, cut into wedges and serve.
