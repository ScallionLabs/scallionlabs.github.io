Monster Wedge Salad

Ingredients

  • extra blue cheese crumbles
  • 1/4 cup blue cheese crumbles
  • chives, for garnish
  • 1/3 cup cooked and crumbled bacon
  • 4 hard boiled eggs, chopped into pieces
  • 10 grape tomatoes, sliced in half
  • iceberg lettuce, cut into 4 {but we're only using 2)
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 1/4 cup plain greek yogurt
  • salt & pepper, to taste
  • 3 tablespoons sour cream
  • 2 teaspoons white balsamic vinegar
  • dash Worcestershire

Preparation

  • In a small bowl, whisk all ingredients for the dressing together. Set aside.
  • On two dinner plates, place wedges of iceberg lettuce, cut side up.
  • Drizzle generously with dressing. Top with bacon, eggs, tomatoes, extra blue cheese and chives.
  • Serve.
