Cashew-Crusted Salmon with Bok Choy

Ingredients

  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 bunch bok choy, rinsed well and chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/3 cup honey mustard
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • 2 tablespoons onion flakes
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped dry-roasted cashews
  • 6 salmon fillets

Preparation

  • Heat 1 tablespoon of the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  • Brush honey mustard all over salmon. Season both sides of salmon with onion flakes and black pepper.
  • Place cashews in a shallow dish, add four of the salmon fillets and turn to coat.
  • Place all salmon fillets in hot skillet and cook 3 minutes per side, until fork-tender.
  • Heat remaining olive oil in separate large skillet over medium heat.
  • Add garlic and cook 1 minute.
  • Add bok choy and cook 2 minutes, until greens wilt but stalks are still crisp-tender. Season, to taste, with salt and pepper.
  • Serve the cashew-crusted salmon with this meal and reserve 2 uncrusted fillets for another meal.
