Preparation

  • Put the crabmeat in a glass or metal bowl. Check for any shards of shell. Tip the brown crabmeat into a small bowl. Stir in the crme frache and the lemon juice until smooth. Lightly season, then set aside.
  • Cut the tomatoes in half, or into quarters if large.
  • Cut the avocado in half lengthways and remove the stone. Peel off the skin, then cut into thin slices.
  • Put the rocket, tomatoes and avocado into a large bowl. Squeeze over the remaining lemon juice and the olive oil. Season, then toss until the salad is coated with the dressing.
  • Arrange the salad onto serving plates. Scatter over the white crabmeat, drizzle with crme frache dressing and serve straight away.
