Easy Crockpot Beef Fajitas

  • http://eatathomecooks.com/2013/02/easy-crockpot-beef-fajitas.html
  • Servings: 8
  • Ready in: 45min
  • Tags:
    Ketogenic Lunch Main Course Main Dish Dinner Gluten Free Dairy Free Mexican

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 - 2 lbs. thin sliced beef, cut in strips
  • 3 bell peppers in variety of colors
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 1 packet fajita seasoning

Preparation

  • Place onions and peppers in crockpot.
  • Place sliced beef on top of peppers and onions.
  • Sprinkle fajita seasoning over beef.Cook on high for 5-6 hours or low for 7-8 hours.
  • Serve in tortillas with any fixings you like.
