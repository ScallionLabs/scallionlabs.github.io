Wilted Kale Shrimp Zucchini Noodle Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • ¼ cup fresh chopped basil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bunch organic kale, stem removed, chopped (about 6 cups)
  • 2 lemons juiced or ½ cup lemon juice
  • ½ teaspoon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 8 ounces shrimp, deveined, tails removed
  • 2 cups zucchini noodles
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • ¼ cup fresh chopped basil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bunch organic kale, stem removed, chopped (about 6 cups)
  • 2 lemons juiced or ½ cup lemon juice
  • 8 ounces shrimp, deveined, tails removed
  • 2 cups zucchini noodles

Preparation

  • Heat oil in a large skillet over medium low heat.
  • Add garlic and cook until fragrant.
  • Add shrimp and cook until pink on both sides and curled around edges.Reduce heat to low and add kale, tomatoes, salt and pepper.Continue to cook until kale is wilted.Stir in zucchini noodles and lemon juice and cook 2-3 more minutes to soften zucchini.
  • Serve topped with fresh basil.
