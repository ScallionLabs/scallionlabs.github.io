Tender Spareribs

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup molasses
  • 1/4 cup prepared mustard
  • 1 to 2 teaspoons hot pepper sauce
  • 4 pounds pork spareribs, cut into serving-size pieces
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce

Preparation

  • Place ribs in a 5-qt. slow cooker.
  • Combine the remaining ingredients; pour over ribs. Cover and cook on low for 5-6 hours or until meat is tender.
