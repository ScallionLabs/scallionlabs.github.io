Preparation

  • While the water comes to a boil, heat the olive oil in a large heavy-bottomed skillet.
  • Add onions and saute for 5 minutes, or until soft but not coloured. Stir in La Bomba sauce and hot pepper flakes, and cook for 2-3 minutes longer or until fragrant.Stir in diced tomatoes and strained tomatoes. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat to low and cook for 10-15 minutes.Bring a large pot of salted water to a rolling boil over high heat.
  • Add pasta and cook for 1 minute less than indicated on the package, or until soft but still quite al dente.
  • Drain.Once the sauce has simmered for 15 minutes, stir in the tuna and chopped olives.
  • Add the pasta, and stir until evenly coated with sauce. Cook for a minute or two to heat the tuna through, then remove from heat.
  • Sprinkle with chopped parsley, and serve immediately.
