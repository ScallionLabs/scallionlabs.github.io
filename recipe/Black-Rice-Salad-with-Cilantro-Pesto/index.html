Black Rice Salad with Cilantro Pesto

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Brazil nuts
  • 1½ cups cilantro leaves with tender stems, divided
  • 3 cups cooked black rice (from about 1½ uncooked)
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • 1 small kabocha squash, peeled, seeded, cut into ½-inch pieces
  • Kosher salt, freshly ground pepper
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
  • 2 teaspoons freshly grated lime zest
  • 1½ cups olive oil
  • 6 radishes, very thinly sliced
  • 3 scallions, thinly sliced
  • 1 shallot, very thinly sliced
  • 3 tablespoons unseasoned rice vinegar

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 35
  • Toast Brazil nuts on a rimmed baking sheet, tossing once, until golden brown, 1012 minutes.
  • Let cool.Toss squash and oil in an 8x8" baking dish; roast until squash is fork-tender, 3040 minutes.
  • Let cool, then strain oil into a small bowl; set squash aside.
  • Whisk vinegar, lime juice, and 3 Tbsp. squash oil in another small bowl. Season vinaigrette with salt and pepper.Pure Brazil nuts, garlic, lime zest, 1 cup cilantro, and remaining squash oil in a food processor until smooth. Season pesto with salt and pepper.Toss radishes, scallions, shallot, black rice, vinaigrette, reserved squash, and remaining cup cilantro in a large bowl; season with salt and pepper.
  • Drizzle some pesto over salad. (Save any extra pesto and stir into yogurt or labneh or serve with cheese and crackers.)Do Ahead: Kabocha squash can be cooked 1 day ahead. Keep in oil; cover and chill.
