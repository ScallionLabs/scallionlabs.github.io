Ingredients

  • 1 large beet, peeled and sliced
  • 1 large head cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 4 cloves garlic, peeled and cut in half
  • ¼ teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 3 cups water
  • ½ cup white vinegar

Preparation

  • First, pack the chopped vegetables tightly in a mason jar or two.Bring the water to a boil, then add vinegar, salt, pepper and other spices and mix well until salt dissolves.
  • Pour over the vegetables.
  • Let cool completely. Close jar(s) tightly and store in a cool dark place for a day (for crisper pickles) or two.Eat up!
