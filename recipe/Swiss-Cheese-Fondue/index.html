Swiss Cheese Fondue

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces etivaz cheese, grated
  • 4 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 8 ounces Emmentaler cheese, grated
  • 4 ounces vacherin fribourgeois or fontina cheese, grated
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 4 ounces gruyere cheese, grated
  • 1 pinch nutmeg, freshly grated
  • 3 cups vegetable stock
  • 1/2 cup water

Preparation

  • Combine cornstarch and water in a small bowl with a whisk and set aside.Rub a stainless steel pot with the garlic, then drop the clove in.
  • Add the white wine and vegetable stock and bring to a simmer.
  • Add the cheeses at once to the simmering wine.While stirring, melt the cheese and bring to a light simmer.Little by little, add a bit of the cornstarch slurry to the cheese until the desiredÂ consistency is achieved.
  • Add the nutmeg, simmer the fondue for 8 minutes and serve over a low flame to keep it warm and melted.
