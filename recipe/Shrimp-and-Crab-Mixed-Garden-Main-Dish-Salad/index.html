  • 6 ounces of crabmeat

  • 1 green onion, sliced
  • Kosher salt and freshly cracked black pepper, to taste
  • Old Bay Seasoning, to taste
  • 4 radishes, sliced
  • 1/2 pound of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tomato, chopped
