Ingredients

  • 1 avocado
  • Black pepper
  • 400g tin chickpeas, drained (140g when drained)
  • 3tbsp fresh coriander, chopped
  • Juice of half a lime
  • To serve: pitta bread, sliced tomato
  • Salt
  • 2 spring onions, sliced

Preparation

  • Coarsely mash the drained chickpeas and the avocado together with a fork or potato masher, and add the lime juice, coriander and sliced spring onions.
  • Mix well and season to taste. I served mine in a pitta bread with some sliced tomato.
