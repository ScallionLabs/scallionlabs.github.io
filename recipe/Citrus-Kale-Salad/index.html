Citrus Kale Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 Tbsp. raw honey or agave nectar
  • 1/4 c. dried unsweetened cranberries [optional]
  • 1-inch chunk of ginger [peel removed and chopped into smaller bits]
  • juice of 2 lemons
  • juice of 1 orange
  • 8 kale leaves
  • 1 orange
  • 2 Tbsp. sunflower seeds [optional]

Preparation

  • Thoroughly wash the kale leaves and pull the leafy part away from the stems. Tear or roughly chop the kale into smaller pieces [think bite-sized]. Set aside while you make the dressing.
  • Add the juice from 1 orange, a whole orange [peel removed and chopped], 1 1/2 tablespoons of honey, and a 1” chunk of ginger to a blender. Blend until it is smooth and frothy.
  • Pour the dressing over the salad and start mixing.
  • Cover the bowl and allow the salad to marinate for about 30 minutes in the refrigerator.
  • Mix with salad tongs before serving.
  • Serve in a bowl or on a plate, sprinkle with sunflower seeds and cranberries and enjoy.
