Hot Crab, Spinach and Artichoke Dip

Ingredients

  • 1 cup coarsely chopped artichoke hearts
  • 3 1/2 cups loosely packed baby spinach leaves, coarsely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 6 ounces fresh dungeness crab meat, picked over for shells (you can substitute a 6oz can of crab meat)
  • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened and cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese, divided

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In a small skillet melt butter over medium heat. Once butter melts add spinach and cook just until spinach wilts, about 2 minutes.
  • Transfer to a mixing bowl. To the spinach add artichoke hearts, cream cheese, 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese and crab meat. Stir to combine. Butter a small casserole dish, spoon mixture into the dish.
  • Bake for 15 minutes.
  • Remove from the oven, stir and sprinkle with remaining Parmesan cheese. Turn on the broiler and cook for 3-5 minutes or until top is browned and edges are bubbly. Allow to cool slightly and serve with tortilla chips.
