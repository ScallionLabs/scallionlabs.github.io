Red Lentil Coconut Soup

Ingredients

  • 1-15oz can of diced tomatoes, low sodium if possible
  • 2 carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 2 celery, chopped
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoon coriander
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Lemon
  • 2 cups lentils, rinsed until water runs clean
  • 1/2 cup light coconut milk
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Parsley or cilantro
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Dash salt and pepper
  • 1-6oz can tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 8 cups vegetable broth
  • Garnishes:[/b]
  • [b]

Preparation

  • Saute onion, garlic, carrots and celery with olive oil over medium heat until soft.
  • Add spices and continue to saute for 3-4 minutes.
  • Add lentils, vegetable broth, diced tomatoes, tomato paste and coconut milk and simmer for 25-30 minutes, until lentils are tender.Puree half with immersion blender and return to the pot.
  • Garnish with parsley, red pepper flakes, lemon juice and serve warm.
