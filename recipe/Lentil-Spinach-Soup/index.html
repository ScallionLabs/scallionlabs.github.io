  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice (more to taste)

Preparation

  • Wash and drain the lentils.
  • Put them in the pressure cooker with the water. Cook per the instructions of your pressure cooker. Mine requires me to cook on medium-high till the first whistle. Then lower the heat and cook for 6 minutes. If you dont have a pressure cooker, you will probably have to cook the lentils for 1 hour till they become tender.
  • While the lentils are cooking, grate the fresh ginger and chop up the coriander.
  • Over medium heat, heat the olive oil in a large pot.
  • Add the coriander and spinach. Stir till the spinach is wilted.
  • Add the cooked lentils, lemon juice and salt to the spinach.
  • Serve super hot with fresh ground pepper!
