Corned Beef Skillet Hash

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups diced cooked beets (about 12 oz fresh beets)
  • 2 cups chopped cooked corned beef brisket
  • 1/3 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 1/2 cups chopped cooked potatoes (about 1 1/2 medium)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup shortening

Preparation

  • In large bowl, mix all ingredients except shortening and parsley.
  • In 10-inch skillet, heat shortening over medium heat until melted.
  • Spread beef mixture in skillet. Cook 10 to 15 minutes, turning occasionally with wide spatula, until brown.
  • Sprinkle with parsley.
