Warm White Bean and Kale Salad with Dried Cranberries and Cashews

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp Balsamic Vinegar
  • 1/2 oz Cashews chopped (14g)
  • 1/8 cup Dried Cranberries (20g)
  • 1 15oz Can of Northern Beans drained and rinsed
  • About 1 cup Chopped Kale or more! (67g)
  • 1/4 cup water

Preparation

  • Heat a non-stick skillet over medium-high heat.
  • Add the kale and a 1/4 cup of water.The water should boil shortly. Toss the kale. It will begin to deepen in color and wilt a little. Once it does add the cranberries. There’s should still be moisture in the pan.Toss and cook for a minute or two until most of the water evaporates.
  • Add the beans.Toss, add the vinegar and cook for 1-2 minutes allowing all the flavors to mix.
  • Add the cashews and cook for one more minute.
  • Remove and EAT!I’m going to be honest, I ate the whole batch for lunch. I couldn’t help myself! I ate half, uploaded the photos, and then ate the other half. I liked it that much! So simple and so tasty! I was planning on it being 2 servings and that’s how I’ll list it, as I think it makes a fabulous side dish.
