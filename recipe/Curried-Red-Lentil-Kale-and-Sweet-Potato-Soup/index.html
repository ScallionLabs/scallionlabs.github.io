Curried Red Lentil, Kale and Sweet Potato Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp minced cilantro
  • 2 tsp curry powder
  • 1 large garlic clove, minced
  • 2 tsp grated ginger
  • 1/2 jalapeno, seeded, diced
  • 2 bunches lacinato kale, stemmed and coarsely chopped
  • 14 oz light coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely diced
  • 1 cup red lentils
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 medium sweet potato, peeled and diced into 1/2-inch cubes
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • 5 cups water

Preparation

  • Heat the olive oil in a large pot.
  • Add the onion and cook over medium heat.
  • Add a pinch of salt and cook for about 5-8 minutes, or until onion becomes tender. Stir in the jalapeno, ginger, garlic, curry powder, and salt. Stir for about a minute, stirring frequently.
  • Add the tomato paste and cook for about 2-3 minutes, or until it starts to darken in color, stirring constantly.
  • Add the water, coconut milk, red lentils, and sweet potato. Bring to a boil, and then lower to a simmer. Cook, partially covered, until the sweet potatoes are tender and the lentils are cooked through, about 30 minutes.Stir in the kale and cook for about 5 minutes, or until wilted and tender. Stir in the cilantro and serve.
