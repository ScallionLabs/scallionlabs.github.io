Healthy Peanut Butter Mousse

Ingredients

  • 240g (2 cups) Peanut Flour
  • 1/4 tsp Salt (I used Pink Himalayan Salt)
  • 2 tsp Stevia Extract
  • 1 16oz container Firm Tofu, drained of liquid (preferably organic)

Preparation

Complete the following steps with the tofu (basically, just try to get as much excess liquid out as possible)
  • Add all the ingredients to a large blender or food processor and puree until smooth. Scrape down the sides of the bowl when necessary.Give it a taste and add more stevia or salt to taste. Be sure to puree the mixture as much as possible, you don't lose anything by overblending but you sacrifice texture and flavor by underblending!
  • Pour into serving bowls and serve immediately. If not serving right away, cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate
  • Top the mousse with chopped, salted peanuts and some mini dark chocolate chips if you're feeling extra indulgent!
