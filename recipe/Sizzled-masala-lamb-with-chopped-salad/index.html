Sizzled masala lamb with chopped salad

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp rapeseed oil
  • 1 tbsp curry paste
  • 2 lamb loin chops
  • seeds from ½ pomegranate
  • ¼ cucumber, peeled, deseeded and finely diced
  • 6 radishes, diced
  • 6 cherry tomatoes, quartered
  • ½ small red onion, very finely sliced
  • small handful mint, roughly chopped, plus 3 leaves to garnish
  • small handful coriander, roughly chopped, plus 3 leaves to garnish
  • ¼ tsp ground cumin
  • juice ½ lemon

Preparation

  • Heat grill. Rub the oil and curry paste all over the chops and season.
  • Put the chops on a baking tray and grill for about 4-5 mins each side for medium, depending on the thickness of the chop, or to your liking. Allow to rest for a couple of mins.
  • Meanwhile, put the remaining ingredients (except the garnish) in a bowl, give everything a stir, then season. Spoon the salad onto a plate and lay the chops on top. Scatter with the remaining herb leaves.
