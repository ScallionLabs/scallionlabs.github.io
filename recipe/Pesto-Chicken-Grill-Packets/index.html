Pesto Chicken Grill Packets

  • Servings: 6
  • Ready in: 40min
  • Preparation time: 15min
  • Cooking time: 25min
  • Tags:
    Ketogenic Lunch Main Course Main Dish Dinner Gluten Free Dairy Free Paleolithic Primal

Ingredients

  • Extra-virgin olive oil, for drizzling
  • 1-1/2 cups pesto
  • 6 plum tomatoes, chopped
  • Salt and pepper
  • 12 scallions, trimmed
  • 6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves (about 2-1/4 pounds)
  • 4 zucchini, thinly sliced

Preparation

  • Prepare your veggies.Salt your chicken on all sides. If they are large pieces, you can cut in half.
  • Lay out six 12-inch-long sheets of heavy-duty foil.
  • Drizzle 1-2 teaspoons olive oil into the center of each sheet.
  • Lay a piece of chicken in the center of each sheet.
  • Spread 1 tablespoon pesto on top of each chicken piece.
  • Place zucchini, tomatoes and scallions on each chicken breast.
  • Spread 3 tablespoons pesto on top of that.Seal up the foil packets.Cover and grill over medium heat for 25 minutes.
  • Remove from the grill and serve with rice.
