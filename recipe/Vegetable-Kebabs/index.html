Vegetable Kebabs

  • http://cooksjoy.com/blog/2012/07/vegetable-kebabs.html
  • Servings: 5
  • Ready in: 75min
  • Preparation time: 15min
  • Cooking time: 60min
  • Tags:
    Vegetarian Side Dish Gluten Free Dairy Free Lacto Ovo Vegetarian Vegan
  • High in: A

Ingredients

  • 2 carrots
  • chilly powder
  • 1 cup green peas
  • 1/2 cup onion
  • 3 potatoes
  • 2 tsp chaat masala
  • salt
  • 1 cup spinach

Preparation

  • Cook potatoes and peas and mash coarsely. Soak skewers in water for 30 minutes.
  • Add carrots, onion, spinach, salt, chilly powder and chaat masala to the potato mix.
  • Combine well.Form small balls of the mix. Take each ball and press against the skewer forming a log. Repeat for other balls.
  • Place the kebabs in a greased sheet and spray them with oil.
  • Bake in a preheated 425F oven for 25 - 30 minutes turning once in the middle. The kebabs are done when the tops are browned.
