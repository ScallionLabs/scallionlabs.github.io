Quinoa Salad with Avocado and Tomatoes

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado cut in cubes
  • 1 cup baby spinach
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes halved
  • 2 T lemon juice
  • 2 T diced onion
  • 1/4 cup parsley
  • 1/2 cup quinoa
  • sea salt to taste
  • 1 cup water

Preparation

  • In a small saucepan, combine the quinoa and water. Bring to a boil, then cover and turn down to simmer until all the water has been absorbed. It usually takes about 10 minutes.
  • Let the quinoa cool before making this salad.
  • Add all the ingredients to a bowl and toss well.
