Roasted Butternut Squash, Shiitake Mushroom and Kale, Kimchi Bibimsoba

  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 40min
  • Preparation time: 10min
  • Cooking time: 30min

  • High in: K A C

Ingredients

  • 4 cups butternut squash, peeled, seeded and cut into 1/2 inch cubes
  • green onions to taste
  • gochujang sauce to taste
  • 4 cups kale, sliced
  • 1 cup kimchi, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon oil
  • toasted seaweed powder to taste
  • toasted sesame seeds to taste
  • 4 ounces shiitake, sliced
  • 8 ounces soba noodles

Preparation

  • Toss with the butternut squash with the oil and gochugaru, place in a single layer on a baking pan and bake in a preheated 400F oven until tender, about 20-30 minutes.Meanwhile, heat the oil in a pan over medium heat, add the kale and saute until tender, about 3-5 minutes, and set aside.
  • Heat the oil in a pan over medium heat, add the shiitake mushrooms, saute until slightly caramelized, about 7-10 minutes, and set aside.Cook the soba noodles as directed.Assemble bibimsoba, garnish with toasted sesame seeds, toasted seaweed powder, green onions to taste and gochujang sauce.
