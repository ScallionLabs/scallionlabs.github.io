Cleansing Detox Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 small head of broccoli, florets
  • 3 medium carrots, diced
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper, or to taste (optional)
  • 3 celery stalks, diced
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, peeled and minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • juice from 1/2 of a small lemon (or a whole lemon, depending how much lemon flavor you prefer)
  • 2 cups kale, de-stemmed and torn in pieces
  • 1 cup purple cabbage, chopped
  • 1/2 of a red onion, diced
  • fine-grain sea salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric (I used powdered)
  • 1/4 cup water (or vegetable broth)
  • 6 cups water (or 4 cups vegetable broth + 2 cups water)

Preparation

  • In a large pot, add the water and turn on the heat to medium-high. After it's hot, add the onion and garlic. Saut for 2 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Add the celery, carrots, broccoli, tomatoes and fresh ginger. Stir and cook for 3 minutes, adding in extra water or broth as needed (another 1/4 cup). Stir in the turmeric, cinnamon, and cayenne pepper plus salt and pepper to taste.
  • Add in the water or vegetable broth and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 10-15 minutes or until vegetables are soft.
  • Add in the kale, cabbage and lemon juice near the last 2-3 minutes of simmering.Enjoy!
