Lemon roasted asparagus

  • http://www.runningtothekitchen.com/2013/04/lemon-roasted-asparagus/
  • Servings: 2
  • Ready in: 12min
  • Preparation time: 2min
  • Cooking time: 10min
  • Tags:

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch thin asparagus, ends trimmed
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • dash of red pepper flakes
  • salt & pepper to taste

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 400 degrees.Arrange lemon slices on a baking sheet.
  • Lay asparagus on top of lemons and drizzle with olive oil.Season with salt & pepper and red pepper flakes.Roast for about 7 minutes then turn to hi-broil and broil for another 3-5 until asparagus tips are crunchy and lemons have started to brown.
