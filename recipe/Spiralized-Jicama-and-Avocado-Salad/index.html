Spiralized Jicama & Avocado Salad

  • Servings: 2
  • Ready in: 15min
  • Tags:

  • High in: C Fiber

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado (diced)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes (quartered)
  • 1 jalapeño (minced)
  • 1 large jicama
  • ½ cup honey lime dressing (click link for recipe)
  • ¼ red onion (diced)

Preparation

  • Begin by peeling the jicama.Once it’s peeled, place the jicama on the spiralizer. Use Blade C to spiralize.Once spiralized, add the jicama to a large bowl with the tomatoes, onions and jalapeños.
  • Pour the dressing over the top and toss to coat.
  • Place in two bowls and top with the diced avocado.
