Kari Kepala Ikan Bersama Belimbing Buluh (Fish Head Curry)

  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 45min

  • High in: Manganese B3

Macros

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp black mustard seeds
  • 1 tbsp plain chili powder
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk (optional)
  • 1/2 tbsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 2 sprigs Curry Leaves-
  • 1 tsp fennel powder
  • 1/2 tbsp fenugreek/halba seeds
  • 1 tablespoon Garlic paste-
  • 1 tablespoon Ginger paste-
  • 1 tenggiri/mackerel fish head - clean and chop into large pieces
  • 3 tablespoons oil (I use sun flower oil)
  • 1 tbsp onion paste
  • 1 tsp white pepper powder
  • 1 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 tsp turmeric/kunyit powder
  • 10 belimbing buluh

Preparation

  • Rub fish with turmeric powder and keep aside.
  • Heat oil and saute ginger, garlic and onion paste.Splatter in mustard and fenugreek seeds.
  • Add curry paste and fry till oil splits
  • Pour 1/2 liter of water; stir and gently drop in fish pieces.Simmer till fish is cooked.Season with salt; add belimbing and curry leaves.Gently stir.Lastly pour coconut milk.Stir and remove from heat.
