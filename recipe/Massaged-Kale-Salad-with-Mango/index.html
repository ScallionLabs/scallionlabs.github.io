Massaged Kale Salad with Mango

Ingredients

  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Juice from ½ lemon
  • 3 cups packed kale, stalks removed and discarded, leaves thinly sliced
  • Kosher salt
  • 1 champagne mango, diced small
  • 2 Tbsp. blood orange infused olive oil* (or replace with extra virgin olive oil)

Preparation

  • Combine all ingredients except for mango in a large bowl.Using your hands massage the kale until soft, about 1 minute.
  • Add chopped mango, toss all together, and serve.
