Kielbasa, Pepper, Onion and Potato Hash

Ingredients

  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • Kielbasa, Pepper, Onion and Potato Hash
  • olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1/2 yellow, red or orange bell pepper, diced
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 (14 ounce) package turkey kielbasa, cut into 1/4 inch rounds

Preparation

  • In a heavy bottomed skillet (I like to use my cast iron), heat 2 tbsp of olive oil over medium-high heat.
  • Add the potatoes to the skillet and season with salt and pepper. Fry until golden brown and cooked through, around 8-10 minutes, stirring a few times to ensure even browning. In a separate skillet, brown the sliced kielbasa for around 5 minutes in 1 tbsp of olive oil over medium high heat.
  • Remove the kielbasa from the pan and set aside.
  • Add the peppers and onions to the skillet and season with a pinch of salt and pepper. Cook for 5 minutes, or until softened, stirring occasionally.
  • Add the cooked potatoes and kielbasa to the skillet with the onions and peppers and mix everything together.
  • Serve nice and hot!
