Quinoa-Stuffed Peppers

Ingredients

  • 1 15-oz. can black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 2 15-oz. cans diced tomatoes, drained, liquid reserved
  • 3 large carrots, grated (1½ cups)
  • 2 ribs celery, finely chopped (½ cup)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced (2 tsp.)
  • 1 Tbs. ground cumin
  • 2 Tbs. olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped (1 cup)
  • 1½ cups grated reduced-fat pepper Jack cheese, divided
  • ¾ cup quinoa
  • 4 large red bell peppers, halved lengthwise, ribs removed
  • 1 10-oz. pkg. frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry

  • 1 10-oz. pkg. frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry

Preparation

  • Heat oil in saucepan over medium heat.
  • Add onion and celery, and cook 5 minutes, or until soft.
  • Add cumin and garlic, and sauté 1 minute. Stir in spinach and drained tomatoes. Cook 5 minutes, or until most of liquid has evaporated.
  • Stir in black beans, quinoa, carrots, and 2 cups water. Cover, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer 20 minutes, or until quinoa is tender. Stir in 1 cup cheese. Season with salt and pepper, if desired.
  • Preheat oven to 350°F.
  • Pour liquid from tomatoes in bottom of baking dish.
  • Fill each bell pepper half with heaping ¾-cup quinoa mixture, and place in baking dish. Cover with foil, and bake 1 hour. Uncover, and sprinkle each pepper with 1 Tbs. remaining cheese.
  • Bake 15 minutes more, or until tops of stuffed peppers are browned.
  • Let stand 5 minutes.
  • Transfer stuffed peppers to serving plates, and drizzle each with pan juices before serving.
