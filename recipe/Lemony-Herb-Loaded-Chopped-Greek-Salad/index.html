Lemony Herb-Loaded Chopped Greek Salad

  • http://pinchofyum.com/chopped-greek-salad
  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 45min
  • Tags:
    Vegetarian Salad Gluten Free Dairy Free Lacto Ovo Vegetarian Vegan Mediterranean European Greek
  • High in: Manganese

Vitamins RDA %

Minerals RDA %

Macros

Ingredients

  • 1 14-ounce can white beans (lentil and garbanzos are yummy too)
  • 2 cups chopped cucumbers (one should be enough)
  • ½ cup chopped kalamata olives
  • lemon juice
  • 3 cups chopped fresh herbs - I like mint, parsley, and basil
  • olive oil
  • 1 cup uncooked quinoa
  • 2 cups chopped red onion (one should be enough)
  • salt and pepper
  • 3 cups chopped tomatoes

Preparation

  • Cook the quinoa according to package directions. While the quinoa cooks, chop all your veggies.Toss everything together!Dress to taste with olive oil, salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
