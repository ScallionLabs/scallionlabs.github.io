Quinoa Avocado Spinach Power Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 medium avocados
  • 3 oz. baby spinach
  • 8 oz. cherry tomatoes
  • 1-2 cloves garlic, minced (I use 2)
  • 3 green onions
  • 2 Tbsp. olive oil
  • 1 cup dry quinoa
  • 2 Tbsp. red wine vinegar
  • 1/8 tsp. salt
  • Vegan, gluten-free

Preparation

  • POW! Nutritional punch! Quinoa, tender spinach, and sweet cherry tomatoes join forces with bites of creamy avocado to bring it all together. I find it to be a perfect marriage of filling and energizing. I'm fascinated with the fact that it's so simple, yet the flavors seem so complex as you eat it. My head starts spinning at how many variations there could be.
  • Add some beans for an extra protein punch, change up the veggies, or use fresh lemon juice in the dressing instead of red wine vinegar. Although I probably won't stray too far from this version because my husband raves about how much he loves it. It's our new favorite quinoa dish! Not to mention it makes a rock star take-along cold lunch. Hope you love it too!Vegan, gluten-free

The heat from the quinoa will lightly wilt the spinach.
  • Add the green onions, tomatoes, and dressing. Stir to combine.
  • Add the avocado and lightly toss.
