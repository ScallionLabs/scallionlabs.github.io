Super Easy White Bean Salad

  • http://mywholefoodlife.com/2012/10/08/super-easy-bean-salad-2/
  • Servings: 5
  • Ready in: 25min
  • Preparation time: 15min
  • Cooking time: 10min
  • Tags:
    Vegetarian Salad Gluten Free Dairy Free Lacto Ovo Vegetarian Vegan

Ingredients

  • 1-2 bell peppers chopped (any color is fine)
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 1/4 cup fresh flat leaf parsley
  • 4 green onions
  • Juice of 1 small lemon
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil
  • salt to taste
  • 2.5 cups white beans
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar

Preparation

  • If you use dried white beans, like we do, soak overnight first. Rinse, drain, and boil to soften.Rough chop the bell peppers.Slice the cherry tomatoes.Chop the green onions.Peel, de-seed, and chop the cucumber.Chop the parsley.
  • Add everything to a mixing bowl and stir.
