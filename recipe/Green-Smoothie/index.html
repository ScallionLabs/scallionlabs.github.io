Ingredients

  • 1 banana, chopped
  • 1/3 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 orange, chopped
  • 2 cups spinach leaves
  • 1 cup strawberries

Preparation

  • Combine the strawberries, orange, banana, spinach, Greek yogurt and 1 cup ice in blender until smooth.
  • Serve immediately.
