Strawberry Quinoa and Spinach Caprese Salad

  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 30min
  • Preparation time: 5min
  • Cooking time: 15min
  • Tags:
    Vegetarian Side Dish Gluten Free Lacto Ovo Vegetarian Mother's Day
  • High in: K

Ingredients

  • 1 (5 ounce) package baby spinach
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinaigrette
  • 1 handful basil leaves, julienned
  • 1 cup fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • 1 cup quinoa, well rinsed
  • 2 cups strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 1 1/2 cups water

Preparation

  • Bring the water and quinoa to a boil, reduce the heat and simmer, covered, until the quinoa is tender and has absorbed the water, about 15 minutes, remove from heat, let sit covered for 5 minutes and let cook to room temperature.Assemble the salad and enjoy!
