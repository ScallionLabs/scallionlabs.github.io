Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons corn starch
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 3 tablespoons ketchup
  • 3-4 pounds country style pork ribs
  • Hot rice
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • ¼ cup water

Preparation

  • In a small bowl combine the soy sauce, orange marmalade, ketchup and minced garlic.
  • Pour about half the mixture into the slow cooker, add the pork ribs, and dribble the remaining sauce over the ribs.Cook on low for 5-6 hours or until tender.
  • Remove the ribs from the slow cooker and move the sauce, to a medium sauce pan. Thicken with the corn starch dissolved in ¼ cup of cold water.
  • Pour the gravy over the pork ribs and serve over hot rice
