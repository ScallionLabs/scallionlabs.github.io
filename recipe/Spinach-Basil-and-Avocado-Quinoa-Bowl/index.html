Spinach, Basil and Avocado Quinoa Bowl

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado
  • ½ cup cannelini beans, drained and rinsed (save the rest of the can for topping bowls)
  • 15 fresh basil leaves
  • 1 large garlic clove
  • juice from half a lemon
  • cooked quinoa and extra beans for bowls
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 large handfuls of fresh spinach

Preparation

For dip
  • Combine all ingredients in a food processor and blend until smooth.For bowls: Scoop ½ to ¾ cup cooked quinoa into each bowl. Top with a few spoonfuls of basil bean dip and about ¼ cup beans per bowl. Stir to combine.
