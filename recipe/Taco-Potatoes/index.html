Taco Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cream cheese
  • 1 can mushroom soup
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • pepper
  • 1 package potatoes O'Brien frozen
  • 1 1/2 cups medium salsa
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • salt and pepper
  • 12 oz. shredded sharp cheddar cheese - reserve some for the top
  • 1 package taco seasoning
  • 1 cup water

Preparation

  • Add a cup of water to the meat and stir it in. Cook over medium heat until the water is gone.
  • Add the seasonings and salsa. Cook for 5 minutes.
  • Put the meat mixture in a greased casserole dish.
  • Mix together all of the ingredients for the potatoes in a bowl.
  • Pour over the meat mixture.
  • Sprinkle with the additional reserved cheese.
  • Bake at 400 degrees for about 50 minutes. If you thaw the potatoes ahead of time you will save a little baking time. It should be bubbly all over and the top browned when done.
