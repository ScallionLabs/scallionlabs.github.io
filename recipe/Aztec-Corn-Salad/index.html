Ingredients

  • 15 ounces black beans, drained and rinsed
  • ½ cup quinoa, cooked
  • 2 cups frozen corn, thawed
  • 3 tbsp lime juice

Preparation

  • Combine all ingredients except lime juice and chill or allow to cool until room temperature.
  • Add lime juice before serving. Chef's note: You can substitute three ears of fresh corn for the frozen.Nutritional Information
