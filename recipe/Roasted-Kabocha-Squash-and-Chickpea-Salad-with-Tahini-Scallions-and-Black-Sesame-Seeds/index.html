Roasted Kabocha Squash and Chickpea Salad with Tahini, Scallions and Black Sesame Seeds

  • Servings: 5
  • Ready in: 45min

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp black sesame seeds, toasted
  • 1 cup dried chickpeas, soaked overnight and boiled until tender
  • 1 kabocha squash, chopped into 1/2-inch cubes
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 1 small bunch scallions, thinly sliced
  • sea salt
  • 1 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1/4 cup tahini

Preparation

  • Heat oven to 40
  • Toss squash with olive oil and spread out on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  • Sprinkle with salt. Roast for 30 minutes or until tender.Toss the squash with the chickpeas.In a small bowl, whisk together the tahini, olive oil, scallions, and sesame oil. Season to taste with salt. Toss with the kabocha and chickpeas.Top with sesame seeds.
