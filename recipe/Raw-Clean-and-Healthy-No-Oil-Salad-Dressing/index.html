Ingredients

  • 5 hot banana pepper rings (or to taste)
  • 1 garlic clove, small
  • 1 1/2 - 2 cups parsley
  • 1/4 cup water

Preparation

  • Combine all ingredients in a Magic Bullet or blender and blend until smooth. Use within 1 hour. It will not be the same the next day. So, for best results use right away.
