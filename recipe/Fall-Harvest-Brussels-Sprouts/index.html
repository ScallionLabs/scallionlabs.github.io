Fall Harvest Brussels Sprouts

  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 18min
  • Preparation time: 10min
  • Cooking time: 8min
  • Tags:

Ingredients

  • 3 cups Brussels sprouts, shaved
  • 2 tbsp. candied pecans, chopped small
  • 1 cup carrot, grated
  • 1/4 cup dried cranberries
  • 1 tsp garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp. olive oil
  • 2 tbsp. orange juice
  • 1 small pear, cut into thin matchsticks

Preparation

  • In a large frying pan, over medium heat, add olive oil and garlic, cook for 30 seconds.
  • Add Brussels sprouts, carrots and pear. Cook for about 5 minutes until the vegetables are the tenderness you like. Just before you take it off the stove, add the orange juice. Give it a mix and pour into a bowl.
  • Mix in dried cranberries and pecans and salt and pepper to taste.
  • Serve warm!
