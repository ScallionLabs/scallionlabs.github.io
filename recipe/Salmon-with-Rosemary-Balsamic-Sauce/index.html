Salmon with Rosemary Balsamic Sauce

  • Ready in: 45min

Ingredients

  • ½ cup balsamic vinegar
  • ¼ cup chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh rosemary
  • Olive oil
  • 4 4-6 ounce salmon filets (preferably wild Alaskan salmon)
  • Salt and pepper

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 450 and line a baking sheet with foil.
  • Place salmon filets on the baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil and season with a little salt and pepper.
  • Bake for 15 minutes.While the salmon is baking, make the sauce: combine all sauce ingredients in a small pot and bring to a simmer. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  • Serve salmon over a bed of rice, pasta or veggies, drizzled with the sauce.
