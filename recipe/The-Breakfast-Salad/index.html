The Breakfast Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 avocados, diced
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 3 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 9 eggs, hard-boiled
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro , chopped
  • 2 lemons, juiced
  • 1/2 - 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 lb pork breakfast sausage or bacon
  • 1/4 cup purple onion, chopped

Preparation

  • Roll the sausage into 1-1/2" meatballs. Over high heat, cook and stir until brown on all sides and cooked through. Set aside to drain and cool.Peel the eggs and cut each into 8 pieces.
  • Combine the eggs, tomatoes, onion, avocado, meatballs, cilantro, salt, pepper, and lemon juice into a large mixing bowl.Stir until the egg yolks and avocado become a little creamy.Spoon into a bowl and enjoy!Will keep covered for 3 days in the refrigerator.
