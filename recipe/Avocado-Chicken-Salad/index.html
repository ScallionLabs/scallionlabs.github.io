Avocado Chicken Salad

  • http://www.emilybites.com/2014/04/avocado-chicken-salad.html
  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 45min
  • Tags:
    Ketogenic Salad Gluten Free Dairy Free

Macros

Ingredients

  • 1 medium avocado, cubed
  • 2 cups cubed cooked skinless chicken breast
  • 2-3 tablespoons minced cilantro
  • ½ a medium cucumber, diced
  • 1 tablespoon + 1 teaspoon fresh lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons low fat mayonnaise (I used Hellmann's low fat)
  • 2 scallions (greens only)
  • 4 slices center cut bacon

Preparation

  • Cook the bacon according to package instructions and then lay on paper towels to drain. When cooled, crumble the bacon into small pieces.
  • Combine the avocado chunks and lime juice in a large mixing bowl and gently stir to coat.
  • Add the chicken, cucumber, mayonnaise, cilantro and scallions stir gently (so as not to mash the avocado) until well mixed.
