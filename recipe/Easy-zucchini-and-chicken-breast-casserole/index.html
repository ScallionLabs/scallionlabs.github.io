Easy zucchini and chicken breast casserole

  • http://myzucchinirecipes.com/easy-zucchini-and-chicken-breast-casserole/
  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 35min
  • Preparation time: 10min
  • Cooking time: 25min
  • Tags:

  • High in: C Saturated Fat

Ingredients

  • black pepper
  • 150 g grated (parmesan) cheese
  • 400 g chicken breast fillet
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • paprika
  • salt
  • 200 ml single cream
  • 1 large or 2 medium zucchini

Preparation

  • Slice the chicken breast fillets into thin slices. Season them with salt, black pepper and paprika as taste. Rub the slices with crushed garlic.Crack the eggs into a deep bowl and beat them with a fork. Season with a pinch of salt.Prepare an ovenproof dish for the casserole: grease the dish with 2 tablespoon oil.Preheat the oven to 160C / 320F.Dip the chicken breast slices in the beaten eggs, then coat the slices in the grated cheese. Line the chicken slices in the ovenproof dish.
  • Cut the zucchinis into 1 cm / 0,4 inches. Line the zucchini slices on top of the chicken breasts.
  • Mix the rest of the beaten eggs with the singe cream.
  • Pour the mixture on top of the zucchini.
  • Sprinkle the top of the casserole with the leftover cheese.
  • Place the casserole in the preheated oven and bake it for 25 minutes until the top is golden brown.
