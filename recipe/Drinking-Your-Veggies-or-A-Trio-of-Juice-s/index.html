Ingredients

  • 3 oz/85g of baby spinach
  • 1 small beet scrubbed, peeled
  • 4 carrots, scrubbed
  • 4 celery stalks
  • 1 small handful of parsley leaves
  • 1 large lemon, peeled and seeds removed
  • 2 oz/56g pea shoots
  • ½ a large sweet potato scrubbed, peeled
  • ¼ of a large tart green apple, seeds removed

Preparation

  • Chop everything into chunks that fit into the juicer chute.Pass all the ingredients through the juicer, alternating hard and soft ingredients. Strain juice to remove foam if desired.
  • Pour juice into ice-filled glasses and serve immediately. Chop everything into chunks that fit into the juicer chute.Pass all the ingredients through the juicer, alternating ingredients. Strain to remove foam if desired.
