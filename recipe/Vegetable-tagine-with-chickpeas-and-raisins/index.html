Vegetable tagine with chickpeas & raisins

  • https://www.bbcgoodfood.com/recipes/4519/vegetable-tagine-with-chickpeas-and-raisins-
  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 30min
  • Preparation time: 10min
  • Cooking time: 20min
  • Tags:

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • ½ tsp each ground cinnamon, coriander and cumin
  • 2 large courgettes, cut into chunks
  • 2 chopped tomatoes
  • 400g can chickpea, rinsed and drained
  • 4 tbsp raisin
  • 425ml vegetable stock
  • 300g frozen pea
  • chopped coriander, to serve

Preparation

  • Heat the oil in a pan, then fry the onions for 5 mins until soft. Stir in the spices.
  • Add the courgettes, tomatoes, chickpeas, raisins and stock, then bring to the boil. Cover and simmer for 10 mins. Stir in the peas and cook for 5 mins more.
  • Sprinkle with coriander, to serve.
