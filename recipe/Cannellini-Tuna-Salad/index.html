Cannellini Tuna Salad

  • Ready in: 6min
  • Preparation time: 5min
  • Cooking time: 1min

  • High in: K Selenium Protein

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) can cannellini beans, drained and rinsed (I prefer Bush's Beans)
  • 1 tablespoon capers, rinsed and pat dry
  • handful of cherry tomatoes, sliced in half
  • 1-2 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
  • juice of 1/2 lemon
  • kosher salt and cracked pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 (12 ounce) can tuna in water, drained

Preparation

  • In a medium bowl, mix cannellini beans, tuna and capers.
  • Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice. Gently fold in cherry tomatoes and half of the parsley. Season with kosher salt and black pepper to taste.
  • Serve immediately on bed of spinach.
  • Garnish with remaining parsley. (Can be served cold, just refrigerate tuna salad and place on spinach when ready to serve)
