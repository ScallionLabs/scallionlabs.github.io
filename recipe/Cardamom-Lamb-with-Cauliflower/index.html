Cardamom Lamb with Cauliflower

  • https://www.azestybite.com/cardamom-lamb-with-cauliflower/
  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 25min
  • Preparation time: 10min
  • Cooking time: 15min
  • Tags:

Ingredients

  • 1 cauliflower head, chopped into florets
  • 2 tablespoons Organic Pompeian Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 pound lamb loin chops, cut into bite size pieces
  • 3/4 cup English peas
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric

Preparation

  • Bring a pot of water to boil.
  • Add in the cauliflower and boil for 3 minutes.
  • Remove from heat and set aside.
  • In a small bowl combine cardamom, cinnamon, turmeric, and salt.
  • Add the lamb to the bowl and thoroughly coat all sides with the seasoning.
  • Add olive oil to a saute pan over medium heat.
  • Add in the lamb mixture and cook for 5 minutes allowing it to brown. Then stir in cauliflower and peas; cook for an additional 5 minutes.
  • Season with salt before serving.
