Shaved Brussels Sprouts Salad with Avocado and Pistachios

  • http://diethood.com/2014/02/06/shaved-brussels-sprouts-salad-avocado-pistachios/
  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 15min
  • Preparation time: 10min
  • Cooking time: 5min
  • Tags:
    Vegetarian Salad Gluten Free Dairy Free Lacto Ovo Vegetarian Vegan
  • High in: K C

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado, peeled and cubed
  • 1-pound brussels sprouts, trimmed
  • salt and fresh ground pepper, to taste
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup pistachios, cleaned, peeled, and coarsely chopped
  • 1 large red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

Preparation

  • Heat olive oil in a nonstick pan and over medium heat.
  • Cut brussels sprouts in half and thinly slice; they will look shredded.
  • Add brussels sprouts and onion to the pan.Season with salt and pepper; saute for 3 to 4 minutes, or until tender.Stir in sugar, pistachios, and lime juice.Continue to cook for 2 more minutes.
  • Remove from heat.Gently mix in avocado chunks.
  • Serve immediately.
