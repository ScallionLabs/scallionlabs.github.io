Pesto Chicken Salad with Walnuts and Red Peppers

  • http://www.pepperlynn.com/2012/04/pesto-chicken-salad-with-walnuts-and-red-peppers/
  • Servings: 5
  • Ready in: 45min
  • Tags:
    Ketogenic Salad Gluten Free

Minerals RDA %

Macros

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup homemade basil pesto (see note in the Tips and Tidbits section below)
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 cup diced celery
  • 4 cups cubed cooked chicken
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • Juice from one lemon (about 1/4 cup)
  • 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • Finely grated zest from one lemon
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 cup diced red bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Preparation

  • In a medium bowl, whisk together the Greek yogurt, mayonnaise, pesto, lemon juice, lemon zest, salt and pepper until combined.
  • Place the chopped walnuts in a dry skillet over medium high heat for 3-5 minutes until fragrant, shaking the pan occasionally.
  • Remove from the heat and let cool.
  • Combine the toasted walnuts, cubed chicken, celery, and red bell pepper in a large mixing bowl; pour the pesto dressing over the ingredients and toss to coat. Season with additional salt and pepper to taste.You may serve the salad immediately or refrigerate for 1-2 hours to chill. It is scrumptious on a bed of greens, tucked between a couple slices of toasted bread, or my personal favorite – straight up with a fork.
