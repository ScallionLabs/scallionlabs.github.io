Seared Steak Salad with Creamy Avocado Dressing

  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 45min
  • Tags:
    Ketogenic Salad Gluten Free Dairy Free Valentine's Day Father's Day

Macros

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup avocado, diced
  • 1 tablespoon grapeseed or canola oil
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup cilantro leaves, plus more for garnish
  • adapted from Cooking Light Magazine
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 cup fresh corn kernels
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 4 cups dark leafy salad greens
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1/4 cup radishes, sliced
  • 1/2 cup red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1-1/2 pound sirloin steak
  • 1/2 yellow onion, sliced into rings

Preparation

  • In a blender or food processor, combine avocado, cilantro, lime juice, olive oil, and salt. Process until smooth. Dressing will be thick.In a large skillet or saute pan, heat oil over medium heat.
  • Add onions and saute for 5 minutes.Season steak with salt and pepper. Increase heat to medium-high, push onions to the sides of the pan and add the steak. Sear for 5 minutes a side —until golden brown— for medium-rare (6 minutes a side for medium).
  • Remove to a carving board and allow to rest for 5 minutes.
  • Add 2 tablespoons of water to the pan with the onions and scrape the bottom with a wooden spoon to remove the browned bits leftover by the steak. Once water has absorbed, remove the onions and set aside. Carve the sirloin into 1/4-inch slices.In a large bowl, combine dressing, lettuce, corn, tomatoes, bell pepper, and radishes; toss to combine. Divide between 4 plates and lay 4 slices of sirloin and a spoonful of caramelized onions on top of each salad.
  • Garnish with cilantro and a spoonful of queso fresco; serve.
