  • 1 1/2 cups water

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Toss squash with olive oil, salt and pepper and place on baking sheet in one even layer.
  • Bake 10 minutes, turn and bake another 10 minutes until browned and soft.
  • Remove from oven and set aside.Rinse wheat berries in cold water.
  • Drain.
  • Place wheatberries + water into small saucepan and sprinkle with salt. Cover and bring to boil. Reduce to simmer and cook covered for 45-50 minutes or until cooked. These will usually stay pretty chewy, fyi.In a large serving bowl, toss kale, feta, pistachios, craisins, roasted squash and wheat berries together with my favorite salad dressing.
  • Serve.
