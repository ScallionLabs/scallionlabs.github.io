Pan fried halibut with kiwi berry salsa

  • Servings: 2
  • Ready in: 15min
  • Preparation time: 10min
  • Cooking time: 5min
  • Tags:
    Pescetarian Lunch Main Course Main Dish Dinner Dairy Free Pescatarian Mexican
  • High in: Selenium C

Ingredients

  • ½ avocado, cut into cubes
  • 1 tablespoon cilantro, chopped
  • 2-3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 6 oz. halibut filets
  • ½ tablespoon honey
  • juice of ½ a lime
  • 6 oz. kiwi berries, sliced in half
  • 1 tablespoon red onion, minced
  • salt & pepper
  • 2 tablespoons flour (I used whole wheat)

Preparation

  • Season both filets with salt & pepper.
  • Place flour in a small, shallow dish.Coat each fillet in flour on all sides, set aside.Bring a medium skillet to med-high heat with olive oil in it.Once hot, place halibut in pan and "fry" on each side for 2-3 minutes until cooked through.
  • Combine kiwis, avocado, red onion and cilantro in a small bowl.
  • Whisk together lime juice, honey, salt & pepper in another small bowl and pour over salsa.Toss gently to combine.Top cooked fish with salsa and serve immediately.
