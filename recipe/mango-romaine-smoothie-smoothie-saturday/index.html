mango romaine smoothie {smoothie saturday}

Ingredients

  • 1 medium banana
  • 16 ounces coconut water
  • 1 1/2 cups ice (less if using frozen fruit)
  • 2 mangos, peeled and pitted
  • 1 small orange, peeled
  • 1 head romaine, washed and cut into thirds

Preparation

  • Place all ingredients in a high powered blender and blend until smooth. If using a regular blender, chop fruit and veggies small and add in smaller amounts. Enjoy!
