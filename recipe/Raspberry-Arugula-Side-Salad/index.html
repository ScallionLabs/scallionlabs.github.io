Ingredients

  • 2 oz arugula
  • 1 oz baby spinach
  • 1 cucumber, chopped
  • 10-15 parsley or cilantro leaves
  • 14 pitted olives
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 Tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 Tablespoon lemon juice
  • 14 raspberries

Preparation

  • Place all the ingredients into a salad bowl (excluding the raspberries) and toss well.
  • Add salt to taste. Top with raspberries but do not mix in as they get crushed easily.
