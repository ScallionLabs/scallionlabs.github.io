Avocado Toast with Spicy Black Bean Burger Strips

  • Servings: 2
  • Ready in: 8min
  • Cooking time: 8min
  • Tags:
    Vegetarian Side Dish Dairy Free Lacto Ovo Vegetarian Vegan American

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 - 2 avocados
  • 2 MorningStar Farms Spicy Black Bean Burgers
  • fresh corn
  • 2 slices multigrain bread, toasted
  • sliced red onion
  • sliced tomato

Preparation

  • Cut the avocado in half lengthwise, twist to separate, and remove & discard the pit. Scoop out the avocado flesh.
  • Add it to a small bowl, and mash with a fork.Cook the Spicy Black Bean Burgers according to package instructions.
  • Cut into strips.
  • Spread the mashed avocado on each piece of toast.
  • Add any optional toppings, then top with veggie burger strips.
  • Serve immediately.
