Tuna Pomodoro

Ingredients

  • 2 anchovies, minced (optional)
  • 1 28-ounce can diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper, or to taste
  • 2 tablespoons thinly sliced fresh basil
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 6-ounce can chunk light tuna, drained and flaked (see Note)
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 8 ounces whole-wheat spaghetti

Preparation

  • Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Cook spaghetti, stirring occasionally, until just tender, 9 to 11 minutes or according to package directions.
  • Drain.Meanwhile, heat oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat.
  • Add garlic and cook, stirring, until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  • Add anchovies (if using) and crushed red pepper and cook for 30 seconds more.
  • Add tomatoes, reduce heat to medium and cook, stirring occasionally, for 8 minutes. Stir in tuna and cook until it is incorporated into the sauce and heated through, 2 minutes more. Divide the spaghetti evenly among 4 plates, top with sauce and garnish with basil.
  • Serve hot.
