hara bhara kabab , how to make hara bhara kabab

  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 45min
  • Tags:
    Vegetarian Side Dish Gluten Free Lacto Ovo Vegetarian

Vitamins RDA %

Minerals RDA %

Macros

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ tbsp besan/gram flour
  • 1 tsp chaat masala powder
  • 1 tsp amchur powder/dry mango powder
  • ¾ cup peas/matar, fresh or frozen
  • 2 medium size potatoes/aloo
  • salt as required
  • 2 cups spinach/palak

Preparation

  • blanch the spinach in salted water. drain and keep them in a cold water. finely chop the spinach and keep aside.roast besan/gram flour in a small pan till it becomes aromatic and changes its color. don't over roast or darken the gram flour.steam or boil the potatoes and peas. in a bowl take the chopped spinach. grate the boiled potatoes in the bowl. add the peas & green-chili paste.mash the kabab mixture with a potato masher or with a wooden spoon.add all the dry spice powder, roasted gram flour and salt. mix the kabab mixture uniformly.shape into round patties and shallow or pan fry the kababs till golden brown.serve hara bhara kabab hot with chutney or sauce.
