Smashed Chickpea & Avocado Salad Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 1 large ripe avocado
  • 1 (15 ounce) can chickpeas or garbanzo beans (I use Bush's Garbanzo Beans)
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons chopped green onion
  • Juice from 1 lime
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh spinach leaves or other sandwich toppings: lettuce, tomato slices, sprouts, etc.
  • Bread (I use whole wheat bread)

Preparation

  • Rinse and drain the chickpeas.
  • Place on a paper towel and remove the outer skins. You can leave them on, but I like to remove them.
  • In a medium bowl, using a fork or potato masher smash the chickpeas and avocado together.
  • Add in cilantro, green onion, and lime juice. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.
  • Spread salad on bread and top with your favorite sandwich toppings. I like to add fresh spinach leaves. Note: This salad also makes a great dip.
  • Serve with cut up veggies, crackers, or pita chips. Also, this salad is best eaten the day it is made because it will turn brown due to the avocado.
