Spicy Sun-Dried Tomato Soup with White Beans & Swiss Chard

  • Servings: 8
  • Ready in: 45min

Ingredients

  • 1 cup torn fresh basil
  • 2 15-oz. cans diced tomatoes
  • 1 15-oz. can small white beans or cannellini beans, rinsed and drained
  • 2 medium carrots, sliced (1 cup)
  • 2 ribs celery, chopped (½ cup)
  • ½ tsp. chopped fresh rosemary
  • ½ tsp. chopped fresh thyme
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced (1 Tbs.)
  • ½ cup oil-packed sun-dried tomatoes, drained and chopped, plus 2 Tbs. oil from jar
  • 2 Tbs. olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped (1 ½ cups)
  • ½ tsp. red pepper flakes
  • ½ bunch (6 oz.) Swiss chard, chopped
  • 2 cups low-sodium vegetable broth
  • 1 small zucchini, sliced (1 cup)

Preparation

  • Heat oil in large saucepan over medium heat.
  • Add garlic and red pepper flakes, and cook 1 minute, or until garlic is fragrant. Stir in onion, carrots, celery, zucchini, and rosemary, and cook 10 to 15 minutes, or until onions are soft.
  • Add broth, 1 can tomatoes, and beans. Scoop 1 cup mixture into food processor or blender, and add remaining can of tomatoes, sun-dried tomatoes, and sun-dried tomato oil. Purée until smooth, stir mixture into soup, and season with salt and pepper, if desired. Simmer 10 minutes.
  • Add Swiss chard and thyme; simmer 5 minutes more, or until chard is wilted.
  • Remove pan from heat, and stir in basil.
