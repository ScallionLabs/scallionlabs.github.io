Veggie burrito bowls

  • http://www.amuse-your-bouche.com/veggie-burrito-bowls/
  • Servings: 2
  • Ready in: 25min
  • Preparation time: 5min
  • Cooking time: 20min
  • Tags:
    Vegetarian Side Dish Gluten Free Lacto Ovo Vegetarian Mexican
  • High in: C Manganese

Ingredients

  • 1 x 400g tin black beans, drained (240g when drained)
  • Black pepper
  • 100g brown rice
  • 40g cheddar cheese, grated
  • - 12 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1tsp hot chilli powder (adjust as desired!)
  • 1tbsp fresh coriander, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper (I used green, but any colour will do)
  • Salt
  • 1½ tsp smoked paprika
  • 3 spring onions, finely chopped
  • 1tbsp sunflower oil

Preparation

  • Boil the brown rice according to the instructions on the packet.Meanwhile, heat the oil in a large, deep frying pan, and cook the onions, garlic and pepper over a medium heat for 5 minutes.
  • Add the cherry tomatoes, along with the chilli powder and paprika, and cook for a few more minutes. Squash the tomatoes a little with your spoon to release their juices.
  • Add the black beans, and season to taste.When the rice is cooked, drain it and add it to the bean mixture. If the mixture seems a little dry, add a dash of water (you might not need it - it will depend on a few things, e.g. how much liquid your tomatoes released).When the mixture is hot and well combined, serve topped with the grated cheese and fresh coriander - I popped mine in the microwave for about a minute to melt the cheese.
