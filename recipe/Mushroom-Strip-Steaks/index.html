Mushroom Strip Steaks

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless beef top loin steaks (8 ounces each)
  • 1 can (10-1/2 ounces) condensed French onion soup, undiluted
  • 1 jar (6 ounces) sliced mushrooms, drained
  • 1/2 cup shredded part-skim mozzarella cheese
  • 1 to 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Preparation

  • In a large skillet over medium-high heat, cook steaks in oil for 4-6 minutes on each side or until meat reaches desired doneness (for medium-rare, a meat thermometer should read 145°; medium, 160°; well-done, 170°).
  • Drain.
  • Top each steak with the soup, mushrooms and mozzarella cheese. Cover and cook for 2-4 minutes or until the cheese is melted.
