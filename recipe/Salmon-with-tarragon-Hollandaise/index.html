Salmon with tarragon Hollandaise

  • https://www.bbcgoodfood.com/recipes/4649/salmon-with-tarragon-hollandaise
  • Servings: 2
  • Ready in: 20min
  • Preparation time: 5min
  • Cooking time: 15min
  • Tags:
    Ketogenic Lunch Main Course Main Dish Dinner Gluten Free Dairy Free Pescatarian

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 salmon fillets, scaled, skin on, about 140g 5oz each
  • 125g pack asparagus tips, ends trimmed
  • 2 bunches cherry tomatoes on the vine
  • 1 tbsp chopped tarragon
  • 150ml hollandaise sauce, to serve (we used Joubére Fresh Hollandaise Sauce)

Preparation

  • Heat oven to 200C/fan 180C/gas
  • Heat the oil in an ovenproof pan over a high heat.
  • Add the salmon, skin-side down, then cook for 5 mins until the skin is crisp.
  • Add the asparagus and vine tomatoes to the pan, then place in the oven. Cook for 7-10 mins until the salmon is just cooked through.
  • Add the tarragon to the Hollandaise and stir through.
  • Drizzle over the salmon, then serve.
