Ingredients

  • 2 Tbs fresh ginger
  • 2 c kale - chopped
  • 2 Tbs lemon juice
  • 1 peach - chopped
  • 1 pear - chopped
  • 2 Tbs water
  • 2 c canteloupe - chopped

Preparation

  • Place all ingredients in a high quality blender and blend on high until juice forms.Feel free to add more/less water or ice depending on desired consistency.
