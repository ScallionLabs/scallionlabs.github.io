Ingredients

  • 1 medium banana (frozen) $0.22
  • ¼ tsp cinnamon $0.02
  • 1 Tbsp ground flaxseed $0.03
  • 1 Tbsp honey $0.12
  • ¼ tsp pumpkin pie spice $0.05
  • 1/3 cup pumpkin puree $0.42
  • 1 cup vanilla soy milk $0.41

Preparation

  • Add everything to a blender.Blend until smooth. You may need to stop to stir once or twice. If the smoothie is too thick, add a touch more soy milk or even a little water.
