Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups small cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 to 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1/3 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 pound cleaned squid, bodies and tentacles separated

Preparation

  • Steam squid bodies and tentacles untiltender, about 3 minutes.
  • Transfer to plateand cool to lukewarm.
  • Cut squid bodies into 1/2-inch-widerings.
  • Cut squid tentacles in half if large.
  • Transfer squid to large bowl.
  • Add babyarugula, cherry tomatoes, beans, oliveoil, and 2 tablespoons lemon juice. Tossgently to blend, adding more lemon juice, ifdesired. Season with salt and pepper.
