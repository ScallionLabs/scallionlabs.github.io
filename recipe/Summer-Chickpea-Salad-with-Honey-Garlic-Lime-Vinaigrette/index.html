Summer Chickpea Salad with Honey Garlic Lime Vinaigrette

  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 35min

    Vegetarian Salad Gluten Free Dairy Free Lacto Ovo Vegetarian 4th Of July Summer

Ingredients

  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes, quartered
  • 3 cups chickpeas (or 2 (15-ounce) cans, drained and rinsed)
  • 2 ears grilled corn, cut from the cob
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
  • 2 tablespoons snipped fresh chives
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced or pressed
  • 4 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 lime, juiced and zested
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Preparation

  • In a large bowl, combine the chickpeas, onions, corn, tomatoes, basil and chives. Toss well with the salt and pepper. In a small saucepan, add the oil, vinegar, honey, garlic, lime juice and zest.
  • Heat over low heat and whisk until the mixture is warm and garlic cloves are sizzling a bit.
  • Pour the liquid over the chickpeas and and toss well to coat. Cover with a piece of plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving. It tastes better as it sits!
  • Serve with toasted baguettes spread with coconut oil or whipped feta.
