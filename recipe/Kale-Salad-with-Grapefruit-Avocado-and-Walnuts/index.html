Kale Salad with Grapefruit, Avocado & Walnuts

  • Servings: 1
  • Ready in: 45min

Ingredients

  • 1/2 of an avocado, chopped
  • 1/2 of a large ruby red grapefruit, segmented (no white pith) and seeds removed
  • 2 tablespoons ruby red grapefruit juice
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 2 cups kale, stems removed, finely chopped
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
  • salt and pepper
  • 1/4 cup walnuts, roughly chopped

Preparation

  • Place the kale in the serving dish (to tenderize your kale, give the leaves a 30 second massage with your hands). In a small bowl, whisk together the dressing ingredients until well combined.
  • Drizzle 2/3 of the dressing over the kale and toss until coated. Top with grapefruit, avocado, and walnuts.
  • Drizzle remaining dressing over the top and serve.
