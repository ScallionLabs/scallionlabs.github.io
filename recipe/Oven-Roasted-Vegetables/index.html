Oven Roasted Vegetables

Ingredients

  • 1/2 teaspoon McCormick® Black Pepper, Ground
  • 1 lb. brussels sprouts
  • 1 small butternut squash, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 1/2 teaspoon McCormick® Nutmeg, Ground
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large red onion, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 1/2 teaspoon McCormick® Sage, Rubbed
  • 1 teaspoon seasoned salt

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 450°F.
  • Mix seasoned salt, pepper, nutmeg and sage in small bowl. Toss vegetables with oil in large bowl.
  • Sprinkle seasoning mixture over vegetables; toss to coat well.
  • Spread vegetables in single layer on foil-lined 15x10x1-inch baking pan.
  • Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until vegetables are tender and golden brown.
