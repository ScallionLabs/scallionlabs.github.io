Asian Pesto Stuffed Chili Glazed Salmon

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup sliced almonds
  • 1/3 cup Canola, Olive and Grapeseed Oil Blend (could also use Peanut Oil)
  • 3 cloves Garlic
  • Crushed Red Pepper Flakes
  • 2 Salmon Filets, skin on
  • Sea Salt
  • 1 Tbsp Sesame Oil
  • 1/8 cup Sweet Chili Sauce (available at Asian Markets)
  • 1 cup loosely packed Thai Basil, leaves and flowers, stems removed
  • 2/3 cup sliced almonds
  • 3 cloves Garlic
  • Crushed Red Pepper Flakes
  • 2 Salmon Filets, skin on
  • 1/8 cup Sweet Chili Sauce (available at Asian Markets)
  • 1 cup loosely packed Thai Basil, leaves and flowers, stems removed

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 350FSlice salmon filets down the middle, leaving a couple of inches at the end intact so that the filet folds open like a book.Put garlic, Thai basil, and almonds in the bowl of a food processor and pulse to chop finely and combine.With machine running, pour oils into the feed tube.Season with Sea Salt and a generous pinch of crushed red pepper flakes.Press a generous layer of the pesto onto the bottom half of the salmon filet to cover.Fold the top half over the pesto.
  • Place the filets in a lightly oiled oven proof ceramic dish.
  • Brush the chili sauce generously over the top of the fish and place in oven
  • Bake for 20 minutes
