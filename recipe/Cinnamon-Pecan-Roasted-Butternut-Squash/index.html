Cinnamon Pecan Roasted Butternut Squash

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar, packed
  • 1 large butternut squash (about 3 pounds), peeled, seeded and cut in 1-inch chunks
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup Fisher Nuts Pecan Halves
  • 2 sprigs rosemary

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Lightly oil a baking sheet or coat with nonstick spray.
  • Place butternut squash in a single layer onto the prepared baking sheet.
  • Add olive oil, maple syrup, brown sugar, cinnamon and nutmeg. Gently toss to combine.
  • Place into oven and bake for 25-30 minutes, turning once, or until tender.*
  • Add pecans during the last 10 minutes of cooking time.
  • Serve immediately, garnished with rosemary, if desired.
