Preparation

  • Arrange the beef in a crockpot that has been sprayed with non stick cooking spray. Arrange the onion around the beef.
  • Mix together the salt, olive oil, vinegar, lime juice, and oregano.
  • Pour the mixture over the beef. Cover and cook the beef on high for 8 hours.
  • Remove the beef and onion from the crockpot. Shred the beef and onions onto a large platter.
  • Serve with the diced tomatoes, cilantro and avocado.
