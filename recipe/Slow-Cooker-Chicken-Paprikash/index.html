Slow Cooker Chicken Paprikash

Ingredients

  • ½ bell pepper
  • 1 kg | 2.3 lbs chicken thighs (about 5-6 pieces), skin on
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • ½ teaspoon parsley
  • pepper
  • salt
  • ½ medium tomato
  • 3 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 cup water (or broth)

Preparation

  • In a large pot heat 2 tablespoons olive oil and add the chopped onions. Cook for 1-2 minutes on medium heat.
  • Add the chicken thighs to the pot, saut them on both sides until they turn golden brown, for 5-8 minutes.
  • Remove the chicken thighs and transfer them to the slow cooker. Season them with salt and pepper as you taste.
  • Chop the tomato, bell pepper and garlic, add them to the onion and on medium heat saut them.
  • Add one tablespoon smocked paprika, stir well and immediately add a cup of water. Cook the stew on low heat for 5-6 minutes, until starts to thicken. Season with parsley, salt and pepper.
  • Pour the paprika stew all over the chicken thighs, make sure the meat is coated well.
  • Cook in slow cooker on high for 3-3 hours or 5 hours on low.
  • Serve the chicken paprikash with sour cream and pickles.
