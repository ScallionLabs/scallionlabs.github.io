Banana Bread in a Bowl

Ingredients

  • 1 banana (I use a frozen one, but you don't have to)
  • 1 cup flake cereal (I used Whole Foods' bran flakes. Feel free to sub your favorite cereal.)
  • 3/4 c milk (I used almond milk)
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1-2 tablespoons walnuts, optional

Preparation

  • Put all the ingredients into a blender, and blend. Now eat! This gets thicker, the longer it sits.For a fun twist, try using the Melted Banana Trick Oh, and cinnamon or chocolate chips make nice additions. But then again,chocolate chipsmake a nice addition to anything!
