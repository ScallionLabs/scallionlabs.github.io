Healthy and Delicious: Confetti Quinoa Salad

  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 50min

Ingredients

  • 1 carrot
  • 2 cups fresh corn, cut from cob
  • 1 jalapeño pepper
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons parsley
  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1 1/2 cups red beans
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • Sea salt and fresh ground pepper to taste
  • 2 shallots
  • 2 cups water

Preparation

  • Rinse quinoa to remove bitter coating. Then cook quinoa in water for 15 minutes or until fluffy. Set aside.
  • Dice red onion, red bell pepper, carrot. Seed and mince jalapeño pepper. Keep the seeds for a spicier salad. Chop parsley.
  • Combine all the chopped vegetables in a large mixing bowl along with corn.
  • Add oil, lime juice, salt, and pepper to the vegetables and stir well to combine.
  • Toss quinoa with veggies, serve warm or chilled, and swoon.
