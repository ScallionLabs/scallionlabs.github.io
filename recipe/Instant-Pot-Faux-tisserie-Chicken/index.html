Instant Pot Faux-tisserie Chicken

  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 38min
  • Preparation time: 5min
  • Cooking time: 33min

Ingredients

  • 1/2 teaspoon basil
  • 1 cup chicken stock/broth, or water
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 Tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 medium onion, cut into quarters
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • sea salt & black pepper, to taste
  • southwest seasoning mix or 2 Tablespoons of your favorite seasoning
  • 2 1/2 to 3 pound whole chicken
  • 5 large cloves fresh garlic, peeled and left whole

Preparation

  • Rub chicken with one Tablespoon of olive oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  • Place the onion wedges and garlic cloves inside the chicken. Use butcher's twine to secure the legs.Turn on the pressure cooker and press the
  • Saute button.
  • Add the remaining olive oil to the metal pan. When hot, add the chicken and sear/brown both sides, about 4 minutes per side. {included in cooking time}
  • Remove the chicken and set aside.
  • Place the trivet at the bottom of the metal pan and add the chicken stock.
  • Sprinkle seasoning mix over the entire chicken, rubbing it in and spreading it around to cover the entire chicken.
  • Place the chicken, breast side up on top of the trivet and secure the lid. Make sure the lid is in the "Sealing" position.Set the pressure cooker to Manual and set timer for 25 minutes.When the timer beeps, allow the pressure to release naturally for 15 minutes. If the lid will not open, quick release the remaining pressure and remove the chicken.Allow chicken to rest for 5 to 10 minutes before serving.
