Vegetable Stir-Fry with Endive & Shiitake Mushrooms

  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 15min
  • Preparation time: 10min
  • Cooking time: 5min
  • Tags:
    Vegetarian Side Dish Gluten Free Dairy Free Lacto Ovo Vegetarian Vegan
  • High in: K A Folate Manganese

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp canola oil
  • 3/4 tsp chili garlic sauce
  • 8 oz. crimini (Baby Bella) mushrooms, sliced
  • 4 heads endive, cut in half lengthwise, then sliced crosswise
  • 1 tbsp minced fresh ginger
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 oz. shiitake mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 tbsp plus 2 tsp soy sauce

Preparation

  • Heat the canola oil in a large nonstick skillet set over medium-high heat.
  • Add the ginger and cook, stirring, for 1 minute.
  • Add the garlic and cook for additional 30 seconds.
  • Add the shiitake and crimini mushrooms, and cook until the mushrooms are almost soft, about 3 minutes.
  • Add the endive and cook until tender, about 2 minutes.Stir in the soy sauce and chili-garlic sauce.
  • Serve.
