Grilled Shrimp and Feta Salad

Ingredients

  • 8 cups baby arugula (about 8 ounces)
  • 1 cup sliced cucumber
  • 8 ounces feta cheese, crumbled, for serving
  • 1/2 cup pitted kalamata olives
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground pepper
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1/3 cup extra-virgin olive oil, plus more for shrimp
  • 1/4 cup thinly sliced red onion
  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined

Preparation

  • Preheat a grill to medium-high heat. Lightly oil the shrimp, then sprinkle with salt and pepper. Grill until just cooked through, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
  • Toss the shrimp, arugula, cucumbers, olives, and onions together in a bowl.
  • Whisk together the lemon juice, 1/3 cup olive oil, and season with salt and pepper.
  • Pour over the salad and toss to coat. Top with the feta cheese.
