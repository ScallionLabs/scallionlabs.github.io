Thai Red Beef Curry with Green Beans

Ingredients

  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • 1 (14 oz) can coconut milk
  • 1 Tbsp coconut oil
  • 2 tsp fish sauce*
  • 1 lb French-cut green beans
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, halved and sliced
  • 3 Tbsp red curry paste*
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 lbs charcoal steak, cut into 1-inch cubes

Preparation

  • Heat the coconut oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Season the beef with salt and pepper and add to hot skillet. Cook until browned on all sides, about 5-6 minutes; transfer to a slow cooker, reserving any fat in the skillet and return to heat.To the skillet, add the onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  • Add the garlic and cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
  • Add the coconut milk and curry paste; whisking well.
  • Transfer coconut mixture to the crock pot.Cook on low for 7-8 hours or on high for 3 to 3 hours.
  • Add the green beans and stir. Allow to cook for 30 minutes (on low), or until crisp-tender, before serving.
