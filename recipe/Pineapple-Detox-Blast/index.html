Pineapple Detox Blast

  • Ready in: 5min
  • Tags:

  • High in: K C A

Minerals RDA %

Macros

Ingredients

  • ½ Small Cucumber
  • 2 Handfuls Kale
  • 2 Tablespoons Lemon Juice
  • ¼ Cup Mint
  • ½ Cup Pineapple
  • Water

Preparation

  • Add all solid ingredients into tall cup
  • Fill to MAX line with liquids
  • Blend until smooth, approximately 30 seconds
