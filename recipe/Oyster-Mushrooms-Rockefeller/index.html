Oyster Mushrooms Rockefeller

Ingredients

  • 2 lb. baby spinach
  • 2 tsp. Dijon mustard
  • ½ cup chopped fresh fennel
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced (2 tsp.)
  • 4 Tbs. olive oil, divided
  • 2 12-oz. pkg. fresh oyster mushrooms, tough parts of stems removed
  • 2 Tbs. chopped shallots
  • ½ cup vegan mayonnaise
  • 2 lb. baby spinach
  • 2 tsp. Dijon mustard
  • ½ cup chopped fresh fennel
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced (2 tsp.)
  • 2 12-oz. pkg. fresh oyster mushrooms, tough parts of stems removed
  • 2 Tbs. chopped shallots
  • ½ cup vegan mayonnaise

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 400°F.
  • Spread mushrooms in bottom of baking dish or baking sheet.
  • Heat 1 Tbs. oil in large skillet over medium-high heat. Sauté half of spinach 1 to 2 minutes, or until wilted, tossing occasionally.
  • Remove from pan with tongs.
  • Add 1 Tbs. olive oil to pan, and repeat with remaining spinach.
  • Spread wilted spinach atop mushrooms in baking dish.
  • Whisk together remaining 2 Tbs. oil, mayonnaise, fennel, mustard, shallots, and garlic in small bowl.
  • Spread sauce over spinach until well covered.
  • Bake 15 minutes, or until sauce begins to brown. Set oven to broil.
  • Place baking dish under broiler, and broil 2 to 3 minutes, or until brown and bubbly.
  • Serve as casserole, or spoon servings onto plates.
