Eat For Eight Bucks: Black Bean and Sweet Potato Chili

Ingredients

  • 3 15-ounce cans black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 15-ounce can diced tomatoes
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 small chipotle chile pepper in adobo sauce, minced
  • ¼ cup chopped cilantro stems
  • ¼ cup chopped cilantro leaves
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 bottle dark beer
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Kosher salt
  • 1/2 lime (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 orange pepper, diced
  • 1 red pepper, diced
  • 1 pound sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 medium yellow onion, diced

Preparation

  • Heat oil in large Dutch oven or heavy-lidded saucepan over medium heat.
  • Saute onion and peppers until soft and beginning to caramelize, 5-8 minutes.
  • Add garlic, cumin, and chili powder and continue to sauté until very fragrant, about 2 minutes. Stir in tomatoes, beans, chipotle, cilantro stems, and 1 teaspoon salt. Turn heat to high, and pour in beer. Simmer for 10 minutes, then add sweet potatoes.
  • Continue to simmer until potatoes are tender, but not falling apart, about 20 minutes. Taste for seasoning. Spoon chili into bowls and garnish with cilantro leaves, a squeeze of lime, and a dollop of sour cream.
  • Serve with corn bread.
