Roasted Lemon Almond Cornish Hens {Slow Cooker}

Ingredients

  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup slivered or sliced almonds, toasted and divided
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 2 Cornish hens, thawed
  • 4 teaspoons minced fresh thyme, divided
  • 4 cloves garlic, minces and divided
  • 3 lemons

Preparation

  • Slice one lemon; place 2 to 3 slices in the cavity of each hen.
  • Place one teaspoon minced thyme and 1 clove of mince garlic in each cavity.
  • Place hens in slow cooker.Juice 1 lemon; mix lemon juice, butter, remaining garlic and thyme, salt and pepper and drizzle over hens.
  • Sprinkle with 1/4 cup almonds.Cover and cook on low 8 to 10 hours (4 to 6 on high). If desired, baste hens with juices throughout the time.Arrange cooked hens on a serving platter.
  • Serve with remaining lemon slices and garnish with remaining almonds.
