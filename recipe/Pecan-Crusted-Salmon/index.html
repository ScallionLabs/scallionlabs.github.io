Pecan Crusted Salmon

Ingredients

  • 1 cloves garlic, minced
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ¼ cup parsley, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons chopped pecans (salted or unsalted)
  • 2 pounds salmon filets
  • Salt & pepper

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C).In a small bowl, combine pecans, parsley, garlic and lemon zest with olive oil.Season the salmon filets liberally with salt and pepper.
  • Spread the topping evenly over the salmon filets.
  • Bake salmon on a parchment lined baking sheet for 20 minutes or until fish is fully cooked.
