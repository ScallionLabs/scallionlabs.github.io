Spaghetti with No-Cook Tomato Sauce and Hazelnuts

  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 45min

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basil leaves, divided
  • 1 beefsteak tomato, chopped
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 pound cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • ½ cup blanched hazelnuts
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt, plus more
  • ¼ cup olive oil, plus more for drizzling
  • 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 ounce ricotta salata (salted dry ricotta), shaved
  • 12 ounces spaghetti or linguine
  • 2 small zucchini (about 8 ounces), coarsely grated

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 35
  • Toast hazelnuts on a rimmed baking sheet, tossing once, until golden brown, 810 minutes.
  • Let cool, then coarsely chop.
  • Place cherry tomatoes in a large bowl; season with salt.Cook spaghetti in a large pot of boiling salted water, stirring occasionally, until al dente.
  • Drain pasta, reserving cup pasta cooking liquid. Meanwhile, pure beefsteak tomato, garlic, red pepper flakes, cup basil, 3 Tbsp. chopped hazelnuts, and 1 tsp. salt in a food processor until smooth; add to bowl with salted cherry tomatoes.
  • Add zucchini, spaghetti, pasta cooking liquid, cup oil, and remaining cup basil leaves and toss to combine; season with salt and pepper. Divide pasta among bowls, drizzle with more oil, and top with ricotta salata and remaining hazelnuts.
