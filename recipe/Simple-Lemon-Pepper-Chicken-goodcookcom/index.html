Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons black pepper
  • 1 stick of butter- softened
  • 2 large carrots
  • Pinch of cayenne pepper
  • 1 whole roaster chicken
  • 5 sprigs of dill
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1½ lemons
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Quarter cup of fresh parsley

Preparation

  • Rinse chicken and remove innards.
  • Cut ½ lemon into quarters and slice carrots into chunks.Stuff chicken with dill, lemons and carrots.
  • Mix butter, spices, ½ teaspoon black peppers and lemon zest and smear over chicken.Use remaining black pepper to sprinkle over chicken.
  • Place thinly sliced lemon over the entire chicken.
  • Place chicken in the oven at 350 degrees.
