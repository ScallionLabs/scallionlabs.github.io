Salmon and Avocado Salad

  • Servings: 2
  • Ready in: 45min
  • Tags:

Ingredients

  • 1/2 ripe avocado
  • 1 -113g (6oz) can wild-caught salmon
  • 2 hard boiled eggs
  • 1 tablespoon Frank's Red Hot
  • 2 tablespoons sliced green olives
  • 1 tablespoon paleo mayo
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup chopped spinach
  • 2 tablespoons raw walnuts, chopped

Preparation

  • Combine all the ingredients except for the chopped spinach in a large mixing bowl. Mash with an egg or potato masher (a fork would also do the trick) until well combined. Using a spoon this time, delicately stir in the chopped spinach. You can serve this salad immediately but it greatly benefits from a short stay in the refrigerator. It can also be made up to a day in advance and will keep for up to 2 days in an airtight container.
  • Serve as is or all wrapped up in collard leaves.
