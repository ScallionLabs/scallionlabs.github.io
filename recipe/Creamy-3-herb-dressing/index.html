Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons chives
  • 1 clove garlic
  • dash worcestshire sauce
  • ½ lemon, juiced
  • ¼ cup parsley
  • ½ cup plain (preferably full fat) Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon chopped red onion
  • 1 head of romaine lettuce, cut into wedges
  • 1 tablespoon sage
  • salt & pepper to taste

Preparation

  • Combine everything but the fresh herbs in a food processor.Process until smooth.Pulse in the fresh herbs until finely chopped.
  • Pour over the lettuce wedges and serve.
