Strawberry Spinach Salad

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup raw almond
  • 5 cups fresh baby spinach
  • 1 lemon, squeezed
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup fresh snow peas, trimmed
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced

Preparation

  • Combine spinach leaves, snow peas, strawberries, almonds in a bowl.
  • Add in salt and lemon juice.
  • Combine well and serve.
