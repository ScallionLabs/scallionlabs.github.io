Easy paleo lunch – Stuffed Avocado with Bacon, Chicken & Tomato

  • Servings: 2
  • Ready in: 45min
  • Tags:
    Ketogenic Lunch Main Course Main Dish Dinner Gluten Free Dairy Free Paleolithic
  • High in: B3 B6 Selenium Protein K

Ingredients

  • 2 avocados
  • 2 chicken breasts, grilled
  • 2-3 strips cooked bacon, chopped
  • 1/3 cup chopped grape tomatoes
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1/4 to 1/3 cup of a good paleo mayo

Preparation

  • Season chicken as desired, grill, and cut into cubes
  • Place cubed chicken in a medium bowl.
  • Add tomatoes, onions and bacon
  • Add paleo mayo and gently mix everything together
  • Just before serving, slice avocados in half and discard pit
  • Pile the chicken, bacon, tomato mix on top of each avocado half and enjoy!
