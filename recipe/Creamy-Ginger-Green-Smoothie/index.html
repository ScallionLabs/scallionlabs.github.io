Ingredients

  • ½ avocado
  • 1 medium banana
  • 2 dates, pitted
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger root, chopped (adjust to your taste)
  • juice of 1 meyer lemon
  • 2 handfuls organic spinach
  • 1 tablespoon tahini
  • 1 cup filtered water

Preparation

  • Simply add all ingredients into a high speed blender and blend until thick and creamy.You may add ice if you'd like to chill further or thicken slightly.Enjoy!
