Tropical Carrot Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 2¼ cups carrot juice
  • 1-2 kiwi fruits, peeled and sliced
  • 1½ cups frozen mango
  • 1½ cups frozen pineapple

Preparation

  • Pour the carrot juice into a blender.
  • Add the pineapple, mango and kiwi and process until smooth.
  • Serve immediately.
