Kale Salad with Citrus, Avocado, and Feta

Ingredients

  • 1 large avocado, pit removed and sliced
  • 2 small blood oranges, peeled and sliced into rounds
  • 1/3 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1 large grapefruit, peeled and cut into segments
  • 1 bunch kale, stalks removed and discarded, leaves chopped
  • Pinch of Kosher salt
  • 1/8 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon orange zest

Preparation

  • Place kale in a large bowl. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, orange zest, fresh orange juice, and salt.
  • Pour mixture over kale and massage the kale until it starts to soften, about 2-3 minutes.
  • Add grapefruit segments, orange slices, avocado slices, and feta cheese. Toss and serve. Note-any kind of oranges will work in this salad.
