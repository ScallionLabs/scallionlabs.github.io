German-Style Warm Potato Salad

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. baby red potatoes, cut into 1- x ½-inch pieces
  • 4 cups loosely packed baby spinach leaves
  • 2 Tbs. chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 Tbs. chopped fresh tarragon or dill
  • 4 oz. green beans, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 3 green onions, white and pale-green parts chopped (¼ cup)
  • 3 Tbs. olive oil
  • 2 Tbs. white balsamic vinegar

Preparation

  • Cook potatoes in pot of boiling salted water 8 minutes, or until tender.
  • Add green beans during last minute of cooking.
  • Meanwhile, heat oil in skillet over medium heat.
  • Add green onions. Cook 3 to 4 minutes, or until tender but not browned.
  • Remove from heat, and stir in vinegar.
  • Drain potatoes and green beans. Toss with olive oil mixture, parsley, and tarragon. Season with salt and pepper, if desired.
  • Serve warm on bed of spinach.
