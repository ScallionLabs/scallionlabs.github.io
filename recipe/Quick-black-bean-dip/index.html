Quick black bean dip

Ingredients

  • 1x 400g tin black beans, drained (240g when drained)
  • Black pepper
  • 2tbsp fresh coriander
  • 1 small clove garlic
  • 1tbsp your favourite hot sauce (you might want to adjust this depending on how hot your sauce is)
  • Salt

Preparation

  • Blitz the garlic and coriander in a small food processor, and then add the black beans and hot sauce. Season generously, and blitz again until smooth. Top with fresh chopped coriander to serve, if desired.
