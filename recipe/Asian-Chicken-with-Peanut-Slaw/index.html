Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup prepared GOOD SEASONS Asian Sesame Dressing Mix, divided
  • 1 carrot, shredded
  • 2 cups finely shredded green cabbage
  • 1/2 cup PLANTERS COCKTAIL Peanuts
  • 4 small boneless skinless chicken breast halves (1 lb.)

Preparation

  • Preheat greased grill to medium-high heat. Grill chicken 10 to 12 min. or until cooked through (165F), turning after 5 min. and brushing occasionally with 1/4 cup dressing.
  • Toss cabbage with carrots, peanuts and remaining dressing.
  • Serve with the chicken.
