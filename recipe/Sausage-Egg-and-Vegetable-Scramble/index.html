Sausage Egg and Vegetable Scramble

Ingredients

  • 8 eggs, beaten
  • 1 lb ground sausage
  • 2 cups fresh chopped veggies, such as red onions, peppers, tomatoes, green onions
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Preparation

  • Light a grill or stovetop over medium heat.
  • Add sausage and cook until no longer pink. Create a pool in the center and add eggs. Cook, stirring, until eggs are cooked through.
