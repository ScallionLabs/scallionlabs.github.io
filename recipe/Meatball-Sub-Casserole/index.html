Meatball Sub Casserole

Ingredients

  • 24 ounce jar marinara
  • 1/2 package Farm Rich Italian Style Meatballs (about 25 meatballs), thawed
  • 1 package Farm Rich Mozzarella Bites
  • 1/2 cup shredded Mozzarella cheese

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  • Place Mozzarella Bites in the bottom of a 9x13 pan.Top with meatballs and marinara.
  • Sprinkle cheese on top.
  • Bake for 15 minutes or until heated through and cheese has melted.
