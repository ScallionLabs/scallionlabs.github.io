Ingredients

  • 10 ozs Crimini or baby Portobello mushrooms

  • 1 clove of garlic minced
  • 1 cup softened goat cheese
  • 2 Tbsps olive oil
  • ¼ cup plain Panko crumbs (don't have Panko, use bread crumbs)
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp freshly cracked pepper for cheese mixture, ½ tsp pepper for topping

  • 1 tsps salt for the cheese mixture, ½ tsp salt for topping
