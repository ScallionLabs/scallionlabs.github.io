Guac-kale-mole

Ingredients

  • 4 medium avocados
  • 3/4 cup cilantro, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 jalapeno, cored and finely chopped
  • juice of 2 small limes
  • 5 kale leaves, destemmed and finely chopped
  • 1 roma tomato, finely chopped (about 1/2 cup)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Preparation

  • I like to use a food processor to help finely chop everything as it saves a lot of time.
  • Place the garlic into the processor first and pulse until minced. Then add the cilantro, jalapeno and the onion and pulse until finely chopped.Next add the flesh from the avocado along with the lime juice. I used juice from 1 and 1/2 limes but you can use less or more, depending on your preferences. Blend until smooth then place into a medium size bowl.Roughly chop the kale leaves then add them to the processor and pulse until finely chopped.
  • Place it in the bowl with the avocado mixture and add the chopped tomato to the bowl as well.Stir until everything is well-combined then serve.To keep it from turning brown, lay a piece of plastic wrap over top and gently pat it down so that there isn’t any air between the the wrap and the guacamole. Seal with an airtight lid and store in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.
