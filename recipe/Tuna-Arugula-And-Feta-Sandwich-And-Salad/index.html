Tuna, Arugula And Feta Sandwich And Salad

  • http://www.self.com/fooddiet/recipes/2007/06/tuna-arugula-and-feta-sandwich
  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 2min
  • Tags:
    Pescetarian Pescatarian
  • High in: Selenium K

Minerals RDA %

Macros

Ingredients

  • 16 cups arugula
  • 4 tsps balsamic vinegar
  • 20 small black olives, chopped
  • 4 oz feta, crumbled
  • 4 cans (3 oz each) chunk-light tuna packed in water (drained)
  • 8 thick slices whole-grain bread (about 80 calories per slice)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 4 medium tomatoes, sliced

Preparation

  • Combine oil and vinegar.
  • Mix tuna in a bowl with 4 tsp dressing, feta and olives.
  • Layer 1 cup arugula, 2 slices of the tomato and tuna mixture on each of 4 slices of bread. In a bowl, toss 12 cups arugula with remaining tomatoes and dressing.
  • Serve with sandwich.
