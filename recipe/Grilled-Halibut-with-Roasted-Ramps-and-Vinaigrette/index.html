Grilled Halibut with Roasted Ramps and Vinaigrette

Ingredients

  • 3/4 Lb Halibut (or other white fish)
  • Juice of 1 Lemon
  • Zest of 1 Lemon
  • 1/4 C Olive Oil
  • 1 TBSP Parsley
  • Small Bunch of Ramps
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 1 Ramp, Minced (Up to leaf)

Preparation

  • Season fish with salt and pepper and place in aluminum foil.
  • Heat grill to medium low.Rinse and dry ramps. Preheat oven to 35
  • Combine lemon zest, juice, parsley, oil and minced ramp.Toss 2 TBSP of vinaigrette with ramps, place in roasting pan and bake 15 minutes, stirring once.
  • Place fish in open foil pack on top rack of grill, cook on low for 8-10 minutes.
  • Serve halibut topped with ramps and drizzled with vinaigrette.
