20+ Heart Healthy s and Walnut Crusted Seared Wasabi Salmon with Warm Napa Cabbage Salad {Gluten-Free and Heart Healthy 2013}

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1/4 cup carrot, thinly sliced (I used a peeler)
  • 1/2 cup steamed brown rice
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger, minced
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1 cup Napa cabbage
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1/4 cup red bell pepper, cut into slivers
  • 1 dried red hot chile pepper
  • 1 teaspoon rice wine
  • 3 ounces fresh salmon (sushi grade)
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon gluten-free low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 ounce walnuts, raw
  • 1/8 teaspoon wasabi powder

Preparation

  • Mix together wasabi powder and soy sauce in a small bowl. Rub all over salmon.
  • Let marinade for 5-10 minutes.
  • Place walnuts in a small sandwich bag and pound into smaller pieces using a rolling pin. You can do this in a mortar and pestle or food processor (pulse it).
  • Roll all sides of salmon in crushed walnuts.
  • Heat olive oil in a cast iron skillet. Sear walnut crusted salmon until desired doneness.
  • Remove from pan. Slice crosswise into pieces.In a small bowl, mix together soy sauce, vinegar and honey.
  • Heat sesame oil in a saute pan.
  • Add chile pepper and ginger.
  • Saute 10-15 seconds, and add cabbage, carrot and red pepper.
  • Saute a minute.
  • Add rice wine and saute another minute. Turn off heat and stir in soy sauce mixture. Toss.
  • Place warm brown rice in a bowl or on a plate. Arrange Warm Napa Cabbage Salad alongside rice. Gently place slices of Walnut Crusted Seared Wasabi Salmon next to salad.
