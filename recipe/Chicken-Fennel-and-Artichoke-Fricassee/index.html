Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons fresh flat-leaf parsley, coarsely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 small red onion, cut into 1/2-inch wedges

  • 1 whole chicken (about 4 pounds), cut into 10 pieces
