The Ultimate Parmesan Roasted Broccoli

Ingredients

  • ½ tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 big heads of broccoli or 3 smaller ones
  • 5 garlic cloves, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 2 to 3 tbsp olive oil
  • ¼ cup roasted pecans, chopped
  • 1½ tsp salt

Preparation

  • Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F.
  • Cut the broccoli florets from the thick stalks and cut the larger pieces through the base of the head with a small knife, pulling the florets apart. I cut mine in really small florets because I wanted bite size florets.
  • Place the broccoli florets on a sheet pan large enough to hold them in a single layer. Toss the garlic on the broccoli and drizzle with olive oil.
  • Sprinkle with the salt and pepper. Roast for 20 to 25 minutes, until crisp-tender and the tips of some of the florets are browned.
  • Remove the broccoli from the oven and immediately toss with lemon juice, pecans and Parmesan cheese.
  • Serve hot.
