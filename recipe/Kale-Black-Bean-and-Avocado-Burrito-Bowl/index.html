Kale, Black Bean and Avocado Burrito Bowl

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado, pitted and sliced into big chunks
  • 2 cans black black beans, rinsed and drained (or 4 cups cooked black beans)
  • 1 cup brown rice, rinsed (short grain/arborio or long grain/basmati recommended)
  • cherry tomatoes, sliced into thin rounds
  • ¼ teaspoon chili powder
  • ½ teaspoon cumin
  • ½ cup fresh cilantro leaves (3 stems are ok)
  • 3 cloves garlic, pressed or minced
  • ½ jalapeño, seeded and finely chopped
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 bunch curly kale, ribs removed and chopped into small, bite-sized pieces
  • ¼ cup lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ cup mild salsa verde (any good green salsa will do)
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

Preparation

  • Cook the rice: bring a big pot of water to a boil, dump in rinsed brown rice and boil, uncovered, for 30 minutes. Turn off the heat, drain the rice and return it to the pot. Cover and let the rice steam in the pot for 10 minutes, then fluff the rice with a fork and season with ¼ teaspoon salt, or more to taste.Make the kale salad: whisk together the lime juice, olive oil, chopped jalapeño, cumin and salt. Toss the chopped kale with the lime marinade in a mixing bowl.Make the avocado salsa verde: in a food processor or blender, combine the avocado chunks, salsa verde, cilantro and lime juice and blend well.Warm the beans: in a sauce pan, warm 1 tablespoon olive oil over medium-low heat. Sauté the shallot and garlic until fragrant, then add the beans, chili powder and cayenne pepper. Cook until the beans are warmed through and softened, stirring often, about 5 to 7 minutes. If the beans seem dry at any point, mix in a little splash of water.To serve, spoon generous portions of rice, beans and kale salad into a bowl along with a couple spoonfuls of avocado salsa verde.
  • Garnish with chopped cherry tomatoes.
