Pasta Salad with Tomatoes, Kalamata Olives and Toasted Pine Nuts

  • Servings: 8
  • Ready in: 23min
  • Preparation time: 15min
  • Cooking time: 8min

  • High in: Manganese K

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup balsamic vinegar
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes - sliced in half
  • 1 cup fresh herbs (ie parsley, basil, dill, chives, etc) - minced
  • ½ cup kalamata olives - pitted and minced
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • ¼ teaspoon pepper
  • ½ cup pine nuts - toasted
  • ½ cup red onion - minced
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ cup sun-dried tomatoes - minced
  • 1 lb whole wheat pasta (such as rotini, penne, etc.)

Preparation

  • Cook pasta in salty water until al dente - usually about one minute less than the package suggests - and set aside.In a large bowl, toss together cherry tomatoes, kalamata olives, red onion, sun-dried tomatoes, fresh herbs and toasted pine nuts.
  • Add reserved pasta to the bowl and drizzle with olive oil, balsamic, salt and pepper. Toss well, check for seasoning and adjust accordingly.
