Endive Petals with Rosemary Chevre

Ingredients

  • 32 small red or green endive leaves (about 4 heads endive)
  • 4 tsp. finely chopped fresh rosemary
  • 8 oz. fresh chèvre or goat cheese (2 4-oz. logs)
  • 2 Tbs. olive oil
  • ½ tsp. cracked black pepper
  • 2 small shallots, finely chopped (¼ cup)
  • 32 candied pecan or walnut halves

Preparation

  • Mash chevre in bowl until chunky. Stir in shallots, oil, rosemary, and pepper, and season with salt, if desired.
  • Spoon 2 tsp. chevre mixture on widest end of each endive leaf, and top each with 1 candied pecan. Set leaves in circular pattern on serving platter.
