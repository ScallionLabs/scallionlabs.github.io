Hawaiian Burgers

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro
  • 4 slices cooked bacon
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 4 slices havarti cheese
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, sliced into rings
  • 4 pineapple rings
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons teriyaki sauce

Preparation

  • To Cook the Burgers and Toppings:In a bowl, mix by hand the ground pork, ground beef, garlic, ginger, salt, pepper and cilantro. Shape pork mixture into 4 patties.
  • Heat an outdoor grill or a grill pan to medium heat.
  • Place patties on grill and cook for 5-8 minutes per side or until done.
  • Place a slice of Havarti cheese on each patty during the last minute or two of cooking to allow cheese to melt.
  • Add pineapple rings to the hot grates and cook rings on each side just until pineapple begins to soften.While patties are grilling, In a large skillet, place onion rings in hot oil over medium heat. Cook onions until they are soft and caramelized, about 10 minutes.
  • Add teriyaki sauce; stir.To Make the Garlic Mayo:In a small bowl, combine mayo and garlic; add pepper to taste.To Assemble Burgers: Divide garlic mayo between the hamburger buns.
  • Place one hamburger patty on the bottom of a hamburger bun.Top with cooked bacon and one pineapple ring.Divide onions among burgers, top with remaining bun half and serve.
