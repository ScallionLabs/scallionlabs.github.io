Fall Minestrone Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 cups cubed peeled butternut squash (¾- to 1-inch cubes)
  • 1 cup carrot, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • 1 Tbs. chopped fresh sage, or 2 tsp. dried sage
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced (4 tsp.)
  • 1 cup chopped green pepper
  • ½ tsp. Italian seasoning
  • 1 cup chopped kale
  • 1 cup sliced leeks
  • 2 Tbs. olive oil
  • 1 cup chopped red bell pepper
  • 1 tsp. salt, optional
  • 2 large tomatoes, peeled and coarsely chopped (1 cup)
  • 1 cup diced zucchini
  • 1 15-oz. can or 1½ cups cooked white beans, rinsed and drained

Preparation

  • Heat oil in 5-qt. Dutch oven or other large soup pot over medium heat.
  • Add leeks, carrots, celery, bell pepper, green pepper, zucchini and salt, if using. Cover, and cook 6 to 8 minutes, or until veggies are softened, stirring occasionally. Uncover, and continue cooking 5 to 6 minutes more.
  • Add garlic, sage, Italian seasoning, and bay leaf. Stir 30 seconds, or until fragrant.
  • Add tomatoes and 8 cups water, and bring to a boil. Season with salt and pepper, if desired. Reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer 20 minutes.
  • Add squash, cover, and simmer 10 minutes.
  • Add white beans, and chopped kale; simmer 10 minutes more, stirring occasionally.
