Ingredients

  • 1 large bok choy, leaves separated
  • 1 clove garlic, minced (1 tsp.)
  • 2 green onions, chopped (¼ cup)
  • ¼ red kuri or kabocha squash, seeded and cut into ½-inch-thick slices
  • ½ tsp. light brown sugar
  • 1 Tbs. miso paste
  • 1 small red onion, sliced
  • ½ tsp. rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp. sesame oil
  • 2 tsp. sesame oil
  • 1 Tbs. toasted sesame seeds
  • 1 large bok choy, leaves separated
  • 1 clove garlic, minced (1 tsp.)
  • 2 green onions, chopped (¼ cup)
  • ¼ red kuri or kabocha squash, seeded and cut into ½-inch-thick slices
  • 1 Tbs. miso paste
  • 1 small red onion, sliced
  • ½ tsp. rice vinegar
  • 1 Tbs. toasted sesame seeds

Preparation

To make Vegetables
  • Brush squash and onion slices with oil. Spray grill with cooking spray, and lay squash slices on grill. Close, and cook on medium-high 10 minutes, or until squash is tender; transfer to plate.
  • Place onion slices on grill; cook 4 to 5 minutes, or until crisp-tender; transfer to plate.
  • Place bok choy leaves on grill; cook 3 to 4 minutes, or until wilted and crisp-tender; transfer to plate.
Meanwhile, to make Miso Sauce
  • Combine miso paste, garlic, brown sugar, and 1/4 cup water in small saucepan. Bring to a simmer over medium heat. Cook 1 minute, or until miso dissolves and begins to bloom.
  • Remove from heat; stir in oil and vinegar. Fold in green onions and sesame seeds.
  • Serve Grilled Vegetables drizzled with Miso Sauce, or serve Miso Sauce on the side.
