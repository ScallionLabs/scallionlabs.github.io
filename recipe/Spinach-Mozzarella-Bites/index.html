Spinach Mozzarella Bites

Ingredients

  • 1 pkg. (10 oz.) frozen chopped spinach, thawed, well drained
  • 1 clove garlic, peeled
  • lower-sodium stoned wheat crackers
  • 2 Tbsp. olive oil
  • 1/3 cup KRAFT Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 cup KRAFT Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped PLANTERS Walnuts, divided

Preparation

  • Heat oven to 350F.
  • Process spinach, Parmesan cheese, 2 Tbsp. nuts and garlic in food processor or blender until blended.
  • Add oil in steady stream to spinach mixture through pour spout of food processor container, processing continuously until mixture is well blended. Spoon into 2- to 3-cup baking dish; stir in mozzarella cheese and remaining nuts.
  • Bake 15 min., stirring after 8 min.
  • Serve on crackers.
