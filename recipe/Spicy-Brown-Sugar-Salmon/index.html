Spicy Brown Sugar Salmon

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup loosely packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 2 pound wild-caught, fresh salmon filet (about 1-inch in thickness)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika

Preparation

  • In a bowl, combine brown sugar, paprika, garlic powder, salt, pepper and red pepper flakes, mixing well.
  • Heat the broiler on your oven to high, and place the oven rack as high as it will go.
  • Place salmon filet on a baking sheet and rub brown sugar mixture all over top. I tend to buy a piece of salmon with skin on, so I place the salmon skin side down on the baking sheet and just rub the spices on top.
  • Place under the broiler and broil for 5-6 minutes, until sugar begins to caramelize and get golden.
  • Serve immediately! It is pretty important to use a piece of salmon that is no thicker than one inch - if your salmon is thicker, I suggest flipping the salmon after 5 minutes and broiling for another 2-3 (this requires that the skin be removed) OR lowering your oven rack one level down - this will prevent the sugar from burning since you have to broil longer. After the initial 5 minutes, the sugar may be a bit smokey but it should not burn.
