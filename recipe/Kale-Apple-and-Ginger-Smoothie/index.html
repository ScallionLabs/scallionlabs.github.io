Preparation

  • Freeze the apple, cucumber, lemon, and ginger.
  • Add the frozen mixture to a high-powered blender along with the kale and chia seeds (if using).
  • Add enough water to get it moving and blend on high.
  • Add additional water to reach desired consistency.
  • Serve right away.
