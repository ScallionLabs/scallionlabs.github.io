Sesame-Seasoned Spinach

  • Ready in: 30min
  • Preparation time: 20min
  • Cooking time: 10min

Ingredients

  • 1 pound baby spinach (about 20 cups)
  • 1 small clove garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons toasted sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds (see Tip)
  • 1/4 cup water

Preparation

  • Place water in a very large pot or Dutch oven and bring to a boil over high heat.
  • Add spinach and cook, stirring frequently, until completely wilted, 2 to 3 minutes.
  • Transfer to a colander and let stand until cool enough to handle. Squeeze out excess water.Coarsely chop the spinach.
  • Place in a bowl and mix in sesame seeds, soy sauce, sesame oil and garlic.
  • Serve at room temperature.
