Spinach Artichoke Hummus with Shamrock Shaped Green Peppers

Ingredients

  • ½ cup artichokes (drained from can – reserve ½ cup of liquid)
  • 15.5 oz can garbanzo beans (chickpeas) – drained & rinsed
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 4-8 green bell peppers
  • 1 lemon (juiced)
  • ¼ cup Enzo Olive Oil
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 2 tbsp tahini

Preparation

  • Add the garbanzo beans, garlic, tahini, spinach and artichokes to a food processor.Juice the lemon into the food processor then drizzle the olive oil through the top as you blend.Blend for 30 seconds or until the hummus is smooth and creamy.
  • Place in a bowl and refrigerate for 10-15 minutes.
  • Remove the tops of each bell pepper and clean the seeds and membranes out of the middle.Use the palm of your hand to gently smash the bell pepper flat.Slice the bell pepper in half, then use a shamrock shaped cookie cutter to cut the bell pepper.
