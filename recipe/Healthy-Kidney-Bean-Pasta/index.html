Healthy Kidney Bean Pasta

  • http://mywholefoodlife.com/2016/03/23/healthy-kidney-bean-pasta/
  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 15min
  • Preparation time: 5min
  • Cooking time: 10min
  • Tags:
    Vegetarian Lunch Main Course Main Dish Dinner Dairy Free Lacto Ovo Vegetarian Vegan

Ingredients

  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups kidney beans
  • 1 T olive oil
  • 1 cup peas
  • 2 cups cooked gluten free penne pasta (I used brown rice pasta)
  • Sea salt to taste
  • 1 T white wine vinegar
  • 1 medium yellow onion, diced

Preparation

  • Cook the brown rice pasta, drain the water and cover. Set aside.In a saute pan, saute the onion, garlic, vinegar, and olive oil for about 5 minutes, or until mostly softened.
  • Add the peas and kidney beans and continue to saute for another 2-3 minutes, stirring constantly.Lastly, add the pasta and mix well.
  • Remove from heat and serve immediately.
