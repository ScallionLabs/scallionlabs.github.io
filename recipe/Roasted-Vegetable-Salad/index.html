Roasted Vegetable Salad

  • Ready in: 50min
  • Preparation time: 10min
  • Cooking time: 40min

Ingredients

  • 1-pound asparagus, ends trimmed
  • 1-pound large brussels sprouts, quartered
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons smooth dijon mustard
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • freshly ground peppercorns, to taste
  • 2 red bell peppers, julienned
  • 3 cups roughly chopped red cabbage
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • salt, to taste

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 400.Line two baking sheets with foil; set aside.
  • Place the vegetables in a large mixing bowl and toss with the olive oil, salt, and pepper, to evenly coat.
  • Spread the vegetables on baking sheets in one layer.
  • Bake for 35 to 40 minutes, or until tender, stirring occasionally for even cooking.In the meantime, prepare the dressing by whisking together all ingredients in a mixing bowl; mix until thoroughly combined.
  • Remove vegetables from oven and let cool for 5 minutes.
  • Drizzle with prepared dressing.
  • Serve.
