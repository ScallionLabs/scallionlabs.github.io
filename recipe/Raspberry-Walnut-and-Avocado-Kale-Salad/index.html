Raspberry, Walnut & Avocado Kale Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado, cubed
  • 1 tsp chia seeds
  • 1 Medjool date, pitted
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 cup frozen red grapes
  • 2 tbsp hemp hearts (hulled hemp seeds)
  • 1 bunch curly kale, destemmed and chopped
  • 1 tsp pure maple syrup (or honey)
  • 1 heaping tbsp peanut butter
  • Freshly ground pepper, to taste
  • 1-6oz container raspberries
  • ½ red onion, sliced thinly
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 handful spinach
  • ½ tsp vanilla
  • ½ cup walnuts, chopped
  • ½ cup cold water
  • 2 tbsp champagne wine vinegar (or apple cider vinegar)

Preparation

  • Place chopped kale in a large bowl.
  • Sprinkle with salt and massage salt into leaves with hands until they turn a bit darker in color.
  • Whisk together the dressing ingredients.
  • Pour over top of salad and massage dressing into leaves for about a minute.
  • Add red onion, walnuts, avocado, raspberries and feta. Toss to combine.
  • Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Place all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.
