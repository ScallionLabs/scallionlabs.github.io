Almond Crusted Salmon

  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 20min
  • Preparation time: 10min
  • Cooking time: 10min
  • Tags:

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup sliced Diamond Almonds, roughly chopped
  • 2 tablespoons chopped basil
  • zest of 1/2 lemon
  • sliced lemons, for serving
  • 1/4 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  • 4 salmon fillets (6-8 oz each)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Preparation

  • Preheat the oven to 400F. Line a baking pan with foil and spray lightly with nonstick cooking spray.In a small bowl, combine the almonds, panko, basil, lemon zest, salt and pepper.
  • Place the salmon fillets on the prepared baking sheet. Divide the almond mixture between the 4 fillets and sprinkle over the tops. Lightly press the breading into the fish.
  • Bake until desired doneness, 8-10 minutes.
  • Serve with additional lemon slices for squeezing.
