Salsa Chickpea Lettuce Wraps

Ingredients

  • 15 ounces chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 whole green bell pepper, seeded and diced
  • 10 whole lettuce leaves
  • 1 whole red bell pepper, seeded and diced
  • 12 ounces salsa

Preparation

  • Combine chickpeas and salsa in a medium sauce pan. Cook over medium-high heat, stirring occasionally, until all of the liquid has absorbed and the chickpeas have taken on the salsa’s color, about 8 minutes. For best results, refrigerate over night. Spoon chickpeas into lettuce leaves and top with chopped bell pepper pieces.
  • Serve chickpeas at any temperature. (Makes about 8 wraps)Nutritional Information
