Italian Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 2 cups hot cooked rice
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped roasted sweet red peppers
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 8 cups fresh spinach (about 10 ounces), chopped

Preparation

  • In a large nonstick skillet, saute garlic in oil for 1 minute. Stir in spinach. Cover and cook for 3-4 minutes or until tender; drain well.
  • Add the vinegar, salt and pepper. Stir in the rice and red peppers until combined. Cook and stir until heated through.
