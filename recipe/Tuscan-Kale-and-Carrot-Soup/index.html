Tuscan Kale and Carrot Soup

  • Servings: 8
  • Ready in: 45min
  • Tags:

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbs. balsamic vinegar
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh basil, plus 6 whole leaves, divided
  • 1 15-oz. can diced tomatoes in juice
  • 1 15-oz. can white beans, rinsed and drained
  • 2 bunches multicolored carrots, cut into 1-inch chunks (4 cups)
  • 2 Tbs. chopped chives, plus handful whole chives, divided
  • 2 12-oz. bunches Tuscan kale, thinly sliced
  • 4 cups low-sodium vegetable broth

Preparation

  • Bring carrots, broth, tomatoes with juice, and 2 cups water to a simmer in large stockpot with steamer insert over medium heat.
  • Place kale, whole basil leaves, and whole chives in steamer insert. Set steamer inside stockpot, cover, and steam 5 to 7 minutes, using tongs to stir halfway through.
  • Remove and discard basil and chives.
  • Add kale, beans, and vinegar to soup, and return to a simmer. Season with salt and pepper, if desired. Stir in chopped basil and chives just before serving.
