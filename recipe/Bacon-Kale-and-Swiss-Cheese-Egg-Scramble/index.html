Bacon, Kale and Swiss Cheese Egg Scramble

Ingredients

  • 5 eggs
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 2 tablespoons real bacon bits
  • 2 whole green onions (chopped (including the green tops))
  • 1 cup (heaping) chopped kale
  • Â½ cup chopped Swiss cheese
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Â¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 dashes salt (or salt to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon real bacon bits
  • 2 teaspoons chopped green onion

Preparation

For the Scramble
  • In a medium size bowl add eggs, milk, pepper, and salt.
  • Whisk together and set aside for a moment.
  • Heat a medium size skillet on medium heat and add butter.
  • To the pan, add real bacon bits, green onions, and kale. Stir together and cook until the kale starts to slightly wilt (about 4 minutes).
  • To the pan, add the egg mixture and stir.
  • Gently move the eggs around with a spoon or spatula as theyre cooking.
  • Continue to cook the eggs and stir together with the other ingredients. Just before the eggs are done cooking (about 3 to 4 minutes), add the cheese.
  • Stir in the cheese with the egg for about 2 minutes or until it melts and the eggs are thoroughly cooked.

Garnish
  • Garnish with bacon bits and chopped green onions.
  • Serve right away while warm.
