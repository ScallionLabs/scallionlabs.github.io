Pork Lettuce Wraps

Ingredients

  • 1 small carrot, diced
  • 1 stalk celery, julienned
  • 1 teaspoon fish sauce (or 1 tablespoon oyster sauce)
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 1 pound lean ground pork or turkey
  • 1 jalapeño, seeded and diced
  • lettuce leaves
  • lime wedges
  • 1 teaspoon oil
  • ½ cup frozen peas
  • ½ cup diced red onion
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric

Preparation

  • Heat the oil in a large skillet and add the pork, onion, garlic and jalapeño.
  • Saute over medium-high heat, stirring constantly to break up the pork, until pork is mostly cooked and in small pieces.
  • Add turmeric, oyster sauce and soy sauce and stir to combine.
  • Add celery, peas and carrots and cook until pork is no longer pink and celery is wilted, about 2-3 more minutes.
  • Serve in lettuce leaves with whatever fresh toppings you like
