White Bean, Lentil and Kale Wraps

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked lentils
  • Juice from 1 lemon
  • 4 leaves curly kale, stems removed, chopped and massaged (see this video for massaging kale)
  • 2 tsp maple syrup
  • ½ cup red onions, thinly sliced
  • 2-3 Roma tomatoes, chopped
  • Sea salt to taste
  • ½ cup tahini
  • 4-5 soft tortilla wraps
  • Water (if needed, to reach desired consistency)
  • 2 cups cooked white beans OR 1 can white beans drained and rinsed

Preparation

  • Place all the salad ingredients in a large bowl.
  • Mix well.In a small bowl, add all the dressing ingredients.
  • Mix well.
  • Pour dressing over salad and mix until everything is incorporated.Spoon about 1 cup of the mixture onto each tortilla, wrap and serve
