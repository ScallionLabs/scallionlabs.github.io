Shaved Brussels Sprouts and Soba Noodles with Ginger-Honey Sauce

  • http://naturallyella.com/2012/12/12/shaved-brussels-sprouts-and-soba-noodles-with-ginger-honey-sauce/
  • Servings: 2
  • Ready in: 15min
  • Preparation time: 5min
  • Cooking time: 10min
  • Tags:
    Vegetarian Side Dish Dairy Free Lacto Ovo Vegetarian
  • High in: K C

Ingredients

  • ½ lb Brussels Sprouts
  • 1" chunk ginger, peeled
  • 1-2 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1-2 tablespoon rice wine vinegar
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 teaspoons sesame oil
  • 1 medium shallot
  • 4 oz soba noodles

Preparation

  • In a food processor attached with a slicing blade, add Brussels a couple at a time. Next, add shallot and continue to slice.
  • Heat olive oil in over medium heat and add shallot/Brussel Sprout mixture. Cook until Brussels Sprouts are tender.
  • Add ginger to food processor and pulse until ginger is in fine pieces.
  • Add oil, 1 tablespoon vinegar, 1 tablespoon honey, and salt, pulsing until combined. Taste and add more vinegar or honey to taste.
  • Add to Brussel Sprouts and cook for 1-2 minutes.Bring a pot of water to a boil, add soba noodles and cook until tender, 6-8 minutes.
  • Drain and add to Brussels Sprouts.
  • Serve with toasted sesame seeds.
