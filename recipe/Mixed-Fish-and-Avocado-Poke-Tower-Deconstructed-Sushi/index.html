Preparation

  • Mix all Poke ingredients except for avocado together in a medium bowl.Use a round biscuit cutter to mold poke. Spoon avocado inside biscuit cutter. Press down gently. Spoon poke on top and press gently. Carefully lift biscuit cutter. Depending on the size of your biscuit cutter, this recipe will probably make two towers.
  • Serve with toasted seaweed wrappers and steamed rice. Each person can spoon a little rice on top of the seaweed sheet, and top with some poke. Use your fingers to eat.
