Preparation

  • Start by boiling a large pot of water. Smash them into the mixture with a fork without turning them into a puree.Next, add the cooked quinoa to the mix.Then add the chopped onion, red pepper, jalapeÃ±o, garlic and the remaining whole black beans.Finally, add the spices, cumin, chipotle pepper and cilantro, as well as the binding ingredients including the rice chex and egg.
  • Mix together, then use your hands to form into 8 patties.
