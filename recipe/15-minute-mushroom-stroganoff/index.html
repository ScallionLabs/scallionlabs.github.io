15 minute mushroom stroganoff

  • http://www.amuse-your-bouche.com/15-minute-mushroom-stroganoff/
  • Servings: 2
  • Ready in: 15min
  • Preparation time: 5min
  • Cooking time: 10min
  • Tags:
    Vegetarian Side Dish Gluten Free Lacto Ovo Vegetarian
  • High in: C K

Macros

Ingredients

  • Black pepper
  • 4tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 500g mushrooms, sliced or diced
  • 1tbsp oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 1tsp smoked paprika
  • 3tbsp sour cream (I used reduced fat)
  • 50ml vegetable stock

Preparation

  • Heat the oil in a large frying pan, and cook the onion, garlic and mushrooms over a medium heat for 5-10 minutes, until slightly softened.
  • Add the vegetable stock and smoked paprika, and season well with black pepper (and a little salt if you used low sodium stock). Cook for a further 5 minutes.Just before serving, stir in the sour cream and half of the parsley, and once heated through, serve topped with the remaining parsley. I served mine alongside a wild rice mixture.
