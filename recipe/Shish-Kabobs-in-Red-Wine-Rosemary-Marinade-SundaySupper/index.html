Preparation

  • Mix marinade ingredients together.
  • Add beef and marinate for at least 2 hours and up to 24 hours.
  • Remove beef from marinade, discarding excess marinade.Assemble shish kabobs by alternating meat cubes and vegetables on a skewer. 3 pieces of meat fit nicely on a 12 inch skewer. Grill over medium heat for about 3 minutes on each side. Use an oven mitt to turn the skewers.
