Soppressata Bundles with Radicchio and Goat Cheese

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces fresh goat cheese, softened
  • 1 teaspoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 5 peperoncini, stemmed, seeded and chopped
  • 3 tablespoons pine nuts, toasted
  • 1 head (small) radicchio, finely shredded
  • 1 teaspoon red wine vinegar
  • 4 ounces (24 thin slices) soppressata

Preparation

  • In a bowl, toss the radicchio with the peperoncini, goat cheese, vinegar, oil and pine nuts. Arrange the soppressata on a work surface and mound the radicchio salad on the sliced soppressata.
  • Roll up like a cone and serve.
