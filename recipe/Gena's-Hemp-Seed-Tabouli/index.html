Gena’s Hemp Seed Tabouli

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 1 cup fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons hemp oil (I used olive oil)
  • 1 cup shelled hemp seeds
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt (I used 1/4 + 1/8 teaspoon)
  • 4 medium yellow vine or Jersey tomatoes, chopped (I used 2-3 large red tomatoes)

Preparation

  • In a food processor fitted with the “S” blade, process the parsley, mint, and sea salt until minced.
  • Transfer the herbs and salt to a large mixing bowl.
  • Add the tomatoes, hemp seeds, hemp oil, and lemon juice.
  • Mix well, and serve.Stored in an airtight container in the fridge, the tabouli will keep for 2 days.
