Paleo Mustard Glazed Chicken

Ingredients

  • black pepper
  • 8 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • ¼ cup coconut oil or butter, melted
  • ½ tsp. dried sage
  • ½ tsp. sea salt
  • 2 T. of stone ground mustard (gluten free)

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 425 degrees.In small mixing bowl, combine melted coconut oil, mustard, black pepper, salt, and sage.
  • Place chicken thighs on a oven-safe dish, and brush the mustard mixture evenly over each one.
  • Bake for 45 minutes or until temperature of chicken is 165 degrees. (insert thermometer into center of chicken thigh)
