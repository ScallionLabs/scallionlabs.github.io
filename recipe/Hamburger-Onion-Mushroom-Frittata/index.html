Hamburger Onion Mushroom Frittata

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • 3 eggs
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ¼ pound ground beef
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ½ cup shredded gruyere (or gouda or swiss)
  • 4 ounces mushroom, sliced or quartered
  • ½ tbsp olive oil
  • ½ medium onion, chopped
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt

Preparation

  • In a small bowl beat eggs together with parsley, garlic powder, salt and pepper. Set aside.In an oven-safe skillet brown ground beef over medium heat.
  • Remove browned meat, set aside. Leave beef fat in the pan.
  • Saute onions and mushrooms over medium heat in remaining beef fat until softened, about 8-10 minutes.
  • Remove onions and mushrooms, set aside.Adjust oven rack to middle and preheat broiler to 500-degrees F.
  • Add olive oil to skillet and swirl to coat. Evenly spread browned ground beef, onions and mushrooms over skillet.
  • Pour beaten egg mixture oven top; swirl skillet to evenly distribute beaten eggs. Cook over medium heat about 2 minutes.
  • Sprinkle cheese evenly over top. Turn burner off and transfer skillet to oven.Cook under broiler for 1-2 minutes until eggs are done.
  • Remove from oven and carefully transfer omelet to plate to serve, folding omelet in half if desired.
