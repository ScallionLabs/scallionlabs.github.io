Ingredients

  • 1 beef flank steak (1 lb.), grilled, sliced
  • 1/2 cup KRAFT Natural 2% Milk Colby & Monterey Jack Cheese Crumbles
  • 1/2 cup KRAFT Lite Zesty Italian Dressing
  • 2 small plum tomatoes, sliced
  • 5 cups tightly packed torn romaine lettuce
  • 1 yellow pepper, halved, grilled and cut into strips

Preparation

  • Cover 4 salad plates with lettuce.
  • Top with vegetables, meat and cheese.
  • Drizzle with dressing just before serving.
