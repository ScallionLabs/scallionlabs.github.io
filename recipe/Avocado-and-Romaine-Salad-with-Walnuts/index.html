Avocado and Romaine Salad with Walnuts

Ingredients

  • 2 to 3 avocados, thinly sliced
  • 2 Tablespoons/ 30ml white wine or champagne vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
  • 4 Tablespoons/ 60ml extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoon fresh chives, finely chopped
  • 3 romaine hearts (1 pound), torn into bite-size pieces
  • Sea salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
  • 2 teaspoons fresh shallots, finely minced
  • ¼ cup/ 28g walnuts, lightly toasted and coarsely chopped

Preparation

  • In a large bowl, whisk the vinegar with the shallots and mustard.Gradually whisk in the olive oil until incorporated and season to taste with salt and pepper.
  • Add the romaine, chives and sliced avocados, toss well.Top with toasted walnuts and serve.
