Yellow Split Pea Dal

  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 45min

Ingredients

  • ½ cup finely chopped carrots
  • ½ cup cilantro leaves, for garnish, optional
  • 2 Tbs. coconut oil
  • 1 tsp. cumin seeds
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced (2 Tbs.)
  • 1 lemon, juiced (2 Tbs.)
  • 2 tsp. red pepper flakes, plus more for garnish
  • 1 tsp. turmeric powder
  • 1 small yellow onion, finely chopped (1 cup)
  • 1½ cups dried yellow split peas

Preparation

  • Set rice cooker setting to “sauté” (or similar), and preheat 2 to 3 minutes.
  • Add coconut oil, and heat 1 minute more.
  • Add onion, garlic, and carrots, and sauté 5 to 6 minutes, or until vegetables are softened.
  • Add cumin, turmeric, and red pepper flakes; cook 1 minute more.
  • Adjust rice cooker setting to “slow cook” (or similar).
  • Pour in 4 cups water, stir in split peas, and season with salt and pepper, if desired. Cover, and cook 3 to 4 hours, or until peas are soft. Thin dal with water, if desired.
  • Serve drizzled with lemon juice, and garnish with cilantro (if using).
