Grilled Pork Steak With Boston Lettuce Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 Boston lettuce
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese cubed
  • 1 green pepper
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 lean pork steaks
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp vinegar

Preparation

  • Start up your grill and wait till grill is very hot.Season your pork steaks with salt and pepper to taste, and any other spices you prefer. I have these spice mixes for pork, chicken, or beef so sometimes I use these for extra flavor.Grill the pork on each side to your liking, usually we do it for 10 min or so per side, we like it medium to well done.In the meantime, to prepare the salad, wash the Boston lettuce really well and cut in thin strips. Chop up the tomatoes and green pepper. In a big bowl, add the lettuce, tomatoes, green pepper and feta.
  • Add the olive oil, salt and pepper and vinegar and toss really well. This salad is so simple, but it's soooo good.
  • Serve the pork with a side of salad.
