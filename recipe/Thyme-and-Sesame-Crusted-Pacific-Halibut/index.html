Thyme- & Sesame-Crusted Pacific Halibut

Ingredients

  • 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 8 ounces Pacific halibut, or mahi-mahi, cut into 2 servings
  • 1/8 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Lemon wedges
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • Freshly ground pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds, toasted
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme leaves

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 450F. Line a baking sheet with foil.
  • Mix lemon juice, oil, garlic and pepper in a shallow glass dish.
  • Add fish and turn to coat. Cover and marinate in the refrigerator for 15 minutes.Meanwhile, combine sesame seeds and thyme in a small bowl.
  • Sprinkle the fish with salt and coat evenly with the sesame seed mixture, covering the sides as well as the top.
  • Transfer the fish to the preparedbaking sheet and roast until just cooked through, 10 to 14 minutes.
  • Serve with lemon wedges.
