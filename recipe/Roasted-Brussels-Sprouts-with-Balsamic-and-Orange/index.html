Roasted Brussels Sprouts with Balsamic & Orange

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 pound fresh brussels sprouts, stems trimmed and loose leaves removed, sliced in half
  • 2 teaspoons pure maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon fresh orange zest
  • salt and pepper

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Line a baking sheet with foil. In a bowl, mix together the vinegar, maple syrup, orange zest, olive oil, and a generous amount of salt and pepper.
  • Add the sliced brussels sprouts and toss until well-coated.
  • Place the sprouts cut-side-down on the foil and roast for 15-20 minutes, or until browned and crisp-tender. Taste for seasoning and add more salt or pepper as needed.
  • Serve promptly while hot.
