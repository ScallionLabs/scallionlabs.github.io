White Bean and Avocado Sandwich

  • http://www.beantownbaker.com/2011/06/white-bean-and-avocado-sandwich.html
  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 45min
  • Tags:
    Vegetarian Lunch Main Course Main Dish Dinner Dairy Free Lacto Ovo Vegetarian Vegan

Ingredients

  • alfalfa sprouts
  • 1 avocado, quartered, peeled, and sliced thin
  • 1 (15-ounce) can white beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 lemon
  • 3 leaves of leaf lettuce, torn into sandwich-sized pieces
  • 8 slices hearty whole-grain sandwich bread, toasted
  • salt and pepper

Preparation

  • In a food processor, puree the beans until they’re completely smooth. Season with lemon juice, salt and pepper to taste.Thickly spread one side of each piece of bread with the bean mixture. Top four of the bread slices with slices of avocado and plenty of lettuce. Press some sprouts into the bean mixture on the other four slices of bread.
  • Place the sprout-bread, sprout side down on the other piece of bread, slice the sandwich in half and enjoy.
