Grilled Salmon with Mango Salsa

  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 25min
  • Preparation time: 10min
  • Cooking time: 15min

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • ¼ cup packed cilantro leaves, roughly chopped
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 small jalapeño, seeded and finely chopped
  • juice of 1 lime
  • 2-3 mangos, diced
  • ½ red pepper, diced
  • ½ red onion, diced
  • 4 6-ounce salmon fillets
  • salt and pepper to taste

Preparation

  • In a medium bowl stir together mangos, red peppers, onions, jalapeos, and cilantro. Set aside until ready to use.Stir together garlic powder, chili powder, and salt and pepper (I used about teaspoon each). Rub mixture into salmon fillets. Grill over medium heat for 6-8 minutes on each side.Squeeze fresh lime juice over grilled salmon, then top with mango salsa and serve.
