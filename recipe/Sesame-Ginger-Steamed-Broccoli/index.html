Sesame Ginger Steamed Broccoli

  • Ready in: 10min
  • Preparation time: 5min
  • Cooking time: 5min

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. broccoli, cut into medium florets (6 cups loosely packed)
  • 1 Tablespoon. minced fresh ginger
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds
  • 1/4 cup water

Preparation

  • Place broccoli, ginger, sesame oil, and 1/4 cup water in large (2- to 3-qt.) pot. Cover, and bring to a simmer over medium heat. Steam 5 minutes, or until broccoli is bright green and crisp-tender.
  • Sprinkle with salt to taste and sesame seeds.
