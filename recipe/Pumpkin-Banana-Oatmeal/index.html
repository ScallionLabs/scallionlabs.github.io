Pumpkin Banana Oatmeal

  • http://thelemonbowl.com/2012/09/pumpkin-banana-oatmeal.html
  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 6min
  • Preparation time: 1min
  • Cooking time: 5min
  • Tags:
    Vegetarian Side Dish Gluten Free Lacto Ovo Vegetarian
  • High in: Manganese

Vitamins RDA %

Minerals RDA %

Macros

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas - thinly sliced
  • Mixed berries - optional topping
  • ¼ c flax seed meal
  • 2 c oats - old fashioned
  • 1 c pure pumpkin
  • 1 Tbs pumpkin pie spice
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 2 c skim milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • ¼ c walnuts - chopped

Preparation

  • In a medium sauce pot, whisk together milk, pumpkin, pumpkin pie spice, vanilla and salt over medium heat.Once milk mixture starts to bubble around the edges, stir in oats, bananas, walnuts and flax seed.Stir mixture and reduce heat to low.Cook 3-4 additional minutes or until oatmeal has reached desired consistency, stirring frequently.Top with berries if you wish!
