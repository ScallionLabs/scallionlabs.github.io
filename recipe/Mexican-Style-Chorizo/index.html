Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons ancho chili powder
  • 3 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon kosher or sea salt

Preparation

  • In a large bowl, gently combine all of the ingredients, using your hands, until well-blended.Wrap tightly in plastic wrap and refrigerate for 4 to 24 hours before using in your favorite recipe.Nutrition (per serving): 218 calories, 16.7g total fat, 54.4mg cholesterol, 402.5mg sodium, 315.4mg potassium, 3.4g carbohydrates, 1.5g fiber, <1g sugar, 13.6g protein
