High Protein Quinoa Almond Berry Salad

  • http://ohsheglows.com/2011/06/20/high-protein-quinoa-almond-berry-salad/
  • Servings: 3
  • Ready in: 15min
  • Tags:
    Vegetarian Salad Gluten Free Dairy Free Lacto Ovo Vegetarian Vegan
  • High in: Manganese

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup almonds, chopped
  • 1 tsp balsamic vinegar
  • 16 oz. fresh blueberries (1.5-2 cups)
  • 1 cup cherries, pitted and sliced
  • Pinch of kosher salt
  • 1 tbsp fresh lime juice (or lemon might work!)
  • 2 tbsp pure maple syrup (or other liquid sweetener)
  • 3/4 cup dry quinoa
  • 1 pint strawberries, sliced (2.5-3 cups)

Preparation

  • Cook quinoa according to package directions.
