Steven Redzikowski’s for Lemon Rubbed Chicken With Asparagus and Mushrooms

Ingredients

  • ½ pound asparagus, trimmed
  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs (about 2 pounds total)
  • 1 tablespoon butter, cut into small pieces
  • ½ lemon, seeded and finely chopped, plus juice of ½ lemon
  • 2 cups morels or other wild mushrooms (about ½ pound), cleaned, trimmed and cut into bite-size pieces
  • 2½ tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon oregano, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley leaves
  • Leaves from 1 sprig rosemary
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon chopped thyme leaves
  • 3 cups watercress

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 450 degrees and place one oven rack on top and another in the middle.
  • Make lemon marinade: In a medium bowl, combine chopped lemon, parsley and tablespoon olive oil. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Place chicken, skin-side down, on a baking sheet, and rub all over with marinade. Roast chicken on ovens floor until skin has browned, 10-15 minutes. Flip chicken, transfer to top rack and roast until meat cooks through, about 15 minutes more.
  • Meanwhile, on another baking sheet, toss asparagus with 1 tablespoon oil, salt and pepper. On a third baking sheet, toss mushrooms with rosemary, thyme and a pinch of salt. Top with butter, dividing evenly among mushrooms.
  • Roast asparagus and mushrooms on center rack until fork tender and lightly blistered, about 15 minutes.
  • Remove vegetables from oven. Once asparagus is cool enough to handle, cut into bite-size pieces.
  • In a large mixing bowl, toss asparagus, mushrooms and their roasting juices with watercress. Season salad lightly with remaining oil, lemon juice, oregano, salt and pepper.
