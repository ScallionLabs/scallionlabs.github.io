Roasted Red Pepper Hummus, Avocado, & Feta Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados, pit removed
  • 1 tablespoon freshly chopped basil
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup roasted red pepper hummus
  • 1/2 cup roasted red peppers, drained
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 4 slices whole wheat bread
  • 2 ripe avocados, pit removed
  • 1 tablespoon freshly chopped basil
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup roasted red pepper hummus
  • 1/2 cup roasted red peppers, drained
  • 4 slices whole wheat bread

Preparation

  • Spread the bread slices evenly with roasted red pepper hummus. Set aside.
  • In a small bowl, combine avocado and feta cheese. Mash with a fork.
  • Add the lemon juice, basil, salt, and pepper.
  • Spread the avocado feta mixture over the red pepper hummus. Top with roasted red peppers, on two slices of the bread.
  • Place other pieces of bread on top to form a sandwich.
  • Brush the outside pieces of bread with olive oil or butter and place on a hot grill or hot pan. Grill each side until toasted, about 2-3 minutes.
  • Serve immediately. Note-you can serve the sandwich cold, without grilling it. It is just as tasty!
