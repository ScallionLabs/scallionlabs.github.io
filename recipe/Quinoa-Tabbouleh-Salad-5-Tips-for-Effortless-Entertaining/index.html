Ingredients

  • ½ cup halved cherry tomatoes (or seeded diced roma tomatoes)
  • ½ cup diced cucumbers
  • 2 tablespoons thinly sliced fresh mint
  • 2 cups finely minced fresh parsley
  • juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ cup leftover cooked quinoa (I used red quinoa but any variety works)
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • ¼ cup minced scallions

Preparation

  • Place quinoa through mint in a large bowl and drizzle with lemon juice, olive oil, salt and pepper; toss well.Check for seasoning and adjust accordingly before serving.
