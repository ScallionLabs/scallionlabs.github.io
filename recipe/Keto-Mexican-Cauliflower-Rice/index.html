Keto Mexican Cauliflower Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 Lbs Cauliflower, cut into florets (about 1 head or about 7-8 cups riced)
  • 2 Tbsp Olive oil
  • 2 tsp Fajita seasoning*
  • 3/4 cup Salsa
  • 1/4-1/2 tsp Salt, to taste (I like 1/2 tsp but Mr.FFF says that's too much)
  • 1/2 cup Cilantro, diced
  • Fresh lime juice

Preparation

  • Place your cauliflower into a large food processor and pulse until rice like.
  • Heat the oil up in a large pan on medium high heat.
  • Add in the rice and cook, stirring occasionally, until golden brown. about 5-6 minutes
  • Add in the fajita seasoning and salt (to taste) and cook one minute.
  • Add in the salsa and cook until the rice begins to dry up, about 3-4 minutes.
  • Stir in the cilantro, squeeze on some lime and DEVOUR!
