Ingredients

  • 8 cups broccoli cut into florets
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 1½ teaspoons sesame oil or olive oil
  • 2 teaspoon sesame seeds for topping
  • Cook Time: 4 minutes
  • Prep Time: 8 minutes

Preparation

  • Heat oil in pan.
  • Add garlic, broccoli, soy sauce and stir fry for 2-3 minutes until heated and still a bit crunchy.
  • Remove from heat and add to serving plate.
  • In the same pan, toast the sesame seed for about 1 minutes until browned.
  • Sprinkle seeds all over the broccoli and serve.Makes 4 servings (about 2 cups each)Food Fact
