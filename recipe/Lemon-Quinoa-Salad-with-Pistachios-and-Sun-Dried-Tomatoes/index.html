Lemon Quinoa Salad with Pistachios & Sun-Dried Tomatoes

  • http://www.cookincanuck.com/2013/10/lemon-quinoa-salad-with-pistachios-sun-dried-tomatoes/
  • Servings: 3
  • Ready in: 30min
  • Preparation time: 10min
  • Cooking time: 20min
  • Tags:
    Vegetarian Salad Gluten Free Dairy Free Lacto Ovo Vegetarian Vegan

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tsp agave nectar or honey
  • 1/4 cup chopped flat-leaf parsley
  • 1/8 tsp ground pepper
  • 1 1/2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup shelled & chopped pistachios
  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 oz. sun-dried tomatoes (not packed in oil), sliced (about 1/2 cup)
  • 2 cups water

Preparation

  • In a medium saucepan, combine the quinoa and water. Bring the water to a boil, reduce the heat to low and cover. Cook until all of the water is absorbed, 15 to 20 minutes. Fluff the quinoa with a fork.
  • Transfer the cooked quinoa to a large baking sheet and spread it out evenly.
  • Let it cool to room temperature.To the quinoa, add the sun-dried tomatoes, pistachios and parsley. Stir to combine.In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, salt and pepper.
  • Pour the dressing over the quinoa salad and stir to combine.
  • Serve or refrigerate, covered, until ready to serve.
