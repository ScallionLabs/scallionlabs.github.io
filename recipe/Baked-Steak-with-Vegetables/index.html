Baked Steak with Vegetables

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pounds Asparagus
  • 2 pounds Beef Flank
  • 1/2 pounds Bell Pepper
  • Light Olive Oil
  • 1 pound Mushrooms
  • 1/2 Purple Onion
  • Salt & Pepper; to taste

Preparation

  • Cut all vegetables into one inch chunks, spread evenly on a baking sheet. Rub both sizes of beef with oil and salt.
  • Place beef on top of the vegetables.
  • Bake it at 400F, for about 12 minutes.
  • Remove all vegetable from under the beef and continue baking beef for another 7-10 minutes, depending on how well you want it done. You can broil beef on high for about a minute, to brown it.
  • Remove beef from heat, let it sit for about a minute. Slice it into thin slices and place them over vegetables.
  • Serve while its warm, it's the best that way.
