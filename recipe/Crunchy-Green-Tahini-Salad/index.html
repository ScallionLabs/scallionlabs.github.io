Crunchy Green Tahini Salad

  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 45min
  • Tags:
    Vegetarian Salad Gluten Free Lacto Ovo Vegetarian
  • High in: K A C Copper Manganese

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbs. Bragg Liquid Aminos, optional
  • 1 cup grated carrots
  • ¼ cup dried cranberries
  • ½ cup feta cheese
  • 3 cloves garlic, peeled
  • ¼ tsp. honey, optional
  • 2 12-oz. bunches kale, stems removed
  • ½ cup lemon juice
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 1 orange, divided into segments
  • 1 cup chopped parsley or mint
  • ½ cup toasted pine nuts or almonds
  • 1 lb. roasted red potatoes
  • 2 Tbs. tahini

Preparation

  • Stack 8 kale leaves, and roll into tight cylinder. Slice into thin strips. Repeat with remaining kale.
  • Transfer to large bowl.
  • Blend 1/3 cup water, lemon juice, oil, tahini, Bragg Liquid Aminos (if using), garlic, and honey (if using) in blender or food processor until smooth. (You’ll have about 1 cup dressing.) Toss kale with 3/4 cup dressing, more if using any add-ins. Season with salt and pepper, if desired.
