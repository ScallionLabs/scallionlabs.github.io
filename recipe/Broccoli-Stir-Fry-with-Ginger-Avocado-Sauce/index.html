Broccoli Stir-Fry with Ginger-Avocado Sauce

Ingredients

  • 2 avocados
  • 4 cups organic baby spinach
  • 1 head broccoli, cut into florets (4 cups)
  • 1 small head cabbage or 1 bunch kale, sliced into thin ribbons (½ lb.)
  • 1 Tbs. coconut oil
  • 1 2-inch piece fresh ginger, peeled and sliced into coins
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced (4 tsp.)
  • 2 Tbs. lemon juice
  • 2 tsp. grated lemon zest
  • 1 Tbs. cold-pressed olive oil
  • 2 tsp. raw honey
  • 1 medium red onion, halved and sliced (1 ½ cups)
  • ¼ tsp. sea salt
  • 2 pinches sea salt
  • ¼ cup lightly toasted sesame seeds, for garnish, optional

Preparation

To make Ginger-Avocado Sauce
  • Place ginger in food processor, and pulse to mince.
  • Add remaining ingredients, and blend until smooth. Blend in up to 1/2 cup water to thin to desired consistency. Store in refrigerator.
To make Stir-Fry
  • Heat coconut oil in wok or large skillet over medium heat.
  • Add onion and salt; stir-fry 5 minutes, or until onion softens.
  • Add garlic, and cook 2 minutes more.
  • Add broccoli and 1/4 cup water; cover, and steam 3 minutes.
  • Add cabbage; cover, and steam 2 minutes more.
  • Place 1 cup spinach on plate. Top with 1 cup broccoli mixture, and drizzle with 2 Tbs. Ginger-Avocado Sauce.
  • Sprinkle with sesame seeds (if using).
