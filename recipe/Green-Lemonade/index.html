Green Lemonade

Ingredients

  • 1 cucumber (I leave the skin on)
  • 1 Granny Smith apple (remove core)
  • 1 bunch kale (use stalks and all)
  • 1 lemon (I leave the peel on)

Preparation

  • Juice all ingredients
  • Place green lemonade in glasses
  • Serve
