Ingredients

  • 6 thinly sliced cucumbers
  • 1 tablespoon feta cheese
  • 1-2 teaspoons Greek dressing
  • 1 tablespoon sliced or chopped kalamata olives
  • 3 thin slices red onion
  • 1/4 cup romaine lettuce or mixed greens
  • 1 slice tomato, cut in half
  • 1 whole wheat wrap

Preparation

  • Layer your lettuce on top of the wrap. Top with sliced cucumbers, tomato slices, olives, and feta cheese.
  • Drizzle with Greek dressing.Fold the two sides of the wrap in and gently roll. Slice in half.
