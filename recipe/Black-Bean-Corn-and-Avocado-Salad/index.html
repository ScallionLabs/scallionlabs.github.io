Black Bean, Corn, and Avocado Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado chopped
  • 1 can (30oz) of black beans
  • 1/4 cup of chopped cilantro
  • 1 can (19oz) of corn kernels
  • 1 jalapeno minced
  • juice from 2 limes
  • zest from one lime
  • 3 large tomatoes chopped
  • 2 tortillas sliced in thin strips and fried in 1 tbsp of olive oil (optional)

Preparation

  • Rinse and drain the black beans and add them to a large bowl.
  • Drain the corn kernels and add them to the bowl.Chop the avocado, tomatoes and jalapeno and add to the bowl.
  • Add the lime zest and lime juice, salt and pepper and mix everything well together.
  • Serve with some crisp tortilla chips.
