Ingredients

  • ¼ cup organic baby carrots
  • ½ banana
  • 2 cups kale
  • 2 tbsp non-fat plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp oats
  • ¼ cup orange juice
  • ¼ cup frozen organic raspberries
  • 5 organic strawberries, hulled
  • 2 tbsp water

Preparation

  • Add all ingredients to a blender and blend well.
