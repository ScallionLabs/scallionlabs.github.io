Cajun Spiced Black Bean and Sweet Potato Burgers

  • Ready in: 20min

Ingredients

  • 400g tin black beans
  • 2 tsp Cajun spice
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • ½ cup / 45g porridge oats
  • a good grinding of black pepper
  • 100g spinach
  • 200g (1 medium) sweet potato, baked

Preparation

  • Rinse the beans and add them to a mixing bowl. Mash, but leave half of the beans whole for a bit of texture.
  • Scoop the flesh out of the baked potato with a spoon and add to the bowl.
  • Rinse your spinach in a colander then pour a kettleful of boiling water over it to wilt it. Rinse with cold water to refresh, then squeeze as much water out as you can. Chop and add to the bowl.
  • Next add the seeds, oats, spices, garlic and black pepper and mix until well combined.
  • Shape your mixture into 4 hearty balls, then flatten out into burger shapes.
  • Pop the burgers on a plate until you are ready to use them. You can bake or fry them in a little oil. I fried my burger in a little cold pressed rapeseed oil, for a few minutes on each side until brown and crisp.
  • These burgers will also freeze well.
  • Layer them between sheets of greaseproof paper in a tupperware tub and pop in the freezer.
  • Enjoy!
