Trout Baked in Cream

  • Ready in: 20min
  • Preparation time: 5min
  • Cooking time: 15min

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon dill weed
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons seasoned bread crumbs
  • 6 trout fillets (about 3-1/2 ounces each)

Preparation

  • Place trout in a greased 13-in. x 9-in. baking dish.
  • Sprinkle with lemon juice, dill, salt and pepper.
  • Pour cream over all.
  • Sprinkle with bread crumbs.
  • Bake, uncovered, at 350° for 11-15 minutes or until fish flakes easily with a fork.
