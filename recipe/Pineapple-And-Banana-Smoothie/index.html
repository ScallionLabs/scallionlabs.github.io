Pineapple And Banana Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1 cup of frozen pineapple
  • 1 cup of unsweetened vanilla soy milk

Preparation

  • Put the milk, banana, and pineapple in a blender, and blend until you have a thick and creamy smoothie.If your smoothie is a tad too thick add a little extra soy milk or some water.  Enjoy!
