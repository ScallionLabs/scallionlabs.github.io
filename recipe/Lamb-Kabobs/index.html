Lamb Kabobs

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds Boneless Lamb
  • 1 large Onion
  • 2 large Red Bell Pepper
  • 1 cup Red Wine
  • 1 tablespoon Stake Seasoning
  • 1/4 cups Soy Sauce
  • 1/2 cups Teriyaki Sauce

Preparation

  • Cut meat into one inch cubes.
  • Place meat in a dish, cover with soy sauce, teriyaki sauce, wine and stake seasoning. Dice onions and bell peppers into large cubes. Using your hands, mix them together and press to extract the juices.
  • Combine all ingredients together.
  • Let them marinate overnight.
  • Place the marinated ingredients on a stick. Grill until it's cooked trough, depending on your preference.
