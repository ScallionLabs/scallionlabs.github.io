Crockpot Cuban Black Bean Soup (Frijoles Negros) – Power Foods

Ingredients

  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 pound dried black beans, rinsed thoroughly, soaked overnight
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 5 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • chopped onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  • chopped red bell pepper
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • chopped scallions
  • 2 tablespoons sherry vinegar
  • 8 cups water
  • 1 large white onion, finely chopped

Preparation

  • Heat oil in a large pan.
  • Add onion, bell pepper, and garlic cloves to pan and saute until onions are soft, about 4-5 minutes.
  • Add bay leaf, cumin, oregano, and black pepper and cook for 2 more minutes.
  • Place in crockpot along with soaked beans and 8 cups of water. Cook on low for 6-7 hours.
  • Add sherry vinegar, wine and salt, and continue to cook on low for another 1 to 1 1/2 hours.
  • Remove bay leaf fore serving.
  • Serve as a soup or over steamed rice.
