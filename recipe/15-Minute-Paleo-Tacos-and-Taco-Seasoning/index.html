Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 4 tbsp. chili powder
  • 1 large cucumber
  • 1 tsp. garlic powder
  • 3 tbsp. ground cumin
  • 1lb ground turkey
  • 1 tbsp. plus 2 tsp. onion powder
  • 3 tbsp. plus 1 tsp. paprika
  • 1 large red bell pepper
  • 2-3 roma tomatoes
  • 3 hearts of Romaine Lettuce
  • approx 1 cup salsa (or more depending on how "wet" you like your salad)
  • 4 scallions (green onions)
  • Several tablespoons homemade taco seasoning (recipe below)

Preparation

  • TO ASSEMBLE TACO SEASONING
  • Combine all spices in an airtight container and shake well to mix.TO ASSEMBLE SALADSet a pan over medium high heat and cook ground turkey until it is no longer pink, stirring frequently.While you're cooking the turkey, chop romaine hearts and divide among 4 large salad plates.Stir turkey to make sure it is cooking evenly.Chop cucumber, avocado, tomatoes, bell pepper, and scallions and add evenly to each plate of lettuce.When turkey is no longer pink, add several tablespoons (I usually use 3-
  • of the homemade taco seasoning and stir to fully incorporate. If the meat is somewhat dry, splash a little water into the pan to loosen everything up and help the seasoning coat the meat crumbles evenly.When the meat is fully cooked and seasoned, top each salad with approximately the total amount.Top with salsa and a few crushed tortilla chips (optional - not paleo) and enjoy!Any extra cooked & seasoned meat can be stored in an airtight container in the fridge for up to a week!
