Ultimate 4-Layer Vegan Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 1/2 avocado, thinly sliced
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon fine grain sea salt, or to taste
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1 large garlic clove
  • 1/4 cup hulled hemp seeds
  • 2 tablespoons hummus
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • lettuce
  • 1/4 cup oil-packed sun-dried tomatoes (about 6)
  • 1-2 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil, to taste
  • pinch of red pepper flakes
  • pinch of salt & pepper
  • sprouted-grain bread (or wraps or Bibb lettuce)
  • 2 tablespoons Sun-dried Tomato Hemp Basil Pesto (from above)
  • 1-2 thin tomato slices
  • 2 tablespoons water

Preparation

  • For the pesto: Mince garlic in a food processor.
  • Add the rest of the pesto ingredients and process until the pesto is smooth, stopping to scrape down the bowl as needed.Toast the bread in a toaster.
  • Spread bread with hummus and pesto (one on each).
  • Layer on the avocado, tomato, and lettuce.
  • Sprinkle on red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper. Slice in half and enjoy!
