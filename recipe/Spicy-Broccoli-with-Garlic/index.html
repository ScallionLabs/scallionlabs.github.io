Spicy Broccoli with Garlic

  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 5min

  • High in: C K

Ingredients

  • 1 crown of broccoli, cut into florets
  • Pinch of crushed red pepper
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2-3 tsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp rice vinegar
  • Sea salt and freshly cracked pepper, to taste
  • 2-3 tbsp water

Preparation

  • Bring water to a simmer in a skillet over medium heat.
  • Add broccoli florets; cook, covered, until bright green, 2-3 minutes.Stir in the olive oil, minced garlic, sea salt & freshly cracked pepper, to taste, and crushed red pepper flakes. Cook, stirring often, for 2 minutes, or until crisp-tender.
  • Remove the skillet from heat; stir in vinegar then mix until evenly coated.
  • Pour into serving bowl.
  • Serve immediately. Enjoy.
