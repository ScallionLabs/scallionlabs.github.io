Avocado Banana Kiwi Kale Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1/2 of a ripe avocado
  • 1 sliced and frozen banana
  • 1/2 cup of very finely chopped kale
  • 2 kiwis, peeled and chopped
  • 1/2 cup of vanilla yogurt

Preparation

  • Start by buying a bundle of bananas.
  • Let them sit on the counter for a few days and when they are nice and ripe open each of them and slice them up.
  • Place each sliced banana in a plastic bag and freeze them.Just before leaving for work, take half of a ripe avocado and chop it. Chop your kiwis and finely chop your kale.
  • Place them in your blender (I use a magic bullet). Drop the frozen banana pieces in the blender and then the yogurt.Blend for about 20-30 seconds or until everything is smooth. If need be, add a bit of water to help everything blend together nicely.Eat now or place in fridge for later. (note that the texture might not be the exact same if you eat it later).
