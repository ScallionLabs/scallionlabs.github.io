Grilled Steak with Avocado Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado, skin and pit removed, diced
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/3 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 1/2 pounds flat iron steak
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • juice from 1 lime
  • 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar

Preparation

  • Combine first 5 ingredients in a bowl. Rub all over the steak.Preheat grill to medium heat.
  • Place remaining ingredients in a food processor and process until smooth.
  • Place in the refrigerator until ready to use.Cook steak about 5 minutes per side for medium rare.
  • Transfer to a plate and allow to rest for 10 minutes.Slice steak and spoon avocado sauce over the top.
  • Serve.
