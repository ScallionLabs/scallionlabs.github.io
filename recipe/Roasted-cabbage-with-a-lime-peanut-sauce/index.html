Roasted cabbage with a lime peanut sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon dijon mustard
  • juice of 1 lime
  • ¼ cup peanuts, chopped
  • 1 medium head of red cabbage, sliced
  • salt & pepper
  • 1½ tablespoons sesame oil

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 400 degrees.Grease a baking sheet.In a small bowl whisk together sesame oil, mustard, lime juice, salt & pepper.
  • Place sliced cabbage in a large bowl, pour dressing over it and toss well.
  • Spread cabbage onto baking sheet and roast for about 60 minutes until crispy, tossing occasionally.Top with chopped peanuts and additional salt & pepper to taste.
