Preparation

  • Pour the tomatoes into the container of a slow cooker.
  • Add vegetables, wine and herbs. Season generously with pepper and stir.Wrap each thigh in half a slice of prosciutto and place the chicken in the slow cooker. Cover and cook on low for 6 hours.
  • Serve over pasta.
