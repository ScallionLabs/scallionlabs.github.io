Simple Navy Bean Soup

  • Ready in: 45min

Macros

Ingredients

  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 can (15 ounces) gluten-free chicken broth
  • Chopped fresh rosemary, or to taste
  • 1 can (15 ounces) white kidney or navy beans, rinsed and drained

Preparation

  • Place all the ingredients in a medium saucepan and heat to boiling, mashing a few of the beans with a fork to thicken the soup slightly.
  • Serve hot, garnished with a little rosemary.
