Crispy Chicken with Wilted Romaine

Ingredients

  • 4 slices bacon (thick cut if you have it), chopped into one inch pieces
  • 2 bone-in chicken breast halves, with skin
  • Coarse salt and fresh ground pepper
  • Lemon slices
  • 1 small head of romaine lettuce, core cut off and leaves torn roughly in half
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Preparation

  • Heat oil in a heavy skillet over medium high heat. Season chicken with salt and pepper and place in skillet, skin side down. Cook for 10 minutes, checking occasionally to make sure skin is getting deep brown and crispy. Turn with tongs and cook for another 10 minutes until done.
  • Place on a plate and cover with foil.
  • Add bacon to skillet and cook until almost done.
  • Add lettuce and stir until just wilted. Season with freshly ground pepper.
  • Place chicken and lettuce on plates and serve with lemon slices on the side.
