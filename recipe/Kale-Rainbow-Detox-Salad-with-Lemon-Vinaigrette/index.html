Kale Rainbow Detox Salad with Lemon Vinaigrette

  • Servings: 4
  • Ready in: 80min
  • Preparation time: 20min
  • Cooking time: 60min
  • Tags:
    Vegetarian Salad Gluten Free Dairy Free Lacto Ovo Vegetarian Vegan
  • High in: K A C

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup roasted almonds
  • 1/2 avocado, diced
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Juice of 3 lemons (about 1/2 cup)
  • 2 bunches kale (about 6-8 cups), desteemed and finely chopped
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 1 orange bell pepper, diced
  • 1/3 cup chopped parsley
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 small red onion, diced
  • salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar, if desired to sweeten it up
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced

Preparation

  • To make dressing: place lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, sugar, and salt in bowl; whisk together until combined and set aside.In a large bowl, toss kale, bell peppers, onions, and carrots together.
  • Add dressing, cilantro, and parsley. Use hands to massage kale with dressing; this is important so that the kale absorbs the dressing.
  • Place in refrigerator for 30 minutes to an hour to let flavors soak in.Before serving, toss salad again. Top with almonds and avocado. Makes 4 servings. Enjoy!
