Company-Ready Crusted Salmon

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons sliced almonds
  • 2 packages (6 ounces each) fresh baby spinach
  • Hot cooked couscous, optional
  • 1/4 cup cornflakes
  • 3 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 cup fat-free mayonnaise
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons chopped pecans
  • 1 salmon fillet (1-1/2 pounds)

Preparation

  • Place spinach in a 13-in. x 9-in. baking dish coated with cooking spray; top with salmon.
  • Drizzle oil over spinach.
  • Combine honey and mustard.
  • Remove 2 tablespoons mixture; brush over salmon.
  • Place the cornflakes, almonds and pecans in a small food processor; cover and process until ground. Press onto salmon. Stir mayonnaise into remaining honey mixture; refrigerate until serving.
  • Bake, uncovered, at 450° for 18-22 minutes or until fish flakes easily with a fork.
  • Drizzle with reserved sauce.
  • Serve with couscous if desired.
