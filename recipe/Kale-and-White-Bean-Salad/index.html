Kale and White Bean Salad

  • http://thefitchen.com/2013/09/26/kale-and-white-bean-salad/
  • Servings: 2
  • Ready in: 10min
  • Tags:
    Vegetarian Side Dish Gluten Free Dairy Free Lacto Ovo Vegetarian Vegan
  • High in: K A C Copper

Macros

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tsp. fresh black pepper
  • 4 cloves of garlic [minced]
  • juice of 1 lemon [about 3 Tablespoons]
  • 6 kale leaves [destemmed and chopped]
  • 1 can of white beans [drained]
  • 1-2 Tbsp. cold pressed olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp. sea salt

Preparation

  • Wash kale leaves thoroughly and remove the tough stem. Chop into bite-sized pieces.
  • In a large bowl, combine kale leaves, beans, garlic, lemon juice, sea salt, and pepper.
  • Mix well to combine.
  • Serve and enjoy!
