Loaded Scrambled Eggs

  • Preparation time: 5min

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (packed) fresh baby spinach leaves
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon butter (or olive oil)*
  • 12 eggs, beaten with 2 tablespoon of water
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 pound ground sausage
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasonings
  • 8 ounces sliced mushrooms
  • optional garnish: chopped fresh parsley, grated cheese
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • additional salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 yellow bell pepper diced
  • 1 medium onion (red or yellow), diced

Preparation

  • Brown sausage in a large skillet over medium heat.
  • Transfer browned sausage to bowl. Leave sausage fat in pan; add 1 tablespoon butter and allow to melt.
  • Add diced onions, diced bell pepper, mushrooms, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, salt and pepper; continue to cook stirring occasionally for 5-7 minutes until tender.Reduce heat to medium-low.
  • Pour in beaten eggs and evenly spread baby spinach leaves over top. Wait 1 minute and stir all together, scraping up any egg from bottom and sides of pan. Continue to cook, stirring occasionally until eggs are done, about 5 minutes.
  • Add browned sausage back to pan; stir to incorporate. Salt and pepper to taste.
  • Serve warm. Optional: garnish with fresh parsley and/or shredded cheese of choice.
