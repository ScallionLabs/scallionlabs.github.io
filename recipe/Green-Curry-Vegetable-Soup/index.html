Ingredients

  • ½ cup baby spinach
  • ¼ cup grated carrot
  • ¾ cup cooked whole-wheat angel hair pasta
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1/3 cup chopped red bell pepper
  • 1 tablespoon Thai green curry paste, or more to taste

Preparation

  • Combine pasta, spinach, bell pepper, carrot and cilantro in a heatproof bowl. Bring chicken broth to a boil. Stir in curry paste.
  • Pour the broth over the pasta mixture.
  • Serve hot.
