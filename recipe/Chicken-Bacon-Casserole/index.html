Preparation

  • Pre-heat the oven to 350F.While melting the butter over medium heat, cube the chicken into one-inch pieces.Season the chicken with salt, pepper, and garlic powder and cook through until slightly golden outside.
  • Add the spinach and cook until the leaves are wilted.Cook the bacon in the microwave until crispy, about 5 minutes.In a large mixing bowl, soften the cream cheese in the microwave about 30 seconds.
  • Add the mayo, sour cream, chicken + spinach, and crumbled bacon to the cream cheese; stir until fully combined.
  • Pour mixture into 8x8 glass baking dish.
  • Bake for 25 minutes, broil for 3 minutes until golden brown.
