Strawberry tabouleh

Ingredients

  • ½ cup cooked bulgar wheat
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh mint, chopped
  • ¼ cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • ½ teaspoon honey
  • ¾ tablespoon lemon juice
  • ¼ cup orange bell pepper, finely chopped
  • salt & pepper
  • 1 cup strawberries, sliced
  • ¼ cup walnuts, chopped

Preparation

  • Combine bulgar, herbs, strawberries and walnuts in a small bowl and toss together.In another small bow, whisk together remaining ingredients to make dressing.
  • Pour dressing over tabouleh and and toss together.Either serve immediately or chill for later. Flavors develop the longer it sits.
