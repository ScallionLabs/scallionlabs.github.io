Brazilian Skirt Steak with Golden Garlic Butter

Ingredients

  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 Tbs. chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley
  • 6 medium cloves garlic
  • Kosher salt
  • 1-1/2 lb. skirt steak, trimmed and cut into 4 pieces
  • 2 oz. (4 Tbs.) unsalted butter
  • 2 Tbs. canola oil or vegetable oil

Preparation

  • Peel the garlic cloves and smash them with the side of a chefs knife.
  • Sprinkle the garlic lightly with salt and mince it. Pat the steak dry and season generously on both sides with salt and pepper. In a heavy-duty 12-inch skillet, heat the oil over medium-high heat until shimmering hot.
  • Add the steak and brown well on both sides, 2 to 3 minutes per side for medium rare.
  • Transfer the steak to a plate and let rest while you make the garlic butter. In an 8-inch skillet, melt the butter over low heat.
  • Add the garlic and cook, swirling the pan frequently, until lightly golden, about 4 minutes. Lightly salt to taste. Slice the steak, if you like, and transfer to 4 plates. Spoon the garlic butter over the steak, sprinkle with the parsley, and serve.
